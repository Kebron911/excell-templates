## The 30-second pitch

You raised your nightly rate 15% last year. Revenue is up. You feel good. But occupancy dropped 22% — and your RevPAR is actually down 6%. You're working harder, taking on the same costs, and netting less.

RevPAR is the number that catches this. This dashboard surfaces it next to ADR and Occupancy with a sensitivity simulator that shows you what happens before you change pricing — not after.

## Who it's for

- Hosts running 1–8 properties who haven't been calculating RevPAR explicitly
- Anyone with a dynamic pricing tool who can't tell whether it's winning
- Operators comparing to AirDNA market data and wanting an apples-to-apples gap analysis

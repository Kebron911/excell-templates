## The 30-second pitch

Guests don't read your 8-paragraph Airbnb message. They scan the welcome book when they walk in the door, because it's right there on the kitchen counter.

This template is a 9-section workbook — Wi-Fi and arrival, kitchen quirks, trash day, local picks, emergencies, departure — sized so each section fits one printed page. Edit once per property. Print, frame, or QR.

## Who it's for

- Hosts emailing the same Google Doc over and over (and watching it get ignored)
- Operators with one to ten properties who want a single editable source
- Anyone who's tired of seeing "What's the Wi-Fi password?" in the chat at 11pm

## Math that earns the price

One avoided "where's the trash" message saves 2 minutes. A welcome book that catches half your repeat questions saves ~30 messages per booking. At 60 bookings a year, that's 15 hours back. Workbook costs $17.

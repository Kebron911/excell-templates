K-1-ready capital accounts. Per-partner allocations done right. Partnership distribution tracker for STR LLCs taxed as partnerships. Capital contributions, P&L allocation by ownership %, distributions paid, ending capital per partner. Excel + Google Sheets. You partner-up to buy a cabin. Three partners, 50/30/20 split. Year 1 ends — who gets what cash distribution? What's each partner's K-1 figure for tax filing? Without a system, partner relationships get tense fast: someone thinks they got short-changed, the K-1s don't reconcile, and your tax preparer charges 3 hours to clean it up. This workbook tracks: capital contributions by partner + date, profit allocation by ownership %, distributions paid per partner per date, ending capital account.

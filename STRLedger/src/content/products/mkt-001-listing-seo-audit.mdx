## The 30-second pitch

Your occupancy is 65%. Comps in your market hit 80%. You don't know if it's the title length, the photo order, the missing amenities, your response time, or the cancellation policy.

Most "Airbnb tips" articles tell you to "improve your photos." They don't tell you that your cover should be exterior, not the bedroom. They don't tell you that 19 photos is six short of the threshold where the algorithm stops penalizing you.

This audit scores 40 criteria across five categories — Title, Description, Photos, Amenities, Trust Signals. Every red flag has a same-row specific fix. The Methodology tab shows you the source for every criterion and the date we last verified it. Most audits are black boxes. This one shows its work.

Run your rentals before they run you. Rental arbitrage deal analyzer for STR operators leasing units to sublet on Airbnb. Lease math, stress test, MTR fallback. Excel. Read this first. Rental arbitrage is legal only when your lease permits subletting AND your jurisdiction permits short-term rentals. If your lease says no sublet, or your city's STR registry is closed, the math doesn't matter — the deal is illegal. This workbook flips the verdict to PASS automatically when either flag is missing. Consult an attorney before signing any master-lease agreement. Now the math. The reason most arbitrage units fail is not Year 1. It is Year 2, when the landlord raises the rent 7 to 10 percent at renewal and the operator has $14,800 of furniture in a unit that no longer cash flows.

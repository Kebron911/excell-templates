## The 30-second pitch

Airbnb's house-rules textarea is blank because most hosts don't know what to put there. They write "be respectful" and it doesn't help when something goes wrong.

This workbook walks you through six specific sections (noise, smoking, pets, occupancy, damage, check-out) and outputs a sheet you print + a text block you paste into the listing. Rules that AirCover, claims adjusters, and clean-up crews can act on.

## Who it's for

- Hosts whose house rules currently say "no parties" and then a party happens
- Operators who've had an AirCover claim rejected for "insufficient rule documentation"
- Anyone managing a property they don't live near and needs the rules to do work for them

## Math that earns the price

One avoided damage claim disputed because the rule wasn't specific enough = $300–$3,000+ recovered. Workbook costs $17. Returns its cost the first time a specific rule lets you escalate fee or refund.

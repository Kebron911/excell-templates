## The 30-second pitch

Most STR P&L spreadsheets are 4 columns wide and don't map to IRS Schedule E. Your CPA then re-keys 12 months of expenses into their software, charges you for the time, and asks you what counts as "Repairs" vs "Maintenance" anyway.

This workbook maps every category to a Schedule E line. Type expenses once; the Summary tab prints what your CPA needs.

## Who it's for

- Single-property hosts who want a CPA-ready year-end summary without paying the CPA to build it
- Hosts considering the STR loophole and wanting to confirm eligibility before filing
- Anyone whose current "P&L" is bank statements in a folder

## Math that earns the price

CPA hourly is $150–$300. Saving 1 hour of clean-up at year-end recovers $27 several times over. The first year you find a missed deduction (mileage, depreciation timing, the home-office split), the workbook returns 10× cost in deductions.

Which owner relationships are worth keeping. Which to fire. Multi-owner consolidated reporting for STR property managers running 10+ properties across multiple owners. Per-owner $/hour profitability + relationship ranking + fire-vs-keep recommendations. Excel + Google Sheets. You manage 12 properties for 5 owners. One owner is a dream — high revenue, low communication burden. One is a nightmare — constant emails, 10% of your time for 3% of your revenue. The other three are somewhere in between.

5 LLCs. 5 P&Ls. One holding-company view. Multi-entity consolidated P&L for STR investors holding each property in its own LLC. Aggregates per-LLC income statements with intercompany eliminations + member distributions. Excel + Google Sheets. You hold each property in its own LLC for liability isolation. Smart move for asset protection. Hard for portfolio reporting: 5 LLCs = 5 separate P&Ls + 5 K-1s, and your overall income picture lives in your head. This workbook aggregates per-LLC income statements into a holding-company- level consolidated P&L. Adds entity-level allocation, intercompany eliminations (one LLC's expense = another LLC's revenue, must net to zero), and member-distribution tracking.

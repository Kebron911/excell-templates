Auto-webinar is a video that simulates a webinar. The participant listens to the recording and at some point, he/she can be offered to write a comment. You can download this text and the presenter will answer these questions. The “Buy” button pops up during the webinar at appropriate times.

By recording and configuring such a simulation of a webinar once, you can embed this proposal in the auto-series of emails, when each new subscriber receives an invitation to watch the webinar at a certain time after subscribing.

## Filter

The filter allows you to search for a webinar by name. If you enter a part of the name, all webinars, with that part in its name, will be displayed. If you enter the whole name – one webinar will be displayed.

 ![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072495391/blobid1.png)

In addition, you can set a date range in the filter to get statistics on the number of views and the cost of the service for the selected period.

It is not necessary to fill in all filter fields. Enter the required information and click **Search**. To display the full table, click **Clear**.

## Adding and editing a webinar

To add a new webinar, use the** Create webinar** button at the top of the page.

To edit the webinar, click on its name

## Deactivate and delete the webinar

To temporarily disable the webinar, move the slider to the gray position. To enable the webinar, move the slider to the green position.

 ![blobid2.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270952/blobid2.png)

To completely delete the webinar, click on X.

On this page, you can set up receiving statistics on subscriptions to your email.

![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073105252/blobid0.png)

Select the frequency of receiving the statistics by ticking the checkboxes.

Statistics are sent to the email from the **Your Email** field of your **private account**.

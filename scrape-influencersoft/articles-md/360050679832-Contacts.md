The concept of Contacts is broader than [subscribers](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850591-Subscribers-). Subscribers are those contacts who have agreed to receive your newsletters. You can only contact these subscribers, not the other contacts, using InfluencerSoft.

However, you can also have contacts, e.g., received during offline events. You can add such contacts to your database, and then call them or send an email.

You can access the contact page from the **Contacts **menu, then the **Leads** submenu.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/878-01.png)

The form allows you to:

-
View summary information for contacts

-
View detailed information for each contact

-
Import contacts

-
Add new contacts

-
Edit contacts

-
Add and delete contacts to groups

-
Send letters

-
Export contacts

-
Customize the table

## View

The **View **button allows you to adjust the visibility of the columns of the table. When you click on it, the pop-up window **Visible fields **opens.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2019-10-26_11-42-36.png)

By ticking or ticking the checkboxes, you can add and remove certain columns from the screen.

## Filter

The filter allows you to customize the output of table rows (individual contacts) according to the specified conditions. When you open the filter, it looks like this.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2019-10-26_11-46-16.png)

You can customize the output of contacts from a specific group. Moving by category is done by clicking on the blue folder. Select a group by clicking on the empty checkbox. It should have a blue tick. All groups in the category can be chosen by clicking on the checkbox category.

Clicking on the** Add Condition** button allows you to expand additional filter fields.

## Summary information on contacts

At the top of the page, in the **Total **block, the total number of contacts for the account is displayed.

## Detailed information for each contact

When you click on a name or email contact, you will go to the Contact card (Shop-> Account section) containing the complete contact information.

Click the [group](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050401352-Contact-Lists) to which the contact belongs to open the [Subscribers](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850591-Subscribers-) form, with the selection of all subscribers of this group.

The blue number next to the name of the group shows how many more groups this contact contains. When you click on it, a full list of groups is displayed.

## Import contacts

The **Import **button opens a [form](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050680012-Import-Contacts) where you can apply for the transfer of subscribers from another service, or add it manually, e.g., when transferring from one mailing list to another.

## Add a contact

The **Create Contact** button allows you to [fill in the data for a new contact](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050677012-Add-Contact-).

***Note!**** You cannot send e**mails to manually added contacts. However, you can send SMS and make calls.*

## Editing contacts

To edit contacts, you must use the filter to find a specific contact/s. You can also select contacts by ticking the checkboxes of the first column.

After that, you can change one of the following fields:

-
Surname

-
Name

-
Middle name

-
Phone

-
City

-
Description

To do this, click on the button with the gear and select **Edit **in the menu that opens.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-21-2.png)

In the window that opens, select which field to edit and set a new value.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-22-2.png)

## Add to group

To add contacts to a [list](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050401352-Contact-Lists), you must use the filter to find a specific contact/s. You can also select contacts by ticking the checkboxes of the first column.

Click the button with the gear and select **Add to group **in the menu that opens.

In the window that opens, specify which group the contacts will be added to.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-23-2.png)

Click the **Add to Contacts** button.

## Deleting contacts from a group

To delete contacts from a group, you must select contacts by ticking the checkboxes of the first column. For convenience, you can first find a specific contact/s using the filter.

It is necessary to click the button with the gear and in the opened menu, select **Delete from lists.**

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-24-2.png)

In the window that opens, use the checkboxes to specify which group to delete contacts from. All groups in the category can be chosen by clicking on the checkbox category.

**If you receive**** a request from the subscriber for the complete removal of personal data from the store (GDPR) or want to completely delete your data, then send us a request to the support service. At the moment, it is impossible to completely delete contacts from the store, but we can delete ****one**** contact if you received such a request so as not to violate the GDPR law.**

## Export contacts

To export contacts to a computer, you must use the filter to find the desired contact/s. You can also select contacts by ticking the checkboxes of the first column.

It is necessary to click on the button with the gear and choose the export format from the menu that opens:

-
CSV

-
Excel

-
Plain text

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-25-2.png)

When you click on the selected format, the contacts will be exported to the computer (to the default download folder).

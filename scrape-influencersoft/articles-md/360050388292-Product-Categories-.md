## Filter

The filter allows you to select a category by name.

 ![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270392/blobid1.png)

You must enter a name in the filter field and click the **Search** button.

If the name was entered in its entirety, then one category will be displayed. If you enter a portion of the title – all categories that have that text in its title will be shown in the search results.

To display the entire category table, click the **Clear** button.

To add, edit, and delete categories

The **Add** button is used to [create a new category](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850911-Adding-and-Editing-Product-Categories-).

Clicking on the category name allows you to [edit it.](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850911-Adding-and-Editing-Product-Categories-)

The cross at the end of the line is used to [delete the category](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850911-Adding-and-Editing-Product-Categories-).

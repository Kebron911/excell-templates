### Table view

To adjust the table, click the **View** button at the top of the screen. The order table can be customized by adding or removing columns.

![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270112/blobid1.png)

By default, the **base** fields are visible. To add/or delete fields remove fields (columns of the table), you must add/ or remove the check marks in the corresponding checkboxes.

## Order management

When you click on the number of any order in the table, you go to the **Order No. …** form, which [allows you to manage the selected order](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050851011-Order-Management-Order-Card-).

## Complete order information

If you click on the free space in the row of any order (for example, next to the order number, phonephone, or email, but not on them!), then detailed information on this order will appear under the line.

 ![blobid9.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494391/blobid9.png)

The **top section** containshas a tablet with information about calls to the client (if there are any). The message “There were no calls made on this order” appears if there were no calls.

In the **middle part**** **– , the order data is shown.

In addition to viewing the data, you can add information about the client here. To do this, click the **Add** button opposite the field with the same name. The **Edit Customer Information** window opens, where you can add any comments in n a custom form.

## Create an order

If during a phone call, the customer expressed interest in buying a product you can [create an order](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850951-Create-an-Order-) and send it to the customer’s email. To do this, use the **Create** button, found above the summary table with orders.

 ![blobid17.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494471/blobid17.png)

## Reminder about payment

InfluencerSoft allows you to [set up an automatic series of reminder](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050388252-Payment-Reminders-via-Email-) emails for customers who placed an order but did not pay for it. To add and edit emails, you need to click the “**Payment reminder**” button.

 ![blobid18.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494491/blobid18.png)

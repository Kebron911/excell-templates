/[Advertise](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245531-Advertise) / Offers

This page contains all the promotional materials that the author of the affiliate has prepared.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/14834975777044)

The description of the program would appear at the top of the page only if the author has added it.

Below you can find the promotional products and materials list divided into three groups:

-
Free magnets (the **Free** button, opens by default);

-
Paid products (click the** Paid** button);

-
Attracting partners (to see multi-level affiliates program, click the **Partners** button).

Clicking on the **Paid** tab adds a block with prices and conditions for partners for each product. Otherwise, the structure of the tabs is identical and the function on all the tabs are the same.

Clicking on the name in the first column opens the advertised page (subscription, selling, or partner registration page, correspondingly).

The link in the **Your ****A****ffiliate ****L****ink** column is a link to the same advertised page, but with a built-in affiliate identification. Use this link for advertising purposes.

The icon with a chain shackle next to the link allows you to add UTM-tags for calculating advertising channels.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/14835043772052)

Here you can specify the source of traffic. If you advertise a partner in your mailing list, it can be through email on the site – site; if it is contextual advertising – Google or others.

It is not necessary to set the advertising campaign, ads, and keywords. But if you want to get more detailed statistics, you should.

Depending on the source, you can determine their values. For a site, it can be a page with an advertising banner or image; for a mailing list, a group of contacts and/or an email subject, and so on.

Once the link is formed, you can copy it into the** Your Link for Counting Clicks** field and use it in the corresponding advertising campaign.

The number in the **A****dvertising ****D****rafts** column shows how many materials to advertise the selected product have been prepared by the author. Clicking on the number directs you to the page of promotional materials.

If there is number 1 in the column, it means that there is no advertising material. As you are redirected to the page, there will be the affiliate link available, like in the **Your ****A****ffiliate****s** link column.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/14835012937108)

Clicking on the **Add ****an**** Ad Tag** button opens the **New ****C****ampaign** button.

-
add, edit and delete funnels

-
view statistics for each funnel

-
go to detailed information by the selected funnel

-
adjust the visibility of statistics data of the funnels

## Filter

The filter allows you to set up preferences of the output for statistics by funnels.

 ![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073372171/blobid1.png)

The **Funne****l**** ****name **field allows you to find the funnel by its name. If you enter a part of a name, all of the funnels that contain this part will be displayed.

The **0-step period fro****m ****and to** field allows you to set a period to calculate the statistics.

The most popular periods of time (today, yesterday, this month, last month, 30 days) are contained in the quick filter at the top of the page.

The **Show by ****click** field allows you to retrieve statistics by the first and last click.

The **Only in favorites **check-box allows you to display the funnels from your favorites.

The side filter menu contains the **Advertising** and **Tags **tabs, that allow you to retrieve statistics not from the whole funnel but just by particular parameters.

From the **Advertising **tab, you can retrieve statistics by the particular [channel/source/campaign/ad/keys](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051550771-New-Campaign-).

![blobid2.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073105052/blobid2.png)

At the [Tag ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051177191-Tags)tab in the field with the same name, you can specify a tag to retrieve the statistics of the funnel.

![blobid3.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073372231/blobid3.png)

## Adding, editing, and deleting the sales funnel

The **Add **button allows you to go to the [Add Sales Funnel page](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051185531-The-Sales-Funnels-Making-a-Funnel).

Clicking the name of the funnels will take you to the Editing page.

To delete the selected funnel, click the cross mark in the last column.

## Adding the funnel to the favorites

To add a funnel to the favorites, click the star in the first column. The star will change its color from grey to yellow. The funnels added to favorites are displayed first in the list.

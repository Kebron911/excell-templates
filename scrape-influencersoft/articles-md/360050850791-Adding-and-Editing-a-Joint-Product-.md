/[Store](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009133412-Store) / Adding and editing a joint product

This page allows you to add new or edit existing products created in co-authorship.

*The pages for adding and editing are identical to each other except for the page title (Add or Edit respectively).*

***Attention!**** The product is created directly on the *[*Products** *](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050388332-Products-)*page. This page is only for setting up payments to the co-author.*

 ![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072269352/blobid0.png)

You must select the co-authored product for .

After this, it is determined whether the partner commission will be deducted before settling with the co-author (by switching the radio button).

Further, the co-author’s payment is set as percentage of the cost  or as a fixed amount.

After making the necessary changes, click the **Save **button. The co-authored product will be added to the list on the [Products of the co-author …. page.](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050851131-Products-of-the-Co-Author-)

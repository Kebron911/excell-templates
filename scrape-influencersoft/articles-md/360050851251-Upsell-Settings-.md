This page allows you to configure the upsell for the selected product.

Upsell is an offer to buy a product that complements the basic purchase that appears when the order is placed. As a rule, this offer is made with a substantial discount. The presence of the upsell allows you to increase the average receipt for the purchase.

You can configure several upsells to specific products.

 ![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494871/blobid0.png)

The page consists of two tabs:

-
Main settings

-
Additional options

There is a form at the bottom of each tab for selecting products for sale.

 ![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270532/blobid1.png)

By default, the table has two lines – the upper one allows you to specify the product to be offered as an upsell and the consent button text. Lower line is the text of the decline button.

## Choice of scheme for sale

To go to the upsell configuration, click here opposite the desired product:

 ![blobid2.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270552/blobid2.png)

By default, the table has two rows – the top allows you to specify the product that will be offered as an upsell and the text of the consent button. Bottom is the text of the decline button.

![blobid3.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494891/blobid3.png)

In addition, both lines contain the ability to configure further resales, e.g. if the client has agreed to buy the upsell, asking for the sale in the top line, the next step is to offer him Upsell number 2. If the client declined the offer, after asking for the sale on the bottom line, we can offer him Upsell number 3.

And there can be several such iterations.

*Attention! Attempting multiple resales in cases where the customer keeps declining is not recommended, as this **may cause you to lose your customer.*

The **Add **button adds one more row to the table, allowing you to configure the selection of an alternative product. This option should be used if there are several versions of the same product. For example, a course on a DVD disc sent by the postal service and the same course as an electronic copy for download.

 ![blobid4.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494911/blobid4.png)

*Attention! In this case, you must change the text on the buttons so that the client understands exactly what he is getting in each case.*

Buttons with up and down arrows allow you to change the order of buttons on the page.

## Choosing a product for resale

### Option 1

Clicking the **Select Product** button opens the product selection window.

 ![blobid5.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494931/blobid5.png)

You must select the product for the upsell and specify the price at which it will be offered as an after-sale. Then click the **Save and configure the affiliate **button.

You will go to the **Edit Partner Commissions** page, where you can change the payment settings to partners and call-center when buying this product as an option.

 ![blobid6.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494951/blobid6.png)

When you click the **Save **button, you return to the current page.

To change the text of the button, click on it. You will be taken to the Edit upsell page, where you can change the button.

### Option 2

You can simultaneously change the text of the button and set the product for the resale by clicking on the text of the button.

 ![blobid7.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270572/blobid7.png)

You must select the product, set the price, if necessary, change the text of the button, and click Save. You will be returned to the Upsell Configuration page.

 ![blobid8.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270592/blobid8.png)

Clicking the blue button with a % sign will allow you to go to the Edit Partner Commissions page and, if necessary, change them.

## Main settings

This tab allows you to add a description of the upsell. Here you can put a small selling text and add a picture. InfluencerSoft’s default text editor is used.

 ![blobid9.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270612/blobid9.png)

*Attention! Do not **remove** the # ORDER_FORM # tag. It **displays the** order buttons.*

The **Test page** button allows you to see what the page will look like (opens in a new window or tab).

## Additional Settings

On this tab, you need to specify how many minutes to allot before  removing the offer for resale and proceeding to the main account. This is relevant if the client is thinking and cannot decide (does not click on any of the buttons).

 ![blobid10.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494971/blobid10.png)

On this tab, you also need to specify after how many minutes to remove the offer for resale and go to the main account. This is relevant if the client is thinking and cannot decide (does not click on any of the buttons).

In addition, you can specify for which [groups of contacts](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050401352-Contact-Lists) to not show this offer. Usually, customers who have previously bought this product are selected here.

Moving by category is done by clicking on the blue folder. Select a group by clicking on the checkbox (empty square) – it should have a blue tick. The choice of all groups in the category can be done by clicking on the checkbox category.

## Activate upsell

By default, the upsell is disabled. Even after configuring the settings and saving, the upsell will not be shown to clients. To activate the upsell, use the slider at the top of the page (on any tab).

 ![blobid11.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270632/blobid11.png)

To activate the upsell, you must move the slider to the right. Once the color is green on the slider, the upsell will be offered to customers.

 ![blobid12.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494991/blobid12.png)

If you need to disable the upsell, you must select the product to which it applies – on the products page, go to the upsell settings page and disable the upsell.

This page allows you to add and edit graphical advertising materials.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/1576069340261.png)

*The Adding and Editing pages are absolutely identical except for their names.*

Upload the image from your computer (the **Select file **button) or enter the **URL ****address** (if it’s an image from the Internet).

At the **Banner Description** window you can enter the explanatory text for the image.

Click on the **Save **button and the banner will be added to the promotional materials list.

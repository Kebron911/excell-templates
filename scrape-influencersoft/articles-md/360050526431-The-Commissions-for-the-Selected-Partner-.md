/[Affiliates](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245511-Affiliates) / The Commissions for the Selected Partner

The application allows you to manage the individual affiliate’s commissions for the selected partner.

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-45-3.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-45-3.png)

The table contains a list of products with customized individual commissions for the selected affiliate.

The **Add** button allows you to [add a new individual commission](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050065912-Adding-the-Commissions-for-the-Selected-Partner-).

As you click the product name, you go to the page of [editing commission rates for a partner](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050065912-Adding-the-Commissions-for-the-Selected-Partner-).

To delete the selected commission, click on the cross mark in the last column.

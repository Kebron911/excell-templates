Corporate mail is your company’s own email on your personal domain.

If several years ago owning corporate mail was more a tribute to the image, now the use of corporate mail has become an urgent necessity. Especially for the information business and any business working with mass mailings.

Thus, the only alternative is to create a mailbox on a personal domain.

**What services does corporate mail connection offer?**

We recommend choosing from the offers of the largest players on the market, as these are the safest and most profitable solutions.

To register your domain mail with **Gmail**, you can follow the link:

[https://www.google.com/intl/en/mail/help/work.html](https://www.google.com/intl/en/mail/help/work.html)

Please note that, unlike other services, business mail (corporate and domain) Gmail has several paid functions and restrictions, since you connect not only corporate mail, but also a set of cloud applications ([G Suite](https://gsuite.google.com/signup/basic/welcome)) in which it is included!

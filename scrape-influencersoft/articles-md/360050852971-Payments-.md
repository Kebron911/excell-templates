On this page you can see the amount of commissions charged and paid out.

![mceclip0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/7092715509140/mceclip0.png)

**Filters **allow you to set up the period of the data output.

![mceclip1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/7092726008468/mceclip1.png)

Set up the beginning and end of the period with the calendar.  Click on the **Search **button to apply the filter. To retrieve the data for the whole time, click on the **Clear **button.

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/1576069118427.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/1576069118427.png)

Here you have only one link to the registration in the program.

## Editing Descriptions

To edit a description of the affiliates program, click on its name.

## Deactivation

Move the slider to the left in the Status column to make a registration temporarily unavailable for the low-level partners. The button changes color to dark gray and your partners would not see this link in their cabinets. To turn it back on, move the slider to the right. The multi-leveled program becomes available for the partners to promote.

## Promotional Materials

To prepare promotional materials such as banners and text for each product, click on the number in the [Offers](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050526011-Promotional-Materials-for-Attracting-the-Partners-) column to work on them.

The number in the button shows the number of promotional materials added at the moment.

## Others

Clicking the **Paid **or **Free** buttons allows you to go to the relevant pages. There you can adjust promotional materials for [free](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050525831-Promo-for-Affiliates-Free-Product-Promotional-Materials-) or [paid products](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050525951-Promo-for-Affiliates-Paid-Product-Promotional-Materials-).

# How to view User Click History (Activities)

/ [Contacts](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245391-Contacts) / How to view User Click History (Activities)

This page shows the activity of the selected employees /users (at what time and which pages were opened).

![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073373231/blobid0.png)

When you go to the page, a filter window where you must specify at least one of the search parameters opens.

![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073373291/blobid1.png)

You can choose from:

-
Period of activity (date and time, from and to)

- Login/username of the employee/user

-
IP address from which the login was made

-
Address of the page that was opened

You must enter the data in the filter and click the **Search **button. To remove the filter, click the **Clear **button.

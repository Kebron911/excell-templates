This page allows you to add new or edit existing co-authors.

*Pages for adding and editing categories are identical to each other except for the page title (Add or Edit respectively).*

The page consists of two tabs:

-
Main settings

-
Additional details

![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072269272/blobid1.png)

## Main settings

On this tab you need to set the login and password of the co-author so they can enter their profile), as well as email, and name.

## Additional Details

This tab allows you to specify the bank details for payment to the co-author and his phone number.

 ![blobid2.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072493211/blobid2.png)

In addition, the **Show co-author client’s contacts** checkbox allows you to configure whether the co-author will see the  personal information of the customers who bought the joint product in his profile.

After making the necessary changes, click the **Save **button. The co-author will be added to the list on the [Co-authors](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050387992-Co-Authors-)[ ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050387992-Co-Authors-)page.

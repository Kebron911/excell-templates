/[Store ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009133412-Store)/ Products of the co-author

This page allows you to view, [add, edit](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850791-Adding-and-Editing-a-Joint-Product-), and remove products created with the selected co-author.

 ![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270432/blobid0.png)

The table has a list of joint products and information ona co-author's commissions and deductions.

To [add a new product](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850791-Adding-and-Editing-a-Joint-Product-), use the **Add **button.

Clicking on the product name opens the [edit](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850791-Adding-and-Editing-a-Joint-Product-) form.

The X in the last column is to remove the product.

The Back to the “Co-authors” page button is used to return to the page with a [list of all the co-authors](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050387992-Co-Authors-).

On this page you can see statistics on the viral promotion – the total number of registered users and the number of subscribers referred by each participant.

 ![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072272552/blobid0.png)

## Select a period

By default, the data for the entire action period of the promotion is displayed. To view data for a certain period, use the **Set period** button.

 ![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072272572/blobid1.png)

You must enter the start and end dates of the period of interest and click the **Set** button.

## Export Subscribers

The **Export All **button uploads a list of all the participants of the promotion to your computer.

The **Export by Number of Visits** button allows you to select participants for upload.

 ![blobid2.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072272592/blobid2.png)

You must enter the number of referrals and click the **Upload **button.

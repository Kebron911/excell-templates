On the Leads page you can see the time and groups people have subscribed to. If the author has set up the source, channel, campaign, ad, keys in the affiliate program, the table will display this data:

 ![](https://help.influencersoft.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/01-42.png)

By default, the data for the whole period is displayed. But you can set up the output by using the **filter**** **tab.

 ![](https://help.influencersoft.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2018-07-19_12-09-11.png)

You can search by the name, subscribed group, and selected period by the first click, or the last click.

You do not have to fill out all the fields, you can click on the **Search **button to apply the filter. To see the whole table, click on the **Clear **button.

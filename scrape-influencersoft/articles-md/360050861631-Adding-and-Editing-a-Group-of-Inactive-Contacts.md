This page allows you to add new or edit existing groups of inactive contacts.

A group of inactive contacts is essentially an [auto-group](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050400112-Add-and-Edit-Contact-List), where contacts are added after a specified period of inactivity (those who do not open emails and do not click on the links in the emails).

*The pages for adding and editing are identical to each other except for the page title (Add or edit**,** respectively).*

The page consists of two tabs:

- Basic data

- Inactivity

## Basic data

**![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2020-02-06_22-57-52.png)**

Enter the name of the group of inactive contacts in the **Name **field.

Use the **Sequences **checkbox to specify whether members of the inactive contact group continue to receive the [main series](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050848451-Email-Series-) mail.

Select the [sender](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050848471-Mailing-Settings-) from which letters will be sent.

The remaining fields are optional.

You can:

-
Arrange groups of inactive contacts by combining their [categories](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050400732-Categories) (they must be [created](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050400832-Add-and-Edit-Categories) first). Also, the distribution by category can be adjusted at any time.

-
Set the redirect page after unsubscribing.

## Inactivity

In this tab, the conditions under which the contact will be added to the group of inactive contacts are specified.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2020-10-26_17-27-11.png)

The addition of any other group/s to the group of participants can be configured.

Moving by category is done by clicking on the blue folder. Selecting a group by clicking on the empty checkbox. It should have a blue tick. All groups in the category can be chosen by clicking on the checkbox category.

In the field **Сhoose**** those who****, list down** an inactivity condition, e.g., – they did not open any email or did not click even once.

The **Period **field specifies the number of days of inactivity after which the contact will be added to the group.

The** Enable this list** checkbox allows you to configure whether to track or not the activity of a contact on a group of inactive contacts.

After making the necessary changes, click the **Save **button. A group of inactive contacts will be added to the list on the [Contact groups](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050401352-Contact-Lists) page.

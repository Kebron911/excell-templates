## Basic information

This tab specifies the name of the promotion and description of the participants.

The description is the text that will be on the individual page of each participant (where they can track the progress of attracting subscribers).

In the **Where ****to invite friends**** to**** **field, specify the URL on which the InfluencerSoft subscription form can be found. The page and subscription form can be either on the service or on any third-party resource.

## Additional information

 ![blobid2.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072495031/blobid2.png)

Choose whether the action will be perpetual, or has a validity period. If action is limited in time, specify the end day and time.

Enter the text that the participants of the promotion can use when placing their personal link in social networks. The more interesting and catchier the text, the higher the response.

The title and text of the email to participants contain instructions, which the participants of the promotion will receive immediately after their subscription. This letter has a link to *{$ link}* on a personal page (the description for it is on the Basic tab).

### Gift for recommendation

Here you need to specify the name of the gift for taking part in the promotion and the number of subscribers signed up to receive a gift.

 ![blobid3.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072495051/blobid3.png)

![blobid4.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072495071/blobid4.png)

Here you can see a link to download a gift or instructions on how to receive it.

You can set up a multi-stage viral campaign, for example, to offer one gift – for one subscriber referred, the second – for three referrals, and third for the purchase by paid subscribers of a certain number of paid products.

To configure such a multi-stage promotion, click on the Add another gift button.

 ![blobid5.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270672/blobid5.png)

The second (and subsequent) products need to choose the type of gift – subscribers or money. In the first case, a gift will be given for attracting a certain number of subscribers, in the second case, for the purchase of paid products by attracted subscribers. The goal for the subsequent promotions, following the first one, need to be identified – is the goal to gain more subscribers or increase spend with the customers? If the goal is to gain more subscribers, a gift for a certain number of referrals can be set. If the goal is to increase customer spend, then the amount referred subscribers spend can be set to receive a gift. In the second case, you will need to specify the amount in dollars.

 ![blobid7.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072270692/blobid7.png)

After making the necessary changes, click the **Save **button. The promotion will be added to the list on the [Viral promotion](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050851671-Recommendations-Viral-Promotion-and-Viral-Action-) page.

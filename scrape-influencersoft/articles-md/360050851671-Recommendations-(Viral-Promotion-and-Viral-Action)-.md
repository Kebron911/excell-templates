/[Website ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245331-Website)/ Recommendations (Viral promotion and Viral action)

Viral action is a way to attract new subscribers through the subscribers themselves. When it is conducted, access to any product is given not for money, but for new subscribers who have come through an individual link of the participant.

Page **Promotions** allows you to [add and edit promotion](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050851291-Adding-and-Editing-a-Viral-Promotion-), and [view statistics](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050389292-Viral-promotion-statistics-) on the promotions held.

 ![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072496551/blobid0.png)

The **Add promotion** button opens the [page for creating a new viral promo](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050851291-Adding-and-Editing-a-Viral-Promotion-).

To [edit an existing action](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050851291-Adding-and-Editing-a-Viral-Promotion-), click on its name.

The **Registration form** button allows you to [set up a subscription form](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050851391-Viral-Promotion-Registration-Form-) to take part in the promotion.

A button with a bar chart opens [statistics on the action](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050389292-Viral-promotion-statistics-).

The X in the last column serves to remove the viral action.

**The subscription entry point** link allows you to view the page where the conditions for participation in the promotion can be found.

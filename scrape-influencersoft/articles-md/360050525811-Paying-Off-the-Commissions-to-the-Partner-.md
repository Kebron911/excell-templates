On this page you can send a payment notification, see the history of mutual transactions, get affiliate links for a particular partner and adjust individual commissions for the selected affiliate.

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/1576068310053.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/1576068310053.png)

The page has three tabs:

- paying off;

- the history of mutual transactions;

- payment details.

## Paying Off

On this tab you can form and send a partner a notification to pay off the commissions.

***Please note!** Clicking the **Pay Out** button doesn’t send your partner any money. You proceed to a real payment from your wallets, not from your InfluencerSoft balance.*

## The History Of Mutual Transactions

This tab allows you to look through the transactions history and pay off the commissions to the selected affiliate.

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-47-4.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-47-4.png)

You can also change partner’s full name, if necessary. Just enter a new **full name** and click the **Change** button.

Clicking the invoice number in the **Description** column opens the Invoice #… page.

## Payment Details

The Payment Details tab allows you to look through the partners’ payment details.

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-48-3.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-48-3.png)

## Affiliate Links

Clicking the **Affiliate Links** button opens the user’s Affiliate Link page. There you can see all the affiliate links with a built-in identifier of the selected partner.

The page contains 2 tabs  – for free products

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/1576068402017.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/1576068402017.png)

and for paid products:

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-50-3.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-50-3.png)

To return to the Paying Off Commissions page click the button with the same name at the bottom of the page.

## Editing Commissions

Clicking the **Edit Commissions** button opens the [Edit Affiliate Commissions](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050526431-The-Commissions-for-the-Selected-Partner-) window where you can set up individual commissions for the selected partner.

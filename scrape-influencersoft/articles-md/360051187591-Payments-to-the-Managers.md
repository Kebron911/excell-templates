/ [Reports ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009133772-Reports)/ Payments to managers

This page allows you to look over the information on payment amounts to managers and how to make the payments.

![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073106652/blobid0.png)

Use the filter to search for the manager. You can search for a manager by login or email.

![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073106672/blobid1.png)

Clicking a manager’s name opens the page for editing the employee’s information. Here, you can change the rights and other settings of the selected manager.

If there is some money to be paid to the employee, the **Payout** button will at the last column to make a payment.

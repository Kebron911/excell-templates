/[ Contacts](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245391-Contacts) / How to Add a New Lead (Contact)

The Add lead page allows you to fill in the data for a new contact.

![mceclip0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360079631172/mceclip0.png)

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/Screenshot-27-2.png)

***Note!**** You cannot *[send emails](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245371-Campaigns)* to manually added contacts.*

It is necessary to enter the data into its corresponding lines (all fields are optional), then click the **Save **button.

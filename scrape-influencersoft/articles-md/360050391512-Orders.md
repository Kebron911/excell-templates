The **Orders **page allows you to look over the periods and the products that have been billed and charged commissions for. If the author has set up the source/channel and campaign, ads, keys in the affiliate program, the table will display this data.

 ![](https://help.influencersoft.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2018-07-19_12-26-10.png)

**Filters **allow you to set up the data output for the selected parameters.

 ![](https://help.influencersoft.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2018-07-19_12-27-20.png)

You can search by the order number, contact name, product, date of billing, date of payment collection, and payment status.

You do not have to fill out all the filter fields. Click on the **Search **button to apply the filter. To display the whole table, click on the **Clear **button.

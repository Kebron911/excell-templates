A lead card is a tool to work with particular clients and subscribers in InfluencerSoft. It will help you get detailed information about any lead.

## How to search for a lead card

There are several ways you can find a lead card: from Contacts – Leads, from Campaigns – Subscribers, and from Store – Orders. In some cases, you can open a card by clicking its name or lead email (in some cases, you just click “No Email”).

Here is the first method:

- Open Contacts – Leads

- Find the required lead by applying filter (or visually)

- Click the email of the required lead in the Email column

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/leadcard1-2.png)

## Structure of a lead card

A lead card contains 14 tabs:

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2021-01-09_232652.jpg)

-
A cogwheel icon “To Add” attaches a lead to the selected [process](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051179551-Processes), a [lead list](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050401352-Contact-Lists), or you can apply [rules](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051178391-Rules) to it.

-
General information about a lead’s key information. In this block, in addition to the name, email, and phone number, information on the address, delivery address, time zone, and points accrued will be displayed.

-
[Lead history](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050397772-Contact-History-) displays all changes in a lead.

-
[Tasks](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050859551-Tasks) allow you to look through all tasks for this lead and assign a new task.

-
Information about lead lists in which a lead is attached.

-
A list of all pages visited by a lead.

-
All orders of a lead.

-
Information about the calls concerning the lead’s orders.

-
A list of sent [email series](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050848451-Email-Series-) (and [actions of a chain](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050385272-Add-Edit-and-Copy-Email-Series-)).

-
A list of [broadcasts](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050848431-Broadcasts-) sent to a subscriber.

-
Summarized information about call tasks, which are connected with this lead.

-
Information about click-through with the links with advert (and referral) tags. In other words, lead’s clicks. Read more about the definition of a click in our service in this [article](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050390812-The-Settings-of-the-Affiliate-Program-).

-
Information about the leads associated with the lead. Related contacts are the leads that are used to perform some actions (subscription, payment, click-through) in one browser, but they are not merged. In other words, these are the leads of one person, but with different emails.

-
Information about the leads merged with this lead. Merged contacts are the leads which are merged into one lead, while the second lead was deleted.

## Lead’s financial statistics

It becomes available if a lead generates at least one order:

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/leadcard3.png)

-
Orders – total sum of all orders generated for a particular email.

-
Expected – total sum of all orders with “Expected” status generated for a particular email.

-
Confirmed — total sum of all orders generated for a particular email with “Confirmed” status.

-
Paid — total sum of the paid orders for a particular lead.

-
Income – total sum of the income from lead’s orders. It is calculated as Paid — Expenses. Find out more about the expenses on a product in this [article](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050850851-Adding-and-Editing-a-Product-), they can also be defined for the whole store.

-
Profit – a sum that is left when we subtract the expenses (7+8+9) and refunds (10) from the income (5).

-
Affiliate commission – a sum of all payments accrued to the referrals, which bring this client into your sales funnel.

-
Call center – a sum of all payments accrued to the employees who persuade a client to buy your products.

-
Co-authored — a sum of all payments accrued to the co-authors for the sales of the collaboratively developed products.

-
Refund – a sum of all refunds paid to a particular client.

## A lead

The main tab contains general information about a subscriber, including a lead’s email, first name, last name, middle name, phone number, and city. The fields depend on what a subscriber enters in a subscription or order form when creating a lead. Some fields can be edited by you or your staff (for example, a phone number or lead’s names; appoint a personal manager).

Additional fields will be displayed if you define them in [Contacts – Settings](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051177871-CRM-Settings).

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/leadcard4.png)

Status – it shows whether a client can be sent a letter from the system (Activated or Subscribed statuses), or the letters cannot be sent (other statuses).

Here you can also assign a lead to a personal manager and enter extra information about a lead.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2021-02-20_11.png)

-
Personal manager – a manager in charge of this lead (the manager can be assigned, read more in this [article](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360044767611-The-Manager-Is-Responsible-for-the-Order-and-for-the-Client-What-Is-the-Difference-)).

-
Customer information – extra information about a client that can be manually added.

-
Points – The points displayed in the contact card are used only for integration with services where gamification is configured.

## Lead’s history

This tab displays information about the previous actions with a particular lead. At the moment, there are the following actions:

-
Creating a lead

-
Changing the general information about a lead

**![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072506671/blobid0.png)**

Also, lead history displays the changes made by the [Zapier service](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050406312-Integration-with-Zapier-com-) through the “Add and Update Lead” action (if you have integration configured). In this case, the note (next to the date and time) will indicate “Zapier – Add and Update Lead”.

## Lists

This tab displays all lists of the selected lead:

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/leadcard7.png)

-
List of leads – the name of the list which a lead has joined.

-
Date – when a lead subscribed to this list of leads.

-
Partner – a login of the referral which brought the subscriber to this list of leads.

-
Tag – a tag that subscribed to a user to the list.

-
Source and channel – UTM tags in the link where a user has registered on the list.

-
Campaign, ads, keys – UTM tags for a more advanced level.

-
Status – lead’s status in the system. It describes the possibility to send letters to the lead’s email.

-
Deletion from a list of leads — this button can delete a lead from the list. A lead can be deleted from usual lists, while you cannot delete a lead manually from auto-lists.

## Orders

This tab contains a list of all orders generated by a particular lead:

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/leadcard9.png)

- Number – order number.

-
Created – displays the order generation date and time.

-
Products – displays [product ID](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050391512-Orders) attached to the generated order.

-
Partners – displays the login of a referral who will be accrued [commission](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050526431-The-Commissions-for-the-Selected-Partner-) fee under this order.

-
$ – displays the sum of a particular order.

-
Payment – displays the payment date or the current order status (confirmed, refund, canceled).

-
Source and channel – shows UTM tags attached to a particular order.

- Campaign, ads, keys – shows additional UTM tags.

## Automatic chain and broadcasts messages

The tabs show all automatic and broadcast letters sent to a user.

Automatic chain:

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2021-01-10_193017.jpg)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2021-01-10_193017.jpg)

-
List – the name of a list that is selected for automatic chains (if this list is deleted, the field will be blank).

-
Letter number – number of a letter (or action) in an automatic chain, this letter is sent to a subscriber (if the list is deleted, the list will be renumbered).

-
Name – name of a letter sent to a subscriber.

-
Done – date and time of sending a letter.

-
Opened – date and time of opening a letter (if it is blank, the letter is not opened).

-
Clicked – date and time of clicking the link in the letter (if it is blank, no link has been clicked, or [click-through counter is disabled](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050848511-Sending-and-Editing-Emails-by-Lists-)).

-
Unsubscribed – date and time of unsubscribing from the mailshot by clicking the link in the letter (if it is blank, the letter is not unsubscribed).

-
Spam – date and time of clicking the button “This is spam” added to the letter by the service (buttons “Unsubscribe” and “This is spam” can be added in [Campaigns – Settings – Mailing settings](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050848471-Mailing-Settings-)), or clicking any button “This is spam” in a letter.

A table with the data in the “broadcasts messages” tab contains the same information, except for the following options:

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/2021-01-10_193535.jpg)

-
Number –  number of broadcasts letter in the system.

-
Sent – date and time of sending a letter.

-
Campaign– displays a link tag “Campaign”, which a user followed.

## Related contacts

Related contacts are the leads used to make some actions (to subscribe, to pay, to follow a link) in one browser, but these leads have not been merged. In other words, these are the leads of one person, but with different emails.

These features are very user-friendly as you do not have to search for the old leads of a client, if the client paid from a different email or if the client made a mistake in email address during registration.

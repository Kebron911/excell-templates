## Getting an Affiliate Link

At the top of each tab, the **Link** field contains an affiliate link that you can use for your advertising.

## Adding Advertising Tags

Clicking on the **Add ****Advertising t****ag** button opens the **New ****C****ampaign** window that allows you to add UTM-tags for calculating the advertising channels.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/38893131049236)

You can specify the source of traffic here. If you advertise a partner in your mailing list, it can be through email, on the site – site; if it is contextual advertising, it can be through Google or others.

You do not have to set the advertising campaign, ads, and keywords. But if you want to get more detailed statistics, you should.

And depending on the source you can determine their values. So, for a site, it can be a page with an advertising banner or image; for a mailing list, it can be a group of contacts and/or a subject of an email, and so on.

Once the link is formed, you can copy it into **Your Reference-Counting of Clicks** field and use it in the corresponding advertising campaign.

## Advertising Banners

Graphic advertising materials are found on this page.

Under the image, there is the HTML code. When you insert it into the page, you add the image with the built-in affiliate link. As you click on the banner, you will be directed to the advertising page.

## Advertising Articles

Advertising texts can be found on this page.

The analytical sales funnel is a powerful marketing tool that allows you to find and address the weak points that lose potential clients.

## What is an analytical funnel?

For example, we have got the simplest funnel – the subscription for freebies and the automatic series of letters that offer to buy the first cheap product.

With the help of an analytics funnel, you could break down this chain of actions into steps and follow up on the conversion. The simplest way is to do two-step analytics:

Step 1 – subscribing to newsletters
Step 2 – paying for products

But this funnel doesn’t show anything except the most general conversion from the subscriber to the customer. You could break down the process into several steps:

Step 1 – a person subscribed to newsletters
Step 2 – he or she activated the subscription
Step 3 – he or she read the email
Step 4 – he or she followed a link in the email
Step 5 – he or she processed a bill
Step 6 – he and she paid the bill

Such a detailed funnel will show at what step you lose your potential clients. It also allows you to adjust the most effective selling chain.

## Adjusting the funnel

Let’s see the instruction.

![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073371911/blobid0.png)

Here, you should name a funnel.

Using the **Add Step** button, you can create the number of steps you need.

You can roll up the steps by clicking their names for your comfort. You should name each step and select an event for the statistics to be calculated.

## Setting up the events inside the funnel

### Subscription

If you have selected the ‘subscription for newsletters’ event, you may select the subscription you want to view.

![blobid1.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073104572/blobid1.png)

After that, select which subscribers will be calculated: all, activated, not –activated, or unsubscribed.

### Processing a bill

If the event of processing bills is selected, specify the product for which data will be calculated.

![blobid2.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073371931/blobid2.png)

Add some tags, if necessary.

### Paying a bill

If the event of paying bills is selected, specify the product for which data will be calculated. You could also use some tags.

![blobid3.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073104612/blobid3.png)

### Instant emails

If the event of actions with instant emails is selected:

-

### Automatic emails

If the event of actions with automatic emails is selected:

![blobid8.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073104672/blobid8.png)

First, select this action:

- Opened

- Did not open

- Clicked

- Did not click

Then select the emails from which statistics will be calculated:

-
all

-
any of the selected emails of the group (you should select the group)

 ![blobid9.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073104712/blobid9.png)

Select the emails in the group.

![blobid10.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073104732/blobid10.png)

After you set up all the necessary steps, click the **Save** button. The funnel will be added to the [funnel list](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051185591-Sales-Funnel).

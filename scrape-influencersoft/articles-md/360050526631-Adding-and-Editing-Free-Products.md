/[Affiliates](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245511-Affiliates) / Adding and Editing Free Products

The page allows you to add and edit a free product available for affiliate promotion.

#### The Adding and Editing pages are identical except for their names.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/41416991236500)

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/41416991236628)

Enter the name of a free product and select its category.

The **Description** field contains information for the partners about the product and the conditions of its advertising.

Enter the link of a free product in the** Link** field.

The **Display ****a**** ****D****irect ****L****ink ****f****or ****P****artners** checkbox allows you to set up the direct affiliate link.

***Please note!**** You **must** copy a script code and insert it on the advertising page for the direct link to work.*

The **Add Material Link** field allows you to enter additional information for the affiliates.

Click the **Save** button after filling in the form. The project will be added to the [Promotion Free Product Materials](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050525831-Promo-for-Affiliates-Free-Product-Promotional-Materials-) page.

/[Store ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009133412-Store)/ Payment reminder emails

The **Email** form allows you to add new [payment reminder ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050388252-Payment-Reminders-via-Email-)emails to the series or edit existing ones.
 ![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360072494511/blobid0.png)

To [add a new letter to the series](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050387952-Adding-and-Editing-a-Payment-Reminder-Email-) (change to: add a new email to the series), click the **Add** button. To [edit an existing ](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050387952-Adding-and-Editing-a-Payment-Reminder-Email-)email, click on its name.

/ [Campaigns](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245371-Campaigns) / Analytics of Automatic Email

This page displays detailed statistics for a specific message of the automatic chain.

![blobid0.png](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/360073975812/blobid0.png)

The top part displays statistics on the message for the entire period of time.

Clicking on the **email**** number **in the page header allows you to go to the[ automatic mail editing form](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/articles/360050385272-Add-Edit-and-Copy-Email-Series-).

Clicking on each of the links **Opened****,**** Not opened****, ****Clicked****, ****Not Clicked****, ****Unsubscribed,**** ****Errors****,****  ****and**** Spam** allows you to go to the **Subscribers **form where the list of subscribers who have performed the selected action will be displayed.

Below, you can set the schedule of opening or clicks of the message for a certain period of time.

Use the drop-down list to select the type of graph (open and clicks), specify the degree of detail (hours and days), use the calendar to specify the period, and click the **Show **button. A corresponding graph will be displayed.

/[Advertise](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/en-us/sections/360009245531-Advertise) / PostBack Notifications

PostBack notifications are used when you need to inform a third-party analytics service about the committed conversion in automatic mode.

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/01-50.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/01-50.png)

Enter the *Postback URL *into the relevant field and click on the **Parameters **button.

In the open window, what parameters need to be transferred.

[![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2019-11-05_13-47-53.png)](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2019-11-05_13-47-53.png)

The transmitted parameters will be added to the *Postback URL* address. In the example, all possible parameters are added.

*http: //Postback_Url.html? aff_medium = {$ aff_medium} & aff_source = {$ aff_source} & aff_content = {$ aff_content} & aff_term = {$ aff_term} & aff_campaign = {$ aff_campaign} & date = {$ date} & time = {$ time} & bill = {$ bill} & profit = {$ profit} & product = {$ product}*

After adding all the necessary parameters, you need to click the **Save** button.

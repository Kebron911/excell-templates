The main page of the partner’s cabinet contains the affiliate’s program list you are a member of.

 **![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/01-41.png)**

You can look over the summary statistics for each program.

## Filters

By default, the data for the whole period of time is displayed. The filters allow you to set the period.

![](https://help.justclick.io/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/2018-07-18_13-48-15.png)

You can also select data for a particular affiliate by specifying the name in the appropriate line.

You do not have to fill in all the fields of the filter. After entering the data, click on the **Search** button to apply the filters. To display the entire table, use the **Clear button**.

## Favorites

Clicking on the Star in the first column adds the affiliate program to the Favorites program. The star becomes yellow. The programs from Favorites are displayed at the top of the page.

A second click deletes the program from Favorites. The star becomes gray again.

## Deleting the Affiliate’s Program

To delete the program, click on the cross mark in the last column.

## Transfer to the Selected Affiliate’s Program

Clicking on the name of the selected program allows you to work on it.

The menu will change the way it looks.

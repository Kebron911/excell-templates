## Getting an Affiliate Link

The available affiliate link for advertising is contained in the **Link** field at the top of the page of each tab.

## Adding Advertising Tags

Clicking on the **Add Advertising**** Tag** button opens the **New ****C****ampaign** window that allows you to add UTM-tags for calculating the advertising channels.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/38896919261460)

You can specify the source of traffic here. If you advertise a partner in your mailing list, it can be email on the site – site; if it is contextual advertising, it can be through Google or others.

You do not have to set the advertising campaign, ads, and keywords. But if you want to get more detailed statistics, you should.

Depending on the source, you can determine their values. For a site, it can be a page with an advertising banner or image; for a mailing list, it can be a group of contacts and/or a subject of a letter, and so on.

Once the link is formed, you can copy it into **Your Reference-Counting of Clicks** field and use it in the corresponding advertising campaign.

## Advertising Banners

This page contains graphic advertising materials

Under the image, you can find the HTML code. When you insert it into the page, you add the image with the built-in affiliate link. When you click on the banner, you will be directed to the advertising page.

## Advertising Articles

Advertising texts can be found on this page.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/38896919264532)

Under the text, you can find the HTML code. When you insert it into the page, you add the text with the author’s formatting and with the built-in affiliate link. As you click on the link, you will be directed to the advertising page.

## Subscription Form Generator

The affiliate’s subscription form contains the partner’s tag. When someone subscribes using this form, they are assigned to the particular partner. You can place this form on your website.

![](https://help.influencersoft.com/hc/article_attachments/38896861743252)

The author sets general settings, while design is set up in this tab.

At the end of the tab, the current view of the form is displayed. At the very end, you will find the
HTML code for inserting the form into the page

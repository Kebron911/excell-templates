# strbuyers.tools — SEO Audit

**Audit date:** 2026-05-10
**Auditor:** Claude (Opus 4.7)
**Scope:** Full source-grounded audit of `STRBuyers-Tools/` (Astro 4.x static site, sister to strhost.tools).
**Live URL:** https://strbuyers.tools
**Production sitemap fetched:** yes (https://strbuyers.tools/sitemap-0.xml — 232 URLs)

---

## Executive summary

The site has a competent SEO foundation that mostly inherits from strhost.tools — clean static HTML, canonical URLs and OG tags on every page, JSON-LD `Organization` + `WebApplication` + `FAQPage` + `Place` schemas (the `Place` builder is a strbuyers-only delta over strhost.tools, well-implemented in `seo.ts:147-172`), per-tool FAQ blocks, a 219-page programmatic city corpus, and Satori-built per-page OG images. Site v0.1.0 is live with 232 URLs in the sitemap. Above-average for an indie tool launch.

The biggest, fixable problems:

1. **Sitemap declares 8 blog URLs that 404.** Live `sitemap-0.xml` includes `/blog/`, `/blog/analyzing-airbnb-comps-before-you-buy/`, and 7 sibling post slugs. **No `src/pages/blog/` directory exists** (verified via Glob over `src/pages/**/*.astro`). Every one of those URLs returns 404. Crawlers will discover broken URLs from the sitemap and downrank. (P0)
2. **Footer links four sitewide 404 paths.** `Footer.astro:36, 43-45` link `/get-the-pdf`, `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/disclosures`. The actual lead-magnet page is at `/get-the-buyer-checklist` (verified at `src/pages/get-the-buyer-checklist.astro`). `/disclosures` exists (live, 200). `/privacy` and `/terms` and `/get-the-pdf` do not. Three sitewide 404 vectors from the footer. (P0)
3. **Thin programmatic content on ~200 of 219 city pages.** `cities/[slug].astro:51-53` reads optional MDX from `src/content/cities/`; only a handful of cities have narrative MDX. The rest render the same chrome (ADR/occ/RevPAR + 1-line `notes` + reg badge + saturation tier + CTA). Same duplicate-content trap strhost.tools' lodging-tax pages exhibit. Verified live at `/cities/austin-tx` — body is ~6 sentences total. (P0)
4. **No `/404` page exists.** `src/pages/404.astro` is absent. Hostinger serves a generic 404, missing the chance to retain users who hit a stale URL or one of the broken footer links above. (P1)
5. **No RSS feed, no `<link rel="alternate">`.** `Layout.astro:25-75` has no feed autodiscovery (strhost.tools ships one at `Layout.astro:56`). No `feed.xml` in `public/`. Blog cluster intent + missing feed = lost passive distribution. (P1)
6. **Schema gaps.** No `BreadcrumbList`, no `HowTo`, no `ItemList`. The 219-page `/cities/` index would benefit from `ItemList`; the four step-driven calculators (DSCR, down-pay, year-1, furnishing) need `HowTo`; every nested route (city pages, future blog posts) needs breadcrumbs. `seo.ts` has no `buildBreadcrumb`, `buildHowTo`, or `buildItemList` builders. (P1)
7. **Calculator React islands likely use `client:load`.** Confirmed for `/cities/index.astro:30` (`<CitiesIndex client:load>`) — eager hydration of a 219-row list. Same pattern on calculator pages by inheritance from strhost.tools. (P1)
8. **OG image referenced for every path; no fallback.** `seo.ts:192-208` always returns a URL. If Satori build silently fails for a route (city pages especially), the page ships with `<meta og:image>` pointing to a 404. No `/og/default.png` fallback in `Layout.astro:42`. (P1)
9. **Trailing-slash inconsistency.** Sitemap entries use trailing slashes (`https://strbuyers.tools/about/`) but `seo.ts:178-181` builds canonicals without (`https://strbuyers.tools/about`). Same flaw flagged in strhost.tools audit. (P1)
10. **Empty `sameAs[]` in Organization JSON-LD.** `seo.ts:37-39` has `sameAs: []` with a TODO comment. Should at minimum include `https://thestrledger.com` for entity disambiguation through the parent brand. (P2)

Headline score: **68/100** — competent base, but the sitemap-vs-pages mismatch and 4 sitewide footer 404s drag this below strhost.tools' 74. Fixable to 85+ within a sprint.

## Prioritized remediation roadmap

### Quick wins (≤1 hour each)
1. **Fix `Footer.astro:36`** to point to `/get-the-buyer-checklist` instead of `/get-the-pdf`. — *eliminates the most expensive sitewide 404 (it's a conversion-funnel link)*
2. **Stub `/privacy` and `/terms`** as minimal Astro pages OR remove the links from `Footer.astro:43-45`. — *eliminates 2 sitewide 404s*
3. **Configure sitemap `filter`** in `astro.config.mjs` to exclude `/blog/*` until the directory exists. — *stops feeding 8 broken URLs to Google*
4. **Create `src/pages/404.astro`** with Layout + cross-links to all 7 calculators + `/cities`. — *retains bounced traffic*
5. **Switch `client:load` → `client:visible`** on the calculator islands and `cities/index.astro:30`. — *meaningful LCP/INP win on the 219-row directory*
6. **Decide trailing-slash convention** and fix `seo.ts:178-181` to match sitemap output. — *removes a duplicate-content signal*
7. **Populate `sameAs[]`** in `seo.ts:37-39` with at least `https://thestrledger.com`. — *entity disambiguation*

### Medium (≤1 day each)
8. **Add `buildBreadcrumb`, `buildHowTo`, `buildItemList`** to `seo.ts`. Wire breadcrumb into `cities/[slug].astro`; HowTo into the 4 P0 calculators (DSCR, down-pay, year-1, furnishing); ItemList into `cities/index.astro`.
9. **Build the 8-post blog directory** that the sitemap promises — `src/pages/blog/index.astro` + `src/pages/blog/[slug].astro` + 8 MDX files. Slugs map cleanly to P0 keyword targets per the keyword research doc.
10. **Ship `public/og/default.png` fallback** + emit it as a baseline `<meta og:image>` in `Layout.astro` so build failures don't cascade to broken social shares.
11. **Add RSS feed** (`public/feed.xml` via `@astrojs/rss`) + autodiscovery `<link rel="alternate">` in `Layout.astro` head. Wait until `/blog` exists.

### Strategic (≥1 day each)
12. **Tier-1 city upgrade:** write 800–1500 word MDX for top 50 cities under `src/content/cities/{slug}.mdx`. Hand-cite city-government regulation links. Without this, the 219-page corpus will degrade.
13. **Tier-3 cleanup:** decide policy on the ~69 thinnest cities (noindex vs collapse to state rollups). Either move materially reduces duplicate-content risk.
14. **Affiliate landing pages** at `/str-insurance` and `/airbnb-furnishing` for the high-CPM affiliate keywords flagged P0 in the keyword research doc. Required to convert the calculator traffic.
15. **GSC + GA4 verification audit:** confirm `strbuyers.tools` is verified in Google Search Console and `PUBLIC_GA4_ID` is set in production. Both are prerequisites for measuring the next 90 days.

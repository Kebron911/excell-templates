## How furnishing budgets actually work

The biggest variance buyers underestimate is the gap between buying functional furniture
and buying photography-driving furniture. Most STR success starts with the listing
photos, and the listing photos start with whether the property looks like an
intentional design or a pile of Wayfair clearance.

This calculator buckets cost by tier:

- **Basic** ($1,800/bedroom) — IKEA + Wayfair sales + Target. Functional, photographs
  acceptably, scales fast for portfolio operators.
- **Mid** ($3,500/bedroom) — mix of mid-market with a few statement pieces (Article
  sofa, West Elm bedroom, real art on walls). Most STR investors start here.
- **Luxury** ($7,500/bedroom) — design-forward, custom-feel, high-margin listings
  in destination markets. Worth it when the per-night ADR justifies it.

## Categories included

- **Living room** — sofa, chairs, coffee table, rug, lamps, side tables, art
- **Bedrooms** — bed frame + mattress + headboard, nightstands × 2, lamps × 2, dresser, art
- **Kitchen** — barstools if applicable, kitchen styling, appliances beyond builder-grade,
  cookware, dishes, glassware
- **Dining** — table, chairs, light fixture, art
- **Bathrooms** — towels, bath rugs, art, soaps, accessories
- **Decor & art** — anything that personalizes the space — books, plants, candles
- **Contingency** — 10% (basic / mid) or 15% (luxury) for items you'll forget
  (stretching, drilling, replacements during the first month)

## How to use this calculator

1. Enter **bedroom count** (1–4).
2. Pick a **tier** based on your market's ADR potential.
3. The calculator returns category-level dollars + a contingency line.

## What the calculator doesn't include

- **Appliances** — assume the property comes with refrigerator, stove, dishwasher,
  washer/dryer. Add ~$3–6k if you need to replace.
- **Smart locks, doorbells, cameras** — these run $400–800 total.
- **Window treatments** — typically $1–3k for a 2br depending on quality.
- **Pool/hot-tub setup** — varies wildly; budget separately.
- **Outdoor furniture** — separate $1–4k depending on outdoor footprint.

Add 10–15% to the calculator output to capture the items above. Use the
contingency line for the unexpected drilling/painting/extra-trips-to-Target costs.

## FAQ

### Should I DIY or use a turnkey package?

Turnkey packages (Stage by Hand, Minoan, Furnished Finder) deliver everything
designed and shipped in 2–3 weeks at ~10–15% premium over a DIY project. Worth it
when you're paying mortgage interest while empty. DIY when you have 6+ weeks before
your first booking.

### How much do photos affect ADR?

Listings with professional photos book 25–30% more nights at the same nightly rate.
The difference between a $3,500/bedroom budget and a $1,800/bedroom budget often
shows up in those photos.

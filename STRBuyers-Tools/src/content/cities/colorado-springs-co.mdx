## Should you buy an STR in Colorado Springs in 2026?

Yes, in the right zone. Colorado Springs operates a two-tier STR permit system: owner-occupied permits are broadly available; non-owner-occupied permits are limited to specific zoning districts with density caps. ADR is $184, occupancy 61%, RevPAR $112, market score 74/100. The Air Force Academy, Olympic Training Center, Garden of the Gods, and Pikes Peak generate durable year-round demand at a lower basis than most Front Range alternatives.

## Regulation: where the city stands

City of Colorado Springs STR ordinance:

- **Owner-occupied STR** — permitted in residential zones citywide with permit.
- **Non-owner-occupied STR** — permitted only in specific zones (commercial, mixed-use, and a few residential overlays); subject to density caps.
- **Lodging tax** stack (City 2% LART + state + El Paso County) runs around 9%.
- **Annual permit renewal** with life-safety inspection.

Unincorporated El Paso County (Manitou Springs is a separate municipality with its own rules) operates a different regime — verify jurisdiction. Colorado fire-insurance hardening hits west-side and foothill parcels (Black Forest, Westside near Garden of the Gods).

## Submarkets that matter

- **Old Colorado City / Westside** — walkable, tourism core, premium ADR.
- **Broadmoor area** — near the Broadmoor resort, upscale SFR, golf travel.
- **Downtown** — condo-heavy, walkable, event-driven.
- **Northgate / Black Forest** — near Air Force Academy, family-traveler SFR.
- **Manitou Springs** — separate municipality, separate STR rules, charm-town premium.

## The 3 mistakes buyers make here

1. **Buying non-owner-occupied in a residential zone without verifying eligibility.** Many residential blocks are not non-owner-occupied-eligible.
2. **Confusing Colorado Springs with Manitou Springs.** Manitou is its own city with its own ordinance.
3. **Underwriting Air Force Academy graduation as a steady event.** Two big weekends drive a meaningful chunk of revenue — but they are weekends, not the year.

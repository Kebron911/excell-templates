# Pre-Launch Email Sequence — *The 47-Deduction Operator*

**Status:** Draft v0.1 — 2026-04-28
**Sequence type:** Pre-launch teaser, 4 emails over 3 weeks
**Audience:** Existing list — book buyers (`source:book-buyer`), Tax Bundle owners, hero-magnet subscribers
**Goal:** Build anticipation → land Day-0 sales velocity that drives the launch's social proof

---

## Sequence overview

```
Email 1   ·  T-21 days   ·  Persona pain               ·  No pitch
Email 2   ·  T-14 days   ·  The system in 21 minutes   ·  Soft preview
Email 3   ·  T-7  days   ·  Behind the scenes          ·  Workbook tour
Email 4   ·  T-3  days   ·  Reader-discount preview    ·  Heads-up
                ─────
                Day 0    ·  PUBLIC LAUNCH (separate broadcast — not in this sequence)
```

The cadence works because it answers the three pre-purchase objections in order:

1. *Do I need this?* (Email 1 — pain)
2. *Will it actually work?* (Email 2 — promise / proof)
3. *Who built it and how?* (Email 3 — credibility)
4. *What's it cost and when does it open?* (Email 4 — pricing preview)

By Day 0, the audience has been answered. The launch broadcast is a "doors open" rather than a sale.

---

## Voice rules

Per §6.1 of brand-decisions.md:

- Editorial-finance register
- Specific dollar figures every email
- IRS code citations sparingly (don't lecture)
- Subject lines are clauses, not announcements
- No emoji, no exclamation marks except in dialogue
- Ledger-firm authority, not coach hype
- Sign-off: *— The STR Ledger* with hero tagline below

## Email 1 — *T-21 days*

**Subject:** *Reading the book was the easy part.*
**Preview text:** *Eighty percent of hosts who finish the book don't run the system in the next ninety days. Here's why.*

```

They mark a few chapters. They highlight Amanda's seven-minute tax tab.
They write down the IRS code sections that mattered. They put the book

Eighty percent of those readers will not run the system inside the next

Not because the rules are unclear — the book is clear. Because reading

contemporaneous, and cyclical. The book ends at the back cover. The
system runs every Sunday at 8 AM, every quarter, every year.

not a judgment — it's the gap that exists between knowing what to do

We are about to ship the structure.

In three weeks we are opening enrollment for a course that installs the
seven-minute tax tab on your portfolio with you, not at you. Forty-nine
video lessons. Seventy-five deliverables. The supervised version of
Amanda's quarterly Saturday.

You'll hear from us once a week between now and launch. No pitch this
week. Just the framing.

If you're already running the quarterly Saturday cleanly — congratulations.
You don't need the course. The next email won't be relevant either. Skip
it without guilt.

— The STR Ledger
*Run your rentals before they run you.*
```

## Engagement triggers

Beyond the four scheduled emails, three trigger-based emails fire conditionally:

| Trigger | Email |
|---|---|
| Opened all 4 prelaunch emails, no click | *T-1 day:* "We're opening tomorrow — anything we should answer first?" — soft personal note from Daniel offering to answer one question by reply |
| Clicked STROPERATOR50 mention in Email 4 | *Same day:* "About the discount — here's how to apply it" — preempts the most-asked support question |
| No opens across all 4 prelaunch emails | *Day 0 only:* "If you missed the prelaunch — here's the one-paragraph version." Single-paragraph summary + link. Avoids re-spamming a clearly disengaged segment after launch. |

These triggers are subtle and non-aggressive. The goal is to answer obvious questions before they become support tickets, not to "rescue" disengaged subscribers.

---

## Frequency cap & exclusion rules

- **Already enrolled:** anyone with `course:self-study`, `course:cohort`, or `course:dwy` is excluded from the entire sequence. They get a separate "you're in" sequence.
- **Recently unsubscribed:** standard list rules apply.
- **Frequency cap:** prelaunch sequence respects the global ≤2 emails/week cap; if a separate broadcast is going out the same week, prelaunch shifts a day.
- **Quiet hours:** sends suppressed between 9 PM and 7 AM in the recipient's timezone.

## Post-launch reuse

The sequence is designed to be reusable for future course enrollments (Cohort #2, Course Vol. 02). Variables to swap each launch:

- Course name + price
- Specific date references
- Discount code
- Module count / deliverable count
- The "Amanda" composite reference *(may be replaced with a fresh composite per course)*

Save the sequence as a template in Influencersoft with `{{course_name}}`, `{{price_x}}`, `{{discount_code}}`, `{{launch_date}}` merge tokens.

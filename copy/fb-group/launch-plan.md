# FB Group — Soft Launch Plan

## Setup

- **Name:** `<brand>` Inner Circle (default — can change after A1 brand lock). Alternatives: "STR Systems Society", "Hosts Who Host Like a Business"
- **Type:** Private + Visible (so people can find it in search, but content is members-only — builds scarcity)
- **URL slug:** facebook.com/groups/`<slug>` (use a clean slug without numbers — more memorable)
- **Cover photo:** Canva 820×312px, brand colors, one clear value prop line

## Rules (set in group settings)

1. **Must be an active or aspiring STR host** — introduce yourself in the pinned thread. No agents, vendors, or coaches outside of members.
2. **No self-promotion.** Share experience and questions, not links. First-time violation = warning, second = remove.
3. **Be kind.** We're here to make each other better operators. Debate is welcome; condescension isn't.

> Three rules. Not fifteen. The more rules, the less people read them.

## Entry questions (set in Facebook group settings)

All new members must answer these to be approved:

1. Are you currently hosting on Airbnb / VRBO / Furnished Finder / similar? (Yes / No / Planning to)
2. How many listings do you have or plan to have?
3. What's the #1 thing you want to solve this quarter?

Questions auto-populate a Google Sheet via Zapier/n8n (Task — Phase 2 automation). Gives persona inference for segmentation: "planning to" → Dreamer, "1 listing" → Sam/Newbie, "3+" → Sarah, "manages for others" → Pam.

## Weekly cadence (commit to your calendar)

| Day | Action | Effort | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | Value post — tip, observation, or "mistake I made" | 15 min | Authority |
| Tue | Question post — "What's your biggest..." | 10 min | Engagement |
| Wed | 30-min live — Q&A, template walk-through, or system demo | 45 min | Trust + content pipeline |
| Thu | Member-highlight or case study | 15 min | Social proof loop |
| Fri | "Wins of the Week" thread (members share their wins) | 5 min to open, check back throughout day | Community flywheel |

Wed live = primary content engine. Recording feeds:
- TikTok cut-downs (Phase 2)
- YouTube Shorts (Phase 2)
- Blog post transcript (Phase 2)
- Email broadcast summary

## What goes in Files tab (Facebook native)

Every member sees a small library of free resources. These are **lighter versions of your T1 templates** — not giveaways of full products:

- "One-Page STR Tax Deduction Checklist" (PDF)
- "Cleaner Turnover Lite Checklist" (PDF)
- "STR KPI Starter Dashboard" (tiny Excel, 1 tab)

These drive buyers to your paid library by demonstrating quality at zero cost. Avoid giving away any product that's live on Etsy or the hub.

## Moderation expectations

- Approve new members within 24 hrs (automate via entry-question review once volume grows)
- Remove self-promoters on second offense
- Lock threads that go off-rails
- Never argue with difficult members publicly — DM, then remove if needed
- Monthly: pin a new topic, unpin the previous

## What this group is NOT

- Not a support desk for product questions (send those to `hello@<domain>`)
- Not a free-coaching zone (one-on-one asks → DM → book a paid call)
- Not a place to cross-post from Reddit, Twitter, or other forums
- Not a venue for "Does anyone know a good CPA?" referral spam (legit question once, then follow-up in DMs)

## Migration to paid community (Phase 2)

At 500 members, the free FB Group becomes the **TOFU layer**. The paid community (members-only content, deeper Q&A, monthly template drops) moves inside Influencersoft. FB Group continues as a free on-ramp — it's not deprecated, just demoted.

Transition communication: "The free community stays exactly as-is. For hosts who want more — monthly template drops, priority Q&A, a quarterly live workshop — we're opening the paid Inner Circle at `<domain>/inner-circle`."

## Outline

### H1: Vacation Rental Expense Tracker: The 12-Category System That Replaces Your Shoebox

### Intro (200 words)
- Hook: most STR hosts use a shoebox of receipts + a Costco run memory + their bank statement at tax time. That works for ~$8K-15K of missed deductions per year per property.
- Promise: 12 expense categories that map 1:1 to Schedule E + the workflow to capture every receipt without spending more than 10 min/wk.

### Section 1: The 12 categories (the master taxonomy)
Each subhead H3 with 80-120 words explaining:
1. **Cleaning + laundry** (your cleaner's invoices + linen service)
2. **Maintenance + repairs** (handyman, lawn, pest, HVAC service)
3. **Supplies + consumables** (paper goods, coffee, soap, batteries)
4. **Utilities** (power, gas, water, sewer, trash, internet, streaming)
5. **Insurance** (STR-specific + umbrella)
6. **Property tax + lodging tax remitted**
7. **Mortgage interest** (not principal — separate line)
8. **Depreciation** (annual entry, not cash)
9. **Software stack** (PMS, pricing, smart-lock, bookkeeping)
10. **Professional fees** (CPA, attorney, registered agent)
11. **Travel + auto** (mileage to property, gas, supplies)
12. **Advertising + listing services** (paid promotion, photography, OTA upgrades)

### Section 2: The capture workflow (where 90% of hosts fail)
- Tap-to-photo at point of purchase (Veryfi, Hubdoc, native iOS Notes scan)
- Weekly 10-min review on Sundays
- Monthly close on the 1st: lock the month, reconcile to bank statement
- Quarterly export to your bookkeeping software

### Section 3: 3 traps STR hosts hit
- Trap 1: Welcome gifts coded as meals (50%) instead of supplies (100%)
- Trap 2: Lodging tax collected booked as revenue (it's a liability passthrough)
- Trap 3: Cleaner passthrough not double-booked (income AND expense; netting = under-reporting)

### Section 5: The annual review
- December checkpoint: did you miss a category? Most common misses are software, mileage, and home-office portion.
- January CPA handoff: clean export, 1 sheet per category

## Description

```
Federal + school + local events. Don't undersell July 4 again. Holiday + event pricing calendar for Airbnb / STR hosts. Per-event lift % applied to your base rate, sorted by next-12-events. Excel + Google Sheets.

Most hosts forget July 4 was a high-rate weekend until July 3rd. By then,
the comp set has filled at 1.5× your rate, and you're fully booked at
1.0× — leaving real money on the table.

This workbook is the calendar that prevents it. Federal holidays pre-loaded
with suggested lift %. School breaks by region. Local events you add
(concerts, marathons, festivals, college games, conferences). Surfaces
the next 12 events sorted by date so you verify rates are bumped BEFORE
the booking-window opens.

WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Federal Holidays Pre-Loaded
   · July 4: +50%, Memorial Day +35%, Labor Day +35%
   · Christmas/NYE: +60%, Thanksgiving: +40%
   · MLK Day, Presidents Day, etc.
📋 School Breaks by Region (Northeast, Southeast, Mountain, West, etc.)
📋 Local Events Tab — add concerts, marathons, festivals, college games
📋 Per-Event Lift % — applied to your base rate
📋 Next 12 Events — sorted by date
📋 Status Column — RATE BUMPED / NOT YET / N/A
📋 Calendar View — rolling 12 months overlaid
📋 Pricing CSV Export — Airbnb / VRBO ready
```

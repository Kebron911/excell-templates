## Description

```
Per-jurisdiction lodging tax. What Airbnb remits — and what's still on you. TOT (Transient Occupancy Tax) filing calendar for Airbnb / VRBO hosts. Per-property × jurisdiction × deadline tracking. Excel + Google Sheets.

Most hosts assume "Airbnb handles all my lodging tax." Reality: Airbnb
collects-and-remits in some jurisdictions, not all. Vrbo may handle some
but not others. Direct bookings are always your responsibility. Multi-
jurisdiction rentals (state + city + special district) split: Airbnb
might handle state, but not city.

Miss a city-level filing for 6 months and the city sends a $4,000 penalty
notice + threat of listing suspension. This workbook prevents that. Per-
property × per-jurisdiction TOT register, monthly filing calendar, and the
"Airbnb-remitted vs you-remit" split.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Property Roster — every property + applicable jurisdictions
📋 Tax Rate Register
   · State + county + city + special district per property
   · Tax rate per jurisdiction
   · Filing cadence (monthly / quarterly / annual)
   · Due date (typically 20th of following month)
   · Airbnb-remitted? Vrbo-remitted? Direct-booking responsibility (always you)

📋 Monthly Filing Calendar
   · Per-property × per-jurisdiction × due date
   · Yellow flag at 14 days; red at 7 days
   · Filed status tracker + confirmation # column

📋 Per-Period Computation
   · Gross rents (excluding Airbnb-remitted portion if applicable)
   · Tax rate applied
   · Amount due

📋 Filing History (audit-defense record)
   · 3-7 year lookback retention
   · Confirmation numbers per filing

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· Multi-jurisdiction STR operators (state + city + special district)
· Hosts in markets where Airbnb does NOT collect-and-remit (verify your jurisdiction)
· Direct-booking hosts (always your responsibility)
· Anyone who's gotten a "your TOT filings are in arrears" letter

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY MULTI-JURISDICTION HURTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Airbnb may handle state-level, leaving city-level to you. You assume "Airbnb
handles taxes." 6 months later the city sends a $4,000 fine + notice. The
listing gets suspended. The fix is structural: track every jurisdiction's
status separately so you know what's actually covered.
```

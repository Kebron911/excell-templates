## Description

```
Run your rentals before they run you. Cost-to-launch calculator for first-time Airbnb and STR hosts. All-in budget across acquisition, furnishing, launch, and 90-day reserve. Editable Excel.

Every blog post says something different. Every TikTok says $5,000. Every podcast says $50,000. You have $40,000 saved. You want to launch your first short-term rental. Nobody can tell you what the actual all-in number is — including the line items you'd forget.

This workbook gives you the all-in number in 30 minutes. 120 furnishing line items pre-populated with low / mid / high price ranges. Acquisition costs. Launch costs. A 90-day operating reserve treated as required, not optional. Year-one ROI projection so you know what you're actually buying.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Excel workbook (6 tabs):

- Start — your launch budget headline, 90-day reserve readout, Y1 break-even occupancy, and a calming reassurance line ("Typical 2BR cabin: $32K-$58K mid finish")
- Acquisition — purchase price, down payment, closing costs, inspection, appraisal, title insurance, legal, loan origination. Defaults built in.
- Furnish & Setup — the marquee tab. ~120 line items pre-populated across 8 zones (bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, living, outdoor, tech / smart-home, safety + supplies, linens + amenities). Three finish-quality columns (low / mid / high) with realistic Amazon-tier price ranges. Per-item override so you can pick low for the headboard and high for the mattress.
- Launch Costs — professional photos, permits, business license, LLC formation, EIN registration, insurance binder, PMS setup, dynamic pricing tool, signage, lockbox. ~18 line items.
- 90-Day Reserve — the operating runway. Mortgage P&I × 3, utilities × 3, insurance, cleaning reserve, maintenance reserve, marketing reserve, software subscriptions × 3.
- Total + Y1 ROI — all-in total, cash invested (excluding mortgage principal), Y1 revenue projection (ADR × occupancy × available nights), Y1 net, cash-on-cash %, payback period, break-even occupancy.

"Did you forget?" sidebar on the Furnish & Setup tab: a sticky checklist that flags the items new hosts skip — LLC, insurance binder, smoke detectors per code, CO detectors, fire extinguisher, first aid, exterior security cams, professional photos, pet supplies. Each row links back to its line item.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY THIS PAYS FOR ITSELF
═══════════════════════════════════════════

The 90-day operating reserve. Most first-property failures aren't because the cabin was bad — they're because the host launched without runway, missed a single bad month, and couldn't make mortgage. This workbook makes the reserve a required line, not an afterthought.

Worth it for that alone. The 120 pre-populated line items are the bonus — they save you 200 trips to Amazon and the panic of remembering at month two that you never bought a fire extinguisher.

WHO IT'S FOR

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Purchase → instant download
2. Open Acquisition tab — enter purchase price + down payment %, the rest defaults reasonably (10 minutes)
3. Open Furnish & Setup — set bedroom and bathroom counts. The 120 line items pre-populate. Pick a finish quality per item or accept the default mid (15 minutes)
4. Open Launch Costs and 90-Day Reserve — defaults populate, override what's wrong for your market (5 minutes)
5. Open Total + Y1 ROI — your all-in number is at the top. Below it: cash invested, Y1 net, cash-on-cash, payback. Print it. Hand it to your spouse, your lender, your CPA.

FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Are the price ranges accurate for 2026?
A: Yes. Low / mid / high reflect Amazon, Wayfair, and Costco bulk-buy benchmarks as of the version date stamped on the Methodology footer. A "low" mattress is around $250. A "high" mattress is around $1,200. Both are real market prices, not designer pricing. Re-check before purchasing — prices move.

Q: Does this work for rental arbitrage?
A: Mostly. Skip the Acquisition tab (you're leasing, not buying). The furnishing, launch, and 90-day reserve tabs apply directly. We have a dedicated arbitrage SKU (ACQ-003) coming if you need the lease-deposit + landlord-approval framing.

Q: Why is the 90-day reserve so high?
A: Because most new hosts launch in the slow season, hit one bad month, and can't make mortgage. Three months of operating costs is the conservative floor. Skip it at your own risk. The Start tab will flag you if your cash invested approaches your available cash.

Q: Does this include rehab / construction costs?
A: No. Minor cosmetic only. Major rehab needs the dedicated Rehab Budget SKU.

Q: Is this US-only?
A: Yes. USD only. Permit, LLC, and insurance assumptions are US-based. International v2 is on the roadmap.
```

```
Print-ready pet policy. Fees, breeds, damage rules — covered. Pet policy document builder for pet-friendly Airbnb / STR hosts. 4-section wizard → one-page print/listing-ready document. Includes ADA-compliant service-animal language. Excel + Google Sheets.

"Pet-friendly" without a written policy is a refund magnet. A guest brings an
undeclared pet. The "small dog" turns out to be a 90lb mastiff that ate the
couch. The emotional-support claim shows up at check-in. Without a written
policy, every dispute defaults to the guest's interpretation — and your damage
claim, refund denial, and cleaning fee enforcement all weaken.

This 4-section wizard outputs a one-page document with the language that holds
up: weight/breed/count limits, fees, designated areas, service-animal
exception (ADA-compliant, pre-filled — don't customize without legal review).
Print it for the welcome book. Paste it into Airbnb's house rules. Done.
```

## Description

```
Every review logged. 8 reply templates. 15 min → 3 min. Review response tracker for Airbnb / VRBO hosts. Cuts response time, prevents the "I forgot to reply to that 3-star" failure mode that hurts ranking. Excel + Google Sheets.

Airbnb's search algorithm rewards fast review responses. Most hosts respond
to 5-stars (easy) and ignore 3-stars (awkward). The 3-star ignored becomes
a permanent listing scar that future bookers read and price into their
decision.

This workbook is the system: every review logged, response tracker, 8
copy-paste reply templates organized by tone (5-star, 4-star, 3-star, 1-2-star,
first-time, repeat, long-stay). Cuts response time from 15 min to 3 min.
Prevents the misses.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Review Log — date, property, guest, rating, content, sentiment
📋 Response Status Tracker — Y/N + response date + response time
📋 8 Copy-Paste Reply Templates
   · 5-star (warm thank, no specifics)
   · 5-star (specific praise — repeat the guest's compliment)
   · 4-star (gentle thank + acknowledge minor issue)
   · 3-star (apology + improvement commitment, no excuses)
   · 1-2-star (firm but professional, factual correction if needed)
   · First-time guest 5-star
   · Repeat guest (acknowledge return)
   · Long-stay guest (acknowledge length, mention specific guest behavior)
📋 Lessons Column — capture the "guest mentioned no body wash" → action item
📋 Average Response Time KPI — rolling 30-day, target ≤24h
📋 Response Rate — % of reviews answered, target 100%
📋 Sentiment Trend — % positive / neutral / negative per quarter

═══════════════════════════════════════════
THE 1-2 STAR PLAYBOOK
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Every future booker reads your response. The template handles it: don't
apologize for things that aren't your fault, factually correct anything
misleading, acknowledge anything legitimate, stay professional. Use the
template — don't write from scratch in the heat of the moment.

WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· Airbnb / VRBO hosts who currently respond inconsistently
· Multi-property operators who lose track of which review needs a reply
· Hosts who want Airbnb-search-rank improvement (response signals matter)
· Anyone who's let a 3-star sit without a response
```

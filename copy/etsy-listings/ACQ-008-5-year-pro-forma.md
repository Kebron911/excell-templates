## Description

```
Five years out. Three scenarios. Sign the offer. 5-year property-level pro forma for Airbnb / STR investors with IRR, equity multiple, and exit value across conservative / base / aggressive cases. Excel + Google Sheets.

The 1-year deal analyzer tells you whether the property cash-flows. The 5-year
pro forma tells you whether the property makes you wealthy. They answer different
questions — and serious operators run both before writing any offer.

This is the long-horizon view: revenue growth, expense inflation, debt amortization
year-by-year, depreciation, exit value at year 5. Outputs IRR, equity multiple,
and cumulative cash flow across three scenarios so you know what range of outcomes
the deal can produce — not just a single number.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Property + Acquisition
   · Purchase price, closing costs, rehab, total cash invested
   · Acquisition basis tracking (feeds depreciation)

📋 Year-1 Inputs
   · Stabilized year-1 revenue + expenses (typically pulled from ACQ-001)

📋 Growth Assumptions
   · Annual revenue growth %, expense inflation %, market appreciation %
   · Pre-loaded with reasonable defaults (3-5% revenue, 3% expense)

📋 Financing
   · Loan terms, interest rate, amortization schedule (auto-builds 5-year debt service)

📋 5-Year Pro Forma Matrix
   · Revenue, expenses, debt service, depreciation, cash flow per year
   · Cumulative cash flow + payback timing

📋 Exit Assumptions
   · Year-5 sale price (cap rate × stabilized NOI, OR % appreciation from purchase)
   · Selling costs, capital gains tax estimate, depreciation recapture

📋 Three-Scenario Output (Conservative / Base / Aggressive)
   · IRR per scenario
   · Equity multiple per scenario
   · Total return per scenario

📋 Pro Forma Summary Print
   · Send to your DSCR / commercial lender — IRR + EM is their language
   · Save in deal folder as the basis for measuring actual-vs-projected

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· Serious STR investors writing offers (not "thinking about" buying)
· Buyers using DSCR / commercial loans — your lender will ask for this
· Operators evaluating a 5-year hold + sale vs. cash-flow-only operators
· Anyone choosing between two deals where one cash-flows now and one builds equity faster

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open the workbook AFTER you've run year-1 underwriting (ACQ-001).
2. Plug in growth assumptions. Pre-loaded defaults are reasonable starting points.
3. Set financing (auto-amortizes), exit assumptions.
4. Read the IRR + EM strip. Validate against your investment thresholds.
5. Run the three-scenario sensitivity. If Conservative IRR < 8%, you don't have margin.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
INVESTMENT TARGETS (FOR REFERENCE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· STR target IRR: 15-25% over 5 years
· STR target equity multiple: 2.0-3.5×
· Below 12% IRR is mediocre; above 30% means assumptions are aggressive
· Conservative scenario should still clear your minimum IRR threshold
```

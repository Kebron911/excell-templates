## Description

```
Turnover chaos has a spreadsheet. Permit and license tracker for multi-city Airbnb hosts. Auto-flags expired permits, days-to-renewal countdown, top-15-city permit reference. Excel.

Read this first. Operating an STR with an expired permit is the single most expensive mistake in this business. Nashville fines start at $750 plus 30-day operating suspension. Austin: $2,000. Some cities revoke STR eligibility for 12 months on a second offense. The IRS does not protect you from this. Your insurance does not cover this. Your CPA does not track this. You do.

This workbook is the alarm clock.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Excel workbook (6 tabs):

- Start — at-a-glance status. ALL CLEAR if no permits in red zones, otherwise a red banner naming the top 5 permits by urgency. Operating-while-expired flag if any permit has lapsed. YTD filing $ tracked.
- Permits Register — one row per property × per permit type. STR permit, business license, sales tax, lodging tax, fire inspection, life safety, building permit, other. 200-row capacity (25 properties × 8 permit types).
- Renewal Calendar — sorted view of every permit by days-to-renewal. Three banded sections: EXPIRED (above the line), RENEW NOW (under 30 days, red), UPCOMING (30 to 90 days, gold). Anything past 90 days collapses into a count footer.
- Permit Discovery — typical permit stack for the top 15 STR cities: Nashville, Gatlinburg, Austin, Asheville, Phoenix/Scottsdale, Orlando, Joshua Tree, Hilton Head, Myrtle Beach, San Diego, Denver/Breckenridge, Savannah, Outer Banks, Miami, New Orleans, Big Bear. Fees range, renewal cadence, "as of" stamp, link to city resource page.
- Filing Log — history. Every renewal, amendment, or initial filing with date, $ paid, confirmation number, certificate received Y/N, file path, notes. 500-row capacity.
- Settings — property list, permit type list, as-of stamp for Permit Discovery snapshots.

Days-to-renewal countdown is formula-driven. Conditional formatting: red under 0 (expired), red 0–29, gold 30–89, parchment 90+.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY THIS PAYS FOR ITSELF ON ONE RENEWAL
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Cost of a missed Nashville STR permit renewal: $750 fine + 30-day operating suspension. At a $300 ADR with 60 percent occupancy, the suspension alone is roughly $5,400 of lost revenue. Total exposure: $6,150 on one missed date.

Cost of this workbook: $47.

Multi-city operators forget renewals because there is no central calendar. Knox County's deadline is a tax bill mailed once a year. Asheville's is an email from a clerk. Joshua Tree's is a paper notice. The brain cannot hold 12 to 40 different renewal dates across 4 to 5 jurisdictions. The spreadsheet can.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Purchase, instant download.
2. Open Settings — enter your property names and permit types.
3. Open Permits Register — one row per property × per permit. Issue date, renewal date, cost, status.
4. The Days-to-renewal column auto-calculates. Conditional formatting handles the red/gold/parchment urgency banding.
5. Open Start tab once a quarter. The banner tells you what to do today.
6. After every filing, log it in Filing Log. Updated certificate file path, payment amount, confirmation number.
7. Adding a new market? Open Permit Discovery, find the city, see the typical permit stack before you list.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Does this auto-renew permits for me?
A: No. Permits are filed manually with each jurisdiction. The workbook tells you when. Most cities do not offer auto-renewal anyway.

Q: What if my city is not in the top 15?
A: The Register works for any city — just enter the permit details manually. There is also a "request your city" callout on the Permit Discovery tab. Daniel adds requested cities in v2 expansions.

Q: How often should I open this?
A: Quarterly. The countdown handles the rest. The Start tab tells you what is red.

Q: What about sales tax filings? Lodging tax?
A: Tracked here as permit/registration items (the registration renewal). The actual filings — monthly or quarterly tax remittance — belong in TAX-002 or TAX-005. This is the registration tracker, not the filing engine.

Q: Capacity?
A: 25 properties × 8 permit types = 200 rows. Filing Log holds 500 rows of history. Beyond that, performance degrades — split into two workbooks.
```

## Description

```
✨ 1099-NEC CONTRACTOR TRACKER FOR AIRBNB HOSTS ✨

Pay your cleaner $600 or more in a year? You need to issue them a 1099-NEC.
Pay your handyman $600 or more? Same thing. Landscaper? Same. Plumber for
one emergency repair that cost $750? You guessed it.

Most hosts don't realize this until January, when they scramble to
reconstruct who got paid what — usually missing some contractors entirely
and risking IRS penalties for late or missing 1099s.

This tracker catches every payment, every contractor, all year — so January
is 15 minutes instead of 15 hours.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Contractor roster tab
   • Name, business name (if different), address, TIN/EIN/SSN
   • Contact info (phone, email)
   • Default service category (cleaning, landscaping, handyman, etc.)
   • W-9 filed? (checkbox) — critical for compliance

📋 Payment log tab
   • Date, contractor, amount, service category, property, method (check/Zelle/Venmo)
   • Auto-links to contractor roster
   • Auto-calculates YTD total per contractor

📋 1099 threshold alert tab
   • Flags any contractor hitting $600 YTD
   • Auto-generates the list of 1099s you need to file in January
   • Calculates total 1099-NEC payout so you can budget for filing fees

📋 Year-end summary tab
   • Print-ready report for your CPA or 1099 filing service
   • Matches what Track1099 or Tax1099 asks for during bulk filing

📋 Multi-property support
   • Tag each payment by property → know which property is eating labor costs

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY HOSTS NEED THIS (REAL IRS CONSEQUENCES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════

💸 Late 1099-NEC filing: $60–$310 penalty PER FORM
💸 Intentional disregard: $630+ per form, no cap
💸 Unreported contractor payments: potential audit trigger
💸 Plus: you can't deduct those contractor expenses if you didn't issue 1099s

The single year where this template would have saved me from missing a
1099 alone was worth 10x the price.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

🧰 Hosts who use cleaning services (every host, basically)
🧰 Hosts with handyman / landscaping / pool / HVAC contractors
🧰 Multi-property hosts tracking contractors across locations
🧰 Anyone who got a surprise 1099 reminder from their CPA last January

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT THIS DOESN'T DO
═══════════════════════════════════════════

❌ File the 1099s themselves — use Track1099, Tax1099, or your CPA for
   actual electronic filing with the IRS
❌ Generate W-9 forms — that's the contractor's job (they send you one,
   you store it in your roster)
❌ Calculate sales tax on payments

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1️⃣ Purchase → download
2️⃣ Add your contractors to the roster (5 min one-time)
3️⃣ Log each payment when you make it (30 seconds — or batch weekly)
4️⃣ In January, view the "1099s needed" tab, file via your preferred service
5️⃣ Save a copy with the year stamped, start fresh next year
```

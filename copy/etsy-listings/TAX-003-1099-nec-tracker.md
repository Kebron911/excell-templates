## Description

```
Close your year before April does. 1099-NEC tracker for Airbnb and STR hosts. Auto-flags the $600 IRS threshold per contractor. Editable Excel.

Pay your cleaner $600 or more in a year? She needs a 1099-NEC from you by January 31.
Pay your handyman across 6 visits — did you hit $600 total? Your landscaper? Your
emergency plumber? Most hosts have no idea who crossed the threshold until their CPA
asks in January — and then spend days piecing it together from Venmo screenshots.

This tracker catches every payment, every contractor, all year — so January is
15 minutes instead of 15 hours.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Contractor Directory (50-capacity)
   · Name, business name, EIN/SSN, full address
   · Email, phone, services provided
   · W-9 on File? (Yes/No dropdown) + W-9 received date

📋 Payment Log (2000-capacity)
   · Date, contractor (dropdown), amount, payment method
   · 7 methods: Venmo, Zelle, Check, Cash, ACH, Credit Card, Other
   · Property tag + description + notes

📋 1099 Prep Dashboard (auto-calculated)
   · YTD paid per contractor — updates as you log payments
   · Auto-flags anyone at or above $600 with YES in red
   · W-9 status per contractor pulled live from Contractor tab
   · Status column: ✓ Ready (green) or ⚠ Need W-9 (yellow)
   · Summary: count of 1099s required, ready, pending W-9, total $ volume

📋 Settings Tab
   · Editable IRS threshold (default $600 — IRS may adjust)
   · Auto tax year
   · IRS Pub 1220 penalty schedule for reference ($60–$660 per form)

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY STR HOSTS NEED THIS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

The IRS requires a 1099-NEC for every contractor paid $600+ in a calendar year.
Deadline: January 31. Penalties for missing forms range from $60 to $660 per form
(IRS Publication 1220). You also risk losing the deduction on those payments entirely.

This template auto-flags who crosses $600 in real time — no manual tallying,
no Venmo scroll, no January scramble.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· Airbnb and VRBO hosts with cleaning, handyman, or landscaping contractors
· Multi-property STR hosts tracking payments across properties
· Anyone whose CPA asked for their 1099 list last January and they had nothing
· Hosts who pay via Venmo or Zelle and want a clean paper trail

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Setup (10 min): add your contractors to the Contractors tab
2. Every payment: log one row in the Payment Log (30 seconds)
3. Monthly: open the Dashboard — see who's crossed $600 and whether their W-9 is on file
4. December: chase any missing W-9s before year-end
5. January: hand the Dashboard to your CPA or upload to Track1099 / QuickBooks
```

## Description

```
Co-host commission math. Per-booking + per-month + year-end 1099-NEC. Commission/split calculator for Airbnb / VRBO co-hosts and property managers handling someone else's STR. % of revenue, flat per booking, or hybrid models all supported. Excel + Google Sheets.

You manage your friend's cabin for a 20% revenue share. By month 6 you
realize: the 20% works out to fine pay on busy months, terrible pay on
slow months, and you can't remember if the September distribution
already went out or not. Year-end rolls around and you scramble to
reconstruct what got paid for the 1099-NEC.

This workbook handles all of it. Per-property commission model
(% of revenue, flat per booking, hybrid). Per-month commission owed.
Owner payment tracker. Year-end 1099-NEC summary auto-feeding TAX-003.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Per-Property Commission Agreement
   · % of revenue (15-25% typical)
   · Flat per booking (e.g., $50/booking)
   · Hybrid (e.g., $50 + 10%)

📋 Booking Log → Commission Calc auto-applies the model
📋 Per-Owner Payment Schedule — what's owed to whom
📋 Year-End 1099-NEC Summary
   · Recipient name + EIN/SSN + address (W-9 required)
```

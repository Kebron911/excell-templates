# Etsy SEO Research — Launch SKUs

## Approach

Etsy ranks listings by: (1) keyword match on title/tags/description, (2) conversion rate, (3) recency, (4) shop-level quality, (5) customer satisfaction. For launch, we can only control (1) and (3). Target: front-load keywords, maximize tag coverage, and renew every 4 months.

**Keyword quality signals:**
- Front-load primary keyword in title (first 60 chars)
- Use all 13 tag slots (each ≤20 chars)
- Weave primary keywords into first 160 chars of description (Etsy SEO signal)
- Use buyer-intent long-tails ("airbnb mileage log excel") over generic ("spreadsheet")

**Monitoring:**
- Daily for first 14 days; weekly thereafter
- Alert thresholds: CTR <1% at Day 30 → rewrite thumbnails; CVR <2% at Day 30 → rewrite description

## Renewal cadence

Every 4 months, renew each listing (Etsy auto-renew or manual) for freshness signal. Cost: $0.20 × 5 × 3 renewals/year = $3/year.

## Monitor

Create a simple weekly tracking log (per `ops/post-launch-tracking.md`). Track per listing:
- Views, favorites, sales, CVR, refunds

Alert thresholds (from master spec §7.8):
- CTR <1% at Day 30 → rewrite thumbnails (biggest lever)
- CVR <2% at Day 30 → rewrite description
- Refund rate >5% → investigate SKU

## Post-first-sale actions

- Ask buyer for review on Day 7 (Etsy convention)
- Add a 6th thumbnail showing a 5-star review once you have one
- If any listing gets 0 views at Day 14, review tags — may have a typo or stale category

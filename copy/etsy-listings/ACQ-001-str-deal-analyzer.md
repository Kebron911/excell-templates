═══════════════════════════════════════════
FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Does it pull live AirDNA data?
No. AirDNA is a paid API and not legally re-distributable inside an Excel template. You paste ADR and occupancy from AirDNA (or your own comp work) into the Market tab. The workbook handles every other calculation.

What's the difference between Y1 and stabilized cash flow?
Y1 occupancy for a new STR is typically 65% of stabilized (BiggerPockets community consensus, cited in workbook). Most failed acquisitions assume Y1 = stabilized. The Full version toggles between them on the Verdict tab; the Lite ships stabilized only.

What appreciation rate does IRR assume?
3% annual appreciation, 6% sell costs, 5-year hold. Editable assumption cells on the Verdict tab.

Can I compare three properties side-by-side?
One property per workbook (this is wizard mode). Multi-property comparison is a separate SKU. Most users duplicate the file once per candidate.

How is this different from the BiggerPockets calculator?
BP's free calculator is built for long-term rentals — no avg LOS, no platform commission, no STR-specific occupancy modeling, no AirCover/cleaning fee mechanics. This workbook is built for short-term rentals from the inputs up.

Why is the verdict tied to BiggerPockets benchmarks?
Because they're the most-quoted STR cash-on-cash benchmarks in the BP forums and BP podcast — the same benchmarks your future buyer or lender will reference. Cited on the Verdict tab so you can argue it.

## Description

```
2 vs 3 vs 7 nights, side-by-side. Net revenue, not gross. Minimum-night-stay (MNS) optimizer for Airbnb / STR hosts. Surfaces the truth most hosts miss: 1-night MNS isn't usually the most profitable. Excel + Google Sheets.

Most hosts assume "1-night MNS = max revenue." It isn't. A 2-night minimum
books fewer total nights but spends 50% less on cleaning costs per night. A
3-night minimum books even fewer but maximizes per-stay revenue. A 7-night
minimum loses you most short-trip bookings but wins for cabins / beach houses
where 5-7 nights is the natural booking length anyway.

This workbook computes net revenue per night under each MNS using YOUR
property's actual cleaning cost, ADR, and demand pattern. Output: a
recommendation per property that often surprises operators by $2K-$5K/yr.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Property Inputs — base ADR, current MNS, current occupancy, cleaning cost per turn
📋 Demand Pattern — your market's typical booking length distribution
   · Default: 30% 2-night, 40% 3-4-night, 20% 5-7-night, 10% 8+-night
📋 Three-Way Comparison
   · 2-night MNS — gross revenue, occupancy, turnover cost, NET
   · 3-night MNS — same metrics
   · 7-night MNS — same metrics
📋 Seasonal MNS Tab — different MNS per season (off-peak 2-night, peak 3-7-night)
📋 Recommendation Cell — which MNS optimizes net revenue per property

═══════════════════════════════════════════
THE PATTERN
═══════════════════════════════════════════

For most STRs:
· 2-night MNS — wins on gross revenue but highest turnover cost
· 3-night MNS — wins on NET revenue (sweet spot — most cleaning recovery, modest booking loss)
· 7-night MNS — wins for cabins / beach / ski / week-long-stay markets

The workbook tells you which applies to YOUR specific property.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· Airbnb / VRBO hosts whose MNS hasn't been audited in 12+ months
· Operators in mixed-LOS markets unsure whether to favor short or long stays
· Hosts considering seasonal MNS strategies
· Anyone whose cleaner is overworked relative to revenue

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Plug in ADR, occupancy, cleaning-cost-per-turn (pull from OPS-004 if you have it).
2. Set your market's demand pattern (defaults work for most).
3. Read the Three-Way Comparison. NET revenue per night is the metric.
4. Apply the recommended MNS in Airbnb / VRBO settings.
```

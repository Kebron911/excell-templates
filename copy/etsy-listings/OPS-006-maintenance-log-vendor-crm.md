## Description

```
Every repair, every vendor — one tab away from the next emergency. Maintenance log + vendor CRM for Airbnb / STR hosts. Single source of truth for repairs, recurring services, and the plumber's after-hours number. Excel + Google Sheets.

It's 9pm Saturday. The guest just called: AC is dead. You scramble for the
HVAC tech's number — was it Bob? Or was Bob the guy from last summer? You
text 3 people. The first one available costs 50% more because it's
weekend after-hours.

This workbook ends the scramble. Every vendor categorized (plumber, HVAC,
handyman, electrician, septic, pool/spa, pest), every repair history logged,
recurring services scheduled. The next emergency is one tab away.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Vendor CRM — every vendor with after-hours number, after-hours rate uplift, specialty, performance score
📋 Work Order Log — chronological repair history per property + cost split (parts/labor) + photos
📋 Recurring Services Schedule
   · HVAC filter swap (90 days)
   · Smoke/CO detector battery (12 months)
   · Hot tub drain + clean (90 days heavy / 180 light)
   · Septic pump (24-36 months)
   · Roof + gutter inspection (annual)
   · Pest control (quarterly)
📋 Cost-Per-Property Roll-up — flags rising maintenance burden (renovation budget building)
📋 Vendor Performance Scoring — drop or replace anyone scoring <3
📋 Emergency Contact Quick View — one-tab access during crisis

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· STR hosts running 1+ properties
· Multi-property operators with multiple vendor relationships
· Hosts who've ever scrambled at 9pm to find a plumber's number
· Operators tracking maintenance for tax depreciation (improvements vs repairs)

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY THIS BEATS A CONTACTS APP
═══════════════════════════════════════════

A phone contact gives you the number. This workbook tells you:
· Is this the right vendor for THIS property?
· What did they charge last time?
· What's their performance score?
· Are they currently the highest-scored vendor in this category?
· What's the last work order — and is this a recurring issue?
```

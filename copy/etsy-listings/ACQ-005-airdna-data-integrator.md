## Description

```
Translate AirDNA's projection into your actual deal math. Workbook that ingests AirDNA Rentalizer / Market Score CSV exports and adjusts for your specific property's quality. Excel + Google Sheets.

You ran the AirDNA report. It said the property could do $87,000 in year-1 revenue.
But — is that for a turnkey listing with hot tub and 5-star design, or for the
default cabin shape and size? AirDNA's projection is market-level; your property
is specific. Most STR investors plug AirDNA numbers directly into their underwriting
and end up over-buying because the comp set was nicer than what they actually got.

This workbook closes the gap. AirDNA in → property-quality adjustment → underwriting-
ready revenue forecast out. Plus a comp-set sanity check tab that flags when AirDNA
is suspiciously high vs. real listings on the actual market.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 AirDNA Paste Tab
   · Paste your Rentalizer or Market Score CSV export
   · Or enter the projected RevPAR / ADR / Occupancy manually

📋 Property Quality Adjustment (8 questions)
   · Renovated last 3 years? Hot tub? Unique view? Superhost-likely?
   · Each question contributes to a quality factor (typically -15% to +15%)
   · Honest framework — not a way to inflate your projection

📋 Comp Sanity Check
   · Paste 5 comparable Airbnb / Vrbo listings + their nightly rates
   · Workbook flags if AirDNA's projection deviates >20% from real market

📋 Underwriting Pass-Through
   · Adjusted forecast formatted to drop directly into ACQ-001 STR Deal Analyzer
   · One-click copy of the right cells

📋 Reconciliation Log
   · Every assumption you changed from AirDNA defaults
   · Date-stamped, reasoning per row
   · Useful when revisiting in 6 months — actual vs projected delta

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· STR investors who use AirDNA Rentalizer or Market Score
· Buyers analyzing out-of-state markets they don't know intuitively
· Anyone who's been burned by an AirDNA-only projection
· Hosts triangulating multiple data sources before writing an offer

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Run AirDNA report on the property. Paste CSV into the workbook.
2. Walk the 8 quality questions. Adjustment factor auto-computes.
3. Open Comp Sanity Check. Paste 5 comparable listings + their actual rates.
4. Read the Adjusted Forecast cell. Drop into your underwriting model.
5. Save the Reconciliation Log with your deal folder.
```

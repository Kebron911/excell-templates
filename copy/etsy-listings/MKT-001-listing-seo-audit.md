FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Does this scrape my listing automatically?
A: No. You paste your title, description, amenity count, and metrics manually. We don't scrape Airbnb because their terms prohibit it and the data goes stale fast either way.

Q: Will following the audit guarantee higher rankings?
A: No. Airbnb's algorithm is opaque and weights signals we can't see. The audit covers Airbnb's published ranking factors, host-community consensus, and documented data studies. Following it raises your score on the things we know matter — it does not promise position #1.

Q: Why does my real listing only score 67%?
A: Because real listings score 60-75%. We don't inflate scores to make buyers feel good. The 25-point gap between 67% and 92% is where the actual lift lives.

Q: How often should I re-audit?
A: Every 90 days, or after any material change (new photos, repriced, cancellation policy switch, new amenities added).

Q: Does this work for VRBO?
A: The conversion criteria (photos, copy, amenities) translate directly. The Airbnb-specific algorithm factors (Instant Book, Superhost) don't map cleanly to VRBO. We're shipping a VRBO version in v2.

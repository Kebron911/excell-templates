## Description

```
45-day ID. 180-day close. Don't miss the deadline that costs you the deferral. 1031 exchange tracker for STR / real estate investors — record-keeping support for like-kind exchanges. Excel + Google Sheets.

§1031 like-kind exchanges defer your capital gain when you sell one investment
property and buy another. The math is huge — a $200K gain at 25% federal
recapture + 20% capital gain = $90K of tax deferred per exchange.

But §1031 has hard, unforgiving deadlines. Miss the 45-day identification
window or the 180-day closing window, and the entire deferral disqualifies.
Your $90K of "deferred" tax becomes due in the year of sale.

This workbook is the record-keeping system. Both deadlines tracked with
countdown alerts, identification rules validated, deferred gain computed,
basis carryover handled. Hand the print-ready summary to your CPA + Qualified
Intermediary at each phase.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Relinquished Property — sale, basis, debt, capital gain
📋 Qualified Intermediary — contact + escrow info (REQUIRED by IRS)
📋 45-Day ID Window — countdown alerts (yellow at 14 days, red at 7)
📋 180-Day Exchange Period — countdown alerts
📋 ID Rules Validator — 3-Property / 200% / 95% rules
📋 Replacement Property — closing details, debt assumed, basis carryover
📋 Boot Calculator — cash or debt-relief that's NOT reinvested = taxable
📋 Deferred Gain Summary — what rolls forward
📋 Carryover Basis Calculator — your basis in new property
📋 Form 8824 Print Summary — for your CPA's filing

═══════════════════════════════════════════
THE TWO DEADLINES (HARD, UNFORGIVING)
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· **Day 45:** identify replacement property in writing to QI. Missed = deferral fails.
· **Day 180:** close on replacement property. Missed = deferral fails.

Both clocks start on the closing date of the relinquished property. No exceptions, no extensions.
```

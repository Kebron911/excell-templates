## Description

```
Which owner relationships are worth keeping. Which to fire. Multi-owner consolidated reporting for STR property managers running 10+ properties across multiple owners. Per-owner $/hour profitability + relationship ranking + fire-vs-keep recommendations. Excel + Google Sheets.

You manage 12 properties for 5 owners. One owner is a dream — high
revenue, low communication burden. One is a nightmare — constant
emails, 10% of your time for 3% of your revenue. The other three are
somewhere in between. Without consolidated data, you can't tell which
relationships are worth keeping vs firing.

This workbook is the Pro Pam dashboard: per-owner total managed revenue,
total commission earned, time allocated per owner, profitability per
owner relationship, and a ranked fire-vs-keep recommendation list.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Owner Roster + properties + commission model + relationship start date
📋 Time Allocation Tab (hours per owner per month — admin / comms / issues / distributions)
📋 Per-Owner Revenue + Commission (pulls from PAM-001 if owned)
📋 Per-Owner $/Hour Effective Rate
📋 Owner-Relationship Ranking (sorted by profitability)
📋 Fire-vs-Keep Recommendations (bottom-quartile flagged)
📋 Trend View — is this owner getting more profitable or less?

═══════════════════════════════════════════
COMMON PATTERNS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· **One owner takes 30% of your time but contributes 8% of revenue** → renegotiate or fire
· **One owner generates 40% of revenue with 12% of time** → A-list relationship; protect it
· **Mid-tier owner with declining $/hour** → property is underperforming OR relationship is heating up; investigate before firing

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· STR property managers (10+ properties, 3+ owners)
· Co-hosts running portfolios for absent owners
· PMs evaluating which client relationships to grow vs sunset
· Anyone whose PM business has 80% of profit from 20% of clients (and they don't know which)

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS NOT FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· Solo hosts (you don't have client relationships to evaluate)
· PMs with <3 owners (too small a sample)
· PMs on a SaaS that already produces this report (Hostfully, Lodgify Pro, etc.)

═══════════════════════════════════════════
FILE COMPATIBILITY
═══════════════════════════════════════════

✅ Excel 2016+ · ✅ Excel 365 · ✅ Google Sheets
```

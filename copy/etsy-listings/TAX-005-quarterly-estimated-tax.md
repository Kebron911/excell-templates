## Description

```
Stop guessing what you owe in April, June, September, January. Quarterly estimated tax calculator for Airbnb and STR hosts. Avoid the IRS underpayment penalty. Editable Excel.

You got hit with a $400 underpayment penalty last April. The IRS doesn't care that
your STR income was lumpy — they care that you didn't pay enough as you went. The
fix is the quarterly schedule: April 15, June 15, September 15, January 15. Pay
roughly 25% of your projected annual tax each quarter, and the penalty goes away.

The hard part is "roughly." This workbook does the math.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Annual Income Forecast
   · Project STR income from current YTD + seasonal pattern
   · Account for spouse W-2 withholding (joint filers)
   · Other 1099 / side-gig income line
   · Auto-recommended Safe Harbor (110% of last year's tax)

📋 Quarterly Schedule (4 quarters × line items)
   · Q1 due April 15, Q2 due June 15, Q3 due September 15, Q4 due January 15
   · Income tax + SE tax + state estimate per quarter
   · Already-paid column (deduct what you've sent in)
   · "What to send" cell — bottom-line dollar amount per quarter

📋 SE Tax Worksheet
   · Schedule E filers: typically $0 (rental income is not SE)
   · Schedule C filers: 15.3% × 92.35% × net SE earnings
   · Auto-routes based on your filing classification

📋 Safe-Harbor Check
   · 100% prior-year (under $150K AGI) or 110% (over $150K)
   · 90% current-year as alternative
   · Whichever is lower — that's your safe harbor target

📋 IRS Form 1040-ES Quick-Reference
   · Voucher line numbers, payment URL
   · IRS Direct Pay link (no fees)
   · State estimated-tax pointers

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY STR HOSTS NEED THIS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Airbnb doesn't withhold tax. Vrbo doesn't withhold tax. Stripe doesn't
withhold tax. Direct-booking deposits don't withhold tax. If your only
withholding is a spouse's W-2 (or zero, if you're self-employed full-time),
the IRS expects you to send them quarterly checks.

Miss enough of those, and you owe Form 2210 underpayment-penalty interest
on top of your tax bill in April. Penalty rates float with the federal
short-term rate — currently about 8% annualized. On a $20K shortfall over
the year that's $1,600 you didn't need to pay.

This workbook is the schedule that prevents it.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· Airbnb / VRBO hosts with no W-2 withholding (or insufficient withholding)
· Hosts who got hit with an underpayment penalty last year
· Multi-property operators projecting a profitable year
· Schedule C filers facing 15.3% SE tax on top of income tax
· DIY filers who file estimateds in TurboTax but want to forecast first

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open in January (or any time during the year — the calculator handles partial years).
2. Paste your prior-year tax (from last year's Form 1040 line 24) into Settings.
3. Project current-year income on the Forecast tab.
4. Open the Quarterly Schedule — the workbook tells you what to send Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4.
5. Pay via IRS Direct Pay (free) or your CPA's portal. Mark paid in column F.
6. Re-run after each quarter to recalibrate.
```

## Description

```
Every room. Every item. No surprise spend. Pre-launch furniture + setup budget for new Airbnb / STR properties. 200+ items across every room with cost benchmarks. Excel + Google Sheets.

Most STR launches blow the furniture budget by 50-100%. The buyer underestimates
the long tail — toaster, can opener, ice tray, soap dispenser, doormat, salt
shaker, lightbulbs, batteries, dryer sheets. "I'll just grab one" turns into
$3,000 of forgotten line items by month two.

This workbook is the comprehensive list. 200+ items pre-loaded across bedrooms,
kitchen, baths, living, exterior, technology, supplies. Cost benchmarks per item
(low / typical / premium). Estimate vs. actual tracking. And a 7-year MACRS class
flag per item that feeds your depreciation tracker for tax time.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

📋 Property Snapshot
   · Beds, baths, sq ft, target opening date, total budget

📋 Room-by-Room Walkthrough (the heart)
   · Bedrooms — beds, sheets, pillows, blackout curtains, lamps, dressers, hangers
   · Kitchen — every appliance, every utensil, dish set, glassware, pantry basics
   · Baths — towels, soap dispensers, shower curtains, bath mats, hair dryer
   · Living — sofa, chairs, coffee table, TV, streaming devices, throw blankets
   · Exterior — patio set, grill, outdoor lighting, hot tub setup
   · Technology — smart locks, security cameras, WiFi router, smart thermostats
   · Supplies — first 90 days of consumables (paper goods, cleaning supplies, light bulbs, batteries)

📋 Cost Benchmarks Per Item
   · Low / typical / premium price ranges
   · Markup vs. low-end calculator (avoid over-spending)

📋 7-Year MACRS Flag Per Item
   · Items eligible for §179 immediate expensing — flagged
   · Items requiring 5-yr depreciation — flagged
   · Direct feed into TAX-013 Depreciation Tracker

📋 Master Shopping List Export
   · Group by retailer (Amazon, IKEA, Target, Wayfair) for efficient runs
   · Total estimated spend at top
   · Furnishing density check ($/sqft) against industry benchmark

📋 Month-1 Operating Reserve Line
   · 90-day cleaning + supply runway, separate from furniture budget
   · Prevents "ran out of money in month 2" trap

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHO THIS IS FOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════

· First-time STR investors furnishing their first property
· Multi-property operators standardizing furnishings across the portfolio
· Hosts re-furnishing after a major guest-damage incident
· Anyone whose last furnish-out blew the budget

═══════════════════════════════════════════
HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open the workbook BEFORE you start ordering anything.
2. Walk room-by-room. Mark each item Buy / Already Have / Skip.
3. Open Master Shopping List. Group by retailer. Place orders in batches.
4. Update Actual column as items arrive.
5. At launch: items with the 7-yr MACRS flag flow into TAX-013 Depreciation Tracker.
```

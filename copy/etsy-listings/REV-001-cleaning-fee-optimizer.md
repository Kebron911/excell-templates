## Description

```
Run your rentals before they run you. Cleaning fee optimizer for Airbnb and STR hosts. Compares 3 fee strategies side-by-side and tells you which one nets the most. Editable Excel.

Airbnb's algorithm penalizes listings when the cleaning fee climbs above 30% of a 3-night total. Most hosts find out by watching their search visibility quietly slide while their cleaner asks for a raise. Charge full pass-through and you take a search hit. Eat the cost and your margin disappears. Bundle into the nightly rate and the math gets fuzzy fast — especially across short stays vs long stays, where the answer flips.

This workbook runs the math for you. Three strategies, side-by-side, with the annual dollar delta and the search-rank impact for each. One recommendation. Re-run when your cleaner's rate changes.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Excel workbook (5 tabs):

- Start — recommendation headline, math summary, and the "+$X,XXX/year" verdict in one glance
- Inputs — cleaning cost, current fee, current ADR, length of stay, monthly bookings, platform commission
- Strategy Compare — 3 strategies side-by-side: Full Fee charged / Partial Bundle 50% / Fully Bundled into rate. Per-booking net, annual revenue, delta vs current, search-rank flag, guest-perception note
- LOS Sensitivity (Full version) — same compare across 1-2 night / 3-4 night / 5+ night cohorts, weighted by your booking mix. The answer is different for short stays than long stays — this tab shows it
- Recommendation — 1-page printable action plan with the new fee, new rate, expected lift, and a 60-day review note

Search-rank impact estimate (Full version): cleaning-fee-to-3-night-total ratio scored against Airbnb's documented ranking factor. Under 15% reads "no penalty." Over 30% reads "penalty." The estimate is directional, sourced from Airbnb's published guidance and host-community testing — footnoted accordingly.

FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Is the search-rank estimate guaranteed?
A: No. Airbnb's algorithm is opaque and changes. The estimate is heuristic, sourced from Airbnb's published ranking factors and host-community testing. Treat it as directional, not exact.

Q: My cleaner just raised her rate. Do I re-run this?
A: Yes. Cleaning cost is one of the four input variables. Re-run any time your cleaner's rate, your ADR, or your LOS profile shifts.

Q: Does this work for VRBO or Booking.com?
A: The economics tabs work for any platform — net per booking is net per booking. The search-rank estimate is Airbnb-specific because Airbnb is the only platform that publishes its cleaning-fee ranking factor.

Q: Will my bookings drop if I raise the nightly rate?
A: Possibly, in the short term. The Recommendation tab includes a 60-day review note for exactly this reason. Most hosts who bundle correctly see flat-to-rising booking pace because the search penalty lifts.
```

## Description

```
Run your rentals before they run you. White-label owner statements, management-fee calculator, and 1099-MISC worksheet for STR property managers and co-hosts. Editable Excel.

If you manage short-term rentals for other owners, you already know month-end. Eight properties, six owners, four hours in Excel building custom statements — one owner wants gross, another wants net after your fee, a third wants every maintenance line itemized. And every time your cleaner gets paid for a turnover, you have to remember which owner owes you the reimbursement.

This workbook ends month-end as a project. One booking log, one expense log, one fee calculator. Owner statements generate from a single tab — your logo at the top, no STR Ledger marks anywhere. Print to PDF, send, done.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHAT'S INCLUDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Excel workbook (9 tabs):

- Start — your at-a-glance view: open statements this month, total $ distributable, alerts (negative balances, missing tax IDs, inactive owners)
- Owners Register — 30-owner capacity. Mailing address, tax ID for 1099, payment method, statement detail-level preference (Summary / Standard / Itemized)
- Property→Owner Map — properties × owners with effective dates if ownership changes mid-year
- Booking Log — all bookings, all properties, 5,000-row capacity. TAX-002 column shape (paste-compatible from existing files)
- Expense Log — all expenses, 8,000-row capacity. "Pam-paid?" flag tracks costs you front and recover from owners via withholding, with a withholding-month field so the right cost hits the right statement
- Mgmt Fee Calculator — per-property fee structure. Five models supported: flat, % of revenue, % of net, hybrid (base + performance bonus on occupancy threshold), other
- Owner Statement — the marquee tab. White-label, 1-page printable, your logo at the top, 7-section structure, distribution figure in gold at the bottom
- Year-End 1099-MISC Worksheet — annual owner distributions ≥ $600, tax IDs flagged for filing
- Settings — tax year, owner list, property list, fee model dropdown source, your company info

Capacity: 50 properties, 30 owners, 5,000 booking rows, 8,000 expense rows.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHITE-LABEL — NO STR LEDGER MARKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

The Owner Statement tab carries no STR Ledger branding anywhere. The header has a logo placeholder cell sized for your image insert. Your company name, your email, your address. The footer says "Generated <date>" — not "Generated by STR Ledger."

This is the workbook your owners receive. They see your brand. The infrastructure is ours; the storefront is yours.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
WHY THIS PAYS FOR ITSELF IN ONE MONTH
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Eight properties, six owners, four hours per month in custom Excel work — that is roughly $400 of your time per month at modest hourly rates, or $4,800 a year. This workbook compresses that to thirty minutes of review per owner.

It also closes the leak that costs property managers the most: forgotten withholdings. Every Pam-paid expense (HVAC repair, replacement linens, plumbing call) is logged once with a withholding-month, and the matching owner statement deducts it automatically. No recovery is forgotten. No reimbursement is left on the table.

HOW IT WORKS

═══════════════════════════════════════════
FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Can owners log in or see this directly?
A: No. The workbook is your tool; the printable statement is the deliverable. For owner-portal access, the Pro Manager Bundle includes the deeper integrations.

Q: What about balance forward from prior months?
A: A hidden Statement History sheet stores prior month ending balances per owner. Copy each month's distribution figure to that history sheet to enable next month's pull. Auto-update is on the v2 roadmap.

Q: My fee structure is unusual — base + escalator + performance bonus over a tiered occupancy threshold. Will it handle that?
A: The five fee models cover ~95% of property-management arrangements. For tiered or compound structures, the "Other" model accepts a free-form per-property cell — enter your monthly fee directly each period.

Q: 1099-MISC for owners?
A: The Year-End 1099 Worksheet flags every non-corporate owner whose annual distributions exceed $600. Hand it to your CPA or upload to Track1099.

Q: Multi-currency? Stripe / Plaid integration?
A: Out of scope. USD only. The workbook produces statements; payment execution stays in your existing flow.

Q: Does the white-label cover everything?
A: The Owner Statement tab is fully white-label. Other internal tabs (Booking Log, Expense Log, etc.) carry the STR Ledger watermark — those are your tabs, not your owners' tabs. Owners only see what you print.
```

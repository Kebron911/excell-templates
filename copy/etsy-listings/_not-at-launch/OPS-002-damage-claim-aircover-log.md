═══════════════════════════════════════════
FAQ
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Does this submit the claim for me?
No. Airbnb, VRBO, and Booking.com all require submission through their own portals. This workbook is the evidence packet, the deadline tracker, and the post-mortem record — everything that lives outside their portal.

What if AirCover changes its policy?
The Coverage Reference tab is "as of" stamped and tells you to verify at the platform's help URL before relying on it. We update the workbook periodically; you re-stamp the date when you confirm.

How is this different from just using the Airbnb resolution center?
The resolution center is a submission form. It does not track deadlines across multiple open claims, does not keep evidence in one place, does not produce an annual recovery rate, and does not exist on VRBO or Booking. This is the workspace that wraps all three platforms.

Will it work for direct-booking damage?
Yes. Coverage Reference includes a fourth column for direct-booking claims (security deposit hold + your own insurance). Same evidence discipline.

Can I use it for security deposit disputes (LTR)?
The structure works, but the Coverage Reference is STR-platform-specific. LTR security deposit work has different statutes by state.

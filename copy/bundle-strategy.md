## Pricing principles

1. **Bundles save 25–35% off list.** Less than 25% feels like a pricing trick; more than 35% trains the audience to never buy à la carte.
2. **Each bundle has one persona, not "everything for everyone."** Mixing personas inside a bundle (e.g., new-host + property-manager) inflates the included-but-irrelevant ratio and tanks perceived value.
3. **Anchor pricing at clean numbers** — $97, $147, $197, $297, $397, $797. Avoid $186 / $263 / $471.
4. **Include 1 surprise SKU per bundle** that the target buyer didn't know they needed (the "oh — and that too?" moment).
5. **Prior-catalog SKUs can be added** to thicken bundles, but the core 12 must justify the price on their own.

## Bundles NOT recommended

- **"Tax Season Bundle"** of just the new 12 — only TAX-011 and OPS-002 fit, which is too thin. A real tax bundle should pull TAX-002 + TAX-004 + TAX-011 from the existing catalog. That's a separate decision.
- **"Everything Bundle"** at $497 — bundles the full 12 + creates buyer-reluctance dynamics ("am I really going to use all of these?"). The Portfolio Bundle at $397 already covers the 10 SKUs that actually compose into one buyer's stack.

## Decisions resolved at launch (2026-05-02)

1. ✅ **Pro Manager Bundle:** $497 at launch. Rises to $797 once Cleaner CRM + Co-Host Commission Splitter + Maintenance Workflow ship.
2. ✅ **Launch timing:** all 5 bundles ship together with the 12 SKUs. Single launch beats staggered for email/Pinterest momentum and avoids "wait for the better deal" buyer hesitation.
3. ✅ **Existing-catalog enrichment:** Portfolio Bundle adds TAX-002 (PL Single Property), TAX-004 (Schedule E), GST-001 (Welcome Book), OPS-001 (Turnover Checklist) at no price change. Raises perceived value from $470 to ~$610 à la carte against the same $397 bundle price (35% savings). Keeps the "10 new workbooks" headline accurate while making the bundle harder to walk past.
4. ✅ **Etsy bundles:** own-site only at launch. Etsy's single-file delivery model fights bundle UX. Revisit at 90 days if own-site bundle conversion data shows ceiling we want to break through.
5. ✅ **Refund window:** 14 days for individual SKUs, **30 days for bundles**. Lower hurdle for higher-ticket purchase. Refund rate is the metric that decides whether to revert.

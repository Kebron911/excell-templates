## The 30-second pitch

How much is your STR portfolio actually worth? Most hosts know what they paid. Few know what it's worth today. This workbook computes current market value using 3 methods — cap rate × NOI, comp sales, GRM — outputs a mid-point estimate, a conservative estimate (use for refi LTV math), and a buyer's-perspective price for sale conversations.

## When to run

- Annually as part of year-end review
- Before any refinance (know your cash-out potential)
- Before any sale conversation
- When the market shifts

## Common surprises

- Debt-to-value <50% → significant equity locked. Refi to recycle capital.
- Debt-to-value >80% → over-leveraged, vulnerable to softening
- One property valued 30%+ above the rest → "winner" worth concentrating on, OR ready to 1031

## FAQ

**Should I buy this if I only have one property?**
No — buy TAX-002 (Single-Property P&L Tracker) instead. That has a depreciation tab built in for the 27.5-year SL building plus simple improvements. Come back to TAX-013 when you have multiple properties or year-by-year asset complexity.

**Will this replace my CPA's software?**
For most CPAs, no — they'll input from their side. But it'll cut their time in half because the asset list, the year-by-year schedule, and the current-year totals are all already done. They verify, sign off, file.

**MACRS — what classes apply to STR assets?**
- 5-year: appliances, carpet, decorative fixtures, furniture (in residential rental)
- 7-year: office furniture (in active business)
- 15-year: land improvements (driveways, fences, landscaping, exterior lighting, decks)
- 27.5-year: residential rental building (the default)
- 39-year: commercial property (only if you've classified the rental as commercial — rare)

**What about the half-year, mid-quarter, mid-month conventions?**
The workbook auto-applies them based on in-service date and aggregate-asset rules (mid-quarter applies if >40% of property is placed in service in Q4). You don't need to know the conventions to use the workbook.

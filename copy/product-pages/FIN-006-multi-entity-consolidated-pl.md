## The 30-second pitch

You hold each property in its own LLC for liability isolation. Smart for asset protection. Hard for portfolio reporting: 5 LLCs = 5 separate P&Ls + 5 K-1s, and your overall income picture lives in your head. This workbook aggregates per-LLC statements into a holding-company-level consolidated view, with intercompany eliminations and member-distribution tracking.

## What's inside

- Entity Roster (every LLC + state, EIN, members, ownership %, properties)
- Per-LLC P&L tabs (paste from each entity's bookkeeping)
- Intercompany Transactions (loans, mgmt fees, shared expenses)
- Member-Distribution Schedule
- Consolidated P&L (after eliminations)
- Per-LLC Summary
- Member-View P&L (per-member share across all entities)

## How it differs from TAX-011

- **TAX-011:** multiple properties, single entity (one Schedule E)
- **FIN-006:** multiple LLCs (multiple Schedule Es / Form 1065s)

If you don't know which: ask CPA whether you file one or multiple Schedule Es.

## Who this is for / NOT for

| For | Not for |
|---|---|

## The 30-second pitch

"Pet-friendly" without a written policy is a refund magnet. The "small dog" turns into a 90lb mastiff. The emotional-support claim shows up at check-in. Without a written policy, every dispute defaults to the guest's interpretation. This 4-section wizard outputs a one-page document with the language that holds up: weight/breed/count limits, fees, damage rules, designated areas, and ADA-compliant service-animal exception (pre-filled — don't customize without legal review).

## Why this protects you

A written policy makes:
- Damage claims defensible (guest violated written rule)
- Refund disputes winnable (your policy was published before booking)
- Cleaning fees enforceable (additional pet cleaning fee was agreed)

Without it: every dispute defaults to "the host didn't make this clear."

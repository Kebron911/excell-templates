## FAQ

**Will following the audit guarantee higher rankings?**
No. Airbnb's algorithm is opaque and weights signals we can't see. The audit covers Airbnb's published ranking factors, host-community consensus, and documented data studies. Following it raises your score on the things we know matter — it does not promise position #1.

**Why does my real listing only score 67%?**
Because real listings score 60-75%. We don't inflate scores to make buyers feel good. The 25-point gap between 67% and 92% is where the actual lift lives.

**Does this scrape my listing automatically?**
No. You paste manually. Airbnb's terms prohibit scraping and the data goes stale fast either way.

**How often should I re-audit?**
Every 90 days, or after any material change — new photos, repriced, cancellation policy switch, new amenities added. The Re-audit Tracker stores your last 5 scores so you can see the trajectory.

**Does this work for VRBO?**
The conversion criteria (photos, copy, amenities) translate directly. The Airbnb-specific algorithm factors (Instant Book, Superhost) don't map cleanly. A VRBO version is on the v2 roadmap.

**What's the difference between Lite and Full?**
Lite ($27 on Etsy): 25 most-impactful criteria. Audit + fix list + re-audit tracker. Full ($47 own-site): all 40 criteria, the Top-5 Comp Compare tab, and the public Methodology page (every criterion sourced and dated).

## FAQ

**Does break-even include my mortgage principal?**
No. The calculation covers operating costs plus mortgage interest. Principal paydown is excluded — and the Start tab tells you so in plain English. True cash break-even is higher. We do not pretend otherwise. That is the integrity differentiator versus every other break-even template on Etsy.

**Does it include my owner labor?**
No. Same footnote. If you charge yourself $25/hour to clean turn supplies and project-manage the property, your cash break-even is meaningfully higher. We tell you that on the Start tab.

**Can I use it for multiple properties?**
One property per workbook. For portfolio-level break-even, use the multi-property P&L tracker. This file is built to be re-run quickly when one property's costs change.

**How does this connect to my P&L?**
The Calculator tab includes a "Pull from your TAX-002 P&L" callout that names which cells in the Single-Property P&L Tracker feed which inputs here. We do not live-link — file paths break — but the cell map is on the page.

**What if I don't know my exact platform commission?**
Default is 15% (Airbnb's standard host fee). VRBO is similar. Direct bookings: 0%. The cell is editable.

**Why a 5% maintenance reserve?**
Industry standard for STRs is 5% of revenue for maintenance and another 3% for capex (roof, HVAC, big-ticket). Default is 5%; the cell is editable. If you reserve nothing you are deferring the cost, not eliminating it.

**Why is the answer the same price on Etsy and on your site?**
Because $17 is a volume tripwire and we designed it to convert from both surfaces with the same friction. No coupon games.

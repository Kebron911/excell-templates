## The 30-second pitch

Storm damages your roof. Or a guest causes $8K of damage that AirCover denies. Or a pipe bursts. The claim drags on 90+ days, the adjuster low-balls, by month 4 you've forgotten what was approved when. This workbook tracks insurance-claim lifecycles cleanly. FNOL → settlement. Recovery $ vs deductible. Per-claim photos + adjuster notes + correspondence. Lessons-learned for preventive cadence updates.

## What's inside

- Property Roster + each property's policy info
- Per-Claim Lifecycle Log (FNOL → Investigating → Approved → Denied → Settled)
- Days-to-Settlement KPI (>60 days = pressure carrier)
- Recovery Rate (settled $ ÷ claimed $; <60% = evidence weak)
- Per-Property Claim History (2+ claims in 3 years = "hot")
- Lessons-Learned Column

## Disclaimer

Filing claims under threshold (e.g., $3K damage with $2.5K deductible) often costs more in future premium hikes than the settlement returns. The workbook helps you decide WHETHER to file — not just how to track.

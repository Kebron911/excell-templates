## The 30-second pitch

Every January you tell yourself last year was "fine." But — was revenue actually up? Did expenses creep? Was occupancy flat-but-rate-was-up, or did rate drop? Without a side-by-side, gut feel doesn't survive the spreadsheet. This workbook puts the P&Ls next to each other and surfaces the top-3 winners + losers per property, so January planning starts from facts not vibes.

## Common patterns the workbook surfaces

- **Revenue ↑ + occupancy ↓** → pricing power gained, push harder
- **Revenue flat + occupancy ↑** → comp set raising rates faster than you (REV-003)
- **Cleaning ↑ 20%+** → renegotiate or replace cleaner (OPS-004)
- **Insurance ↑** → shop carriers
- **NOI ↓ but revenue ↑** → expense creep — find it on the Top-3 Unfavorable

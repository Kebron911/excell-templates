## The 30-second pitch

Airbnb's search algorithm rewards fast review responses. Most hosts respond to 5-stars (easy) and ignore 3-stars (awkward). The 3-star ignored becomes a permanent listing scar future bookers read and price into their decision. This workbook is the system: every review logged, response status tracked, 8 copy-paste reply templates per tone. Cuts response time from 15 min to 3.

## What's inside

- Review Log (date, property, guest, rating, content, sentiment)
- Response Status Tracker (Y/N + response date + response time)
- 8 Reply Templates (5-star warm / 5-star specific / 4-star / 3-star / 1-2-star / first-time / repeat / long-stay)
- Lessons Column (capture "guest mentioned no body wash" → action item)
- KPIs: avg response time (target ≤24h), response rate (target 100%)
- Sentiment Trend (% positive / neutral / negative per quarter)

## The 1-2 star playbook

Every future booker reads your response. Don't apologize for things that aren't your fault, factually correct anything misleading, acknowledge anything legitimate, stay professional. Use the template — don't write from scratch in the heat of the moment.

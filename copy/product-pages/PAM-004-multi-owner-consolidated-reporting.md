## The 30-second pitch

You manage 12 properties for 5 owners. One is a dream — high revenue, low communication burden. One is a nightmare — constant emails, 10% of your time for 3% of revenue. The other three are somewhere between. Without consolidated data, you can't tell which relationships to keep vs fire. This is the Pro Pam dashboard: per-owner total managed revenue, total commission, time allocated, profitability, and a ranked fire-vs-keep list.

## What's inside

- Owner Roster (properties + commission model + relationship start)
- Time Allocation (hours per owner per month — admin / comms / issues / distributions)
- Per-Owner Revenue + Commission (pulls from PAM-001)
- Per-Owner $/Hour Effective Rate
- Owner-Relationship Ranking
- Fire-vs-Keep Recommendations (bottom-quartile flagged)
- Trend View (more profitable or less over time?)

## Common patterns

- One owner takes 30% of your time for 8% of revenue → renegotiate or fire
- One owner generates 40% of revenue with 12% of time → A-list; protect
- Mid-tier with declining $/hour → property is underperforming OR relationship heating up; investigate

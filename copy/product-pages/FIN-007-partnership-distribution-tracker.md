## Important — disclaimer

Partnership tax is complex. Special allocations (not strictly proportional), guaranteed payments, §704(b) rules, capital-account maintenance — all require CPA judgment. This workbook handles the standard case (proportional allocation, no special allocations). For anything more complex, your CPA may need to override.

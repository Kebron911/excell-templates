## The 30-second pitch

Most hosts list 4 generic rules: "no smoking, no pets, no parties, quiet hours after 10pm." When a guest plays music until 2am, "be respectful" doesn't help your damage claim or your neighbors. This wizard walks 6 sections (quiet hours / smoking / pets / guests / property care / departure) and outputs a 25-40 rule document with the specifics that actually work — dB thresholds, time windows, fees, fee triggers.

## Why specific rules win

A vague rule ("be respectful") doesn't help when a guest plays music at 2am. A specific rule ("no amplified music outdoors after 9pm; sound monitor at the property") gives your cleaner, your neighbors, and Airbnb's resolution team a clear reference. Damage claims succeed when the rule was specific and the guest violated it explicitly.

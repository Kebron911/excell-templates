## The 30-second pitch

DSCR loans don't care about your W-2. They care if the property covers its own debt. Most lenders want ≥1.20 to qualify and rate-shop you down at 1.50+. Most STR investors don't know their per-property DSCR off the top of their head — let alone portfolio-wide. This workbook computes it, color-codes it, and tells you which properties are refi-ready.

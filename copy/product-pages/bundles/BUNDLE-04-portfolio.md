## Why 14 workbooks

A serious multi-property operator runs the same set of decisions every quarter:

- **What's the portfolio actually doing?** TAX-011 + FIN-001 = consolidated income + RevPAR
- **Should we buy the next property?** ACQ-001 + ACQ-002 + STR-001 + FIN-002
- **Where's revenue leaking?** REV-001 + MKT-001
- **Where's risk leaking?** OPS-002 + OPS-003
- **Year-end tax filing?** TAX-002 + TAX-004
- **Day-to-day ops?** GST-001 + OPS-001

Without all of them, every quarter you're patching with one-off spreadsheets, losing data between them, and doing reconciliation work that shouldn't exist.

## The 30-second pitch

§1031 defers your capital gain when you sell one investment property and buy another. A $200K gain at 25% federal recapture + 20% capital gain = $90K of tax deferred per exchange. But §1031 has hard, unforgiving deadlines — miss the 45-day ID or 180-day close and the entire deferral disqualifies. This workbook is the record-keeping system that prevents that loss.

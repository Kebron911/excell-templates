Your homeowner's policy excludes STR rental — but you're still paying premium to it. Your umbrella doesn't actually cover commercial liability above AirCover's gap. ## The 8 risk categories

1. Dwelling/structure damage — base policy
2. Personal liability — base policy
3. Loss of business income during repairs (often missing)
4. Short-term rental endorsement (many homeowners' policies EXCLUDE)
5. Commercial liability above AirCover's gap
6. Cyber/data breach (for direct-booking hosts)
7. Vehicle commercial use (personal auto often excludes)
8. Workers comp (if you have employees, even part-time)

## Common gaps the workbook catches

- "STR endorsement not on dwelling policy" → claim denied for guest damage
- "Umbrella excludes STR rental" → fine print most hosts don't read
- "Vehicle commercial use not covered" → denial for accident on supply run
- "Business interruption coverage missing" → roof damage = 6 weeks closed = $48K of lost revenue with NO recovery

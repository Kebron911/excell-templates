## The 30-second pitch

You pay your cleaner $185. You charge $185. Airbnb takes a cut on both the rate and the cleaning fee. Your cleaning-fee-to-3-night ratio sits north of 30% and Airbnb's algorithm — by their own published guidance — quietly pushes you down the rankings.

You've considered bundling some of the fee into the nightly rate. The math gets fuzzy fast. It also flips depending on whether you're getting 1-night stays or 5-night stays.

This workbook runs the math for you. Three strategies — Full Fee, 50% Bundle, Fully Bundled — side-by-side. Per-booking net. Annual revenue delta. Airbnb search-rank flag for each. And — in the Full version — a length-of-stay sensitivity tab that shows you why short stays favor bundling and long stays don't.

One recommendation. One number. Re-run when your cleaner's rate changes.

## FAQ

**Is the search-rank estimate guaranteed?**
No. Airbnb's algorithm is opaque and changes. The estimate is heuristic, sourced from Airbnb's published ranking factors and host-community testing. Treat it as directional, not exact. Footer disclaimer is in the workbook.

**My cleaner just raised her rate. Re-run?**
Yes. Cleaning cost is one of six input variables. Re-run any time the cleaner's rate, your ADR, or your LOS profile shifts.

**Will my bookings drop if I raise the nightly rate?**
Possibly, in the short term. The Recommendation tab includes a 60-day review note for exactly this reason. Most hosts who bundle correctly see flat-to-rising booking pace because the search penalty lifts and the listing surfaces in front of more eyes.

**Does this work for VRBO or Booking.com?**
The economics work for any platform — net per booking is net per booking. The search-rank estimate is Airbnb-specific because Airbnb is the only platform that publishes its cleaning-fee ranking signal.

**What's the difference between Lite and Full?**
Lite ($27 on Etsy): Inputs + 3-strategy compare + recommendation, single-LOS only. Full ($37 own-site): adds LOS sensitivity (1-2 / 3-4 / 5+ night cohorts) plus the Airbnb search-rank impact estimate.

**Can I run it across multiple properties?**
Yes — once per property. The workbook is a wizard, not a portfolio dashboard. For multi-property strategy, see the Pricing Bundle below.

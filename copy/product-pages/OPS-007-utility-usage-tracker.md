## Common anomaly patterns

- **Water 2x normal** = leak (toilet, dishwasher, irrigation). Call plumber today.
- **Electric 1.5x normal in shoulder season** = HVAC running with windows open OR pool heater stuck on
- **Internet creeping up annually** = ISP rate hikes; shop alternatives every 24 months
- **Propane spike** = tank gauge wrong OR theft (yes, this happens)

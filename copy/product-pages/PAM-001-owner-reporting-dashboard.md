## FAQ

**Q: Is the white-label real?**
Yes. The Owner Statement tab has zero STR Ledger marks. Logo placeholder cell at the top sized for your image insert. Footer reads "Generated <date>" — never "Generated by STR Ledger." Other internal tabs (Booking Log, Expense Log, etc.) carry the STR Ledger watermark, but those tabs never reach your owners. They see only what you print.

**Q: What about balance forward from prior months?**
A hidden Statement History sheet stores prior-month ending balances per owner. You copy each month's distribution to that sheet at month-end, which enables next month's pull. Auto-update is on the v2 roadmap. The manual step is one row per owner per month — about ninety seconds total.

**Q: My fee structure is unusual.**
The five built-in models — flat / % revenue / % net / hybrid / other — cover ~95% of US property-management agreements we have seen. Tiered or compound structures use the "Other" model with manual per-period entry. Hybrid is the workhorse: base fee plus performance bonus when occupancy clears a threshold.

**Q: Can owners log in?**
No. This is a manager's tool that produces a printable deliverable. For owner-portal access, the Pro Manager Bundle includes the deeper integrations.

**Q: 1099-MISC handling?**
The Year-End 1099 Worksheet flags every non-corporate owner whose annual distributions exceed the IRS $600 threshold. Hand the worksheet to your CPA or upload to Track1099 / QuickBooks. Tax IDs surface from Owners Register.

**Q: Multi-currency?**
USD only.

**Q: How does this differ from PMS software (Hostfully, Guesty, Hospitable)?**
PMS software handles bookings, messaging, channel sync. None of them produce a clean white-label owner statement with the math an STR-aware CPA expects. This workbook complements your PMS — paste bookings in, get statements out.

**Q: Does it work in Google Sheets?**
Yes. Formulas remap on import. The Owner Statement print area is tested in both Excel and Sheets.

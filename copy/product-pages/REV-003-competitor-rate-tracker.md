## What to do with the data

- **15%+ below comp avg for 3 weeks** → raise base rate or peak multiplier
- **Comps raised for July 4 / Memorial Day** → match within 24h
- **Comps drop a weekend** → hold, don't follow down (booking-rank suffers more than rate)
- **Comps raised but you're 20%+ above** → don't follow; you're price-leading

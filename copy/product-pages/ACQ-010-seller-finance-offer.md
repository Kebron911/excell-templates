## The 30-second pitch

Seller financing sounds great until you do the math. 7% from seller when bank is 7.5% — sounds like a win. But seller wants 25% amortization on a 5-year balloon, and you can't refi if rates haven't dropped. That "win" turns into a forced sale. This workbook runs the real math: monthly payment, total cost over loan life, cash flow vs conventional, balloon-risk year, plus a draft term sheet.

## The 30-second pitch

Airbnb takes ~15% of every gross booking. On a $2,500 stay, that's $375 you gave to the platform. If you can rebook the same guest direct, you keep the $375. Multiply by 30 repeat guests/year = $10K+ in recovered channel fees, every year. This workbook is the CRM that makes direct rebooking systematic.

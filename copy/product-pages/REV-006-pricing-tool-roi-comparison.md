## The 30-second pitch

PriceLabs costs $20/mo per listing. Wheelhouse $15-25. Beyond similar. On 4 listings that's $80-100/month or $1,000+/year. The tools all claim "average customer sees 15-30% revenue lift." That's marketing math from people selling the tool — not your specific market. This workbook is the A/B test that answers honestly.

## When the tool wins / loses

| Tool wins | Tool loses |
|---|---|
| High-volatility markets (NYC, LA, weather-driven beach) | Stable seasonal markets (mountain cabins) |
| 5+ properties (manual not tractable) | ≤2 properties (manual via REV-002 + REV-003 wins) |
| Markets with frequent large events the tool catches | Disciplined REV-003 weekly comp tracking |

## Sample-size caveats

A 60-day test on a single property has noisy data. The workbook flags low-confidence results. For definitive answer: run multiple properties or year-on-year comparisons.

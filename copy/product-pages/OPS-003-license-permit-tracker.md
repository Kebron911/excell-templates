## The 30-second pitch

Operating an STR with an expired permit is the single most expensive mistake in this business. Nashville: $750 fine plus 30-day operating suspension. Austin: $2,000. Some cities revoke STR eligibility for a year on a second offense.

Your CPA does not track this. Your insurance does not cover this. The cleaner does not know. You do. Or you do not, and a Knox County renewal lapses and you find out 60 days later from a paper notice.

The reason multi-city operators forget renewals is that there is no central calendar. Knox County mails a tax bill once a year. Asheville sends an email from a clerk. Joshua Tree mails paper. The brain cannot hold 12 to 40 different renewal dates across 4 to 5 jurisdictions. The spreadsheet can.

Open it once a quarter. The Start tab tells you what is red. Done.

## What's inside (the workbook)

- **Start** — at-a-glance status. ALL CLEAR or red banner. Top 5 by urgency. Operating-while-expired flag.
- **Permits Register** — one row per property × per permit type. STR permit, business license, sales tax, lodging tax, fire, life safety, building, other. 200-row capacity.
- **Renewal Calendar** — sorted by days-to-renewal. EXPIRED · RENEW NOW (<30) · UPCOMING (30–90).
- **Permit Discovery** — typical permit stack for the top 15 STR cities. Fees, cadence, "as of" stamp, city resource link.
- **Filing Log** — every renewal with date, $ paid, confirmation number, certificate path. 500-row capacity.
- **Settings** — property list, permit types, as-of stamps.

## FAQ

**Does this auto-renew permits?**
No. Permits are filed manually with each jurisdiction. The workbook tells you when. Most cities do not offer auto-renewal anyway.

**What if my city is not in the top 15?**
The Register works for any city. Enter permits manually. There is also a "request your city" callout — Daniel adds requested cities in v2 expansions.

**How often do I open this?**
Quarterly. The countdown handles the rest.

**Sales tax filings? Lodging tax remittance?**
Tracked here as registration items. The monthly or quarterly tax filings belong in TAX-002 or TAX-005. This is the registration tracker, not the filing engine.

**Capacity?**
25 properties × 8 permit types = 200 rows. Filing Log holds 500 rows of history. Beyond that, split into two workbooks.

**Why no Etsy Lite version?**
A stripped permit tracker is dangerous. Expired permits = fines. Either you have the full thing or you do not need it. We will not sell a half-version.

## FAQ

**Does this submit the claim for me?**
No. Airbnb, VRBO, and Booking.com require submission through their own portals. This workbook is the evidence packet, the deadline tracker, and the post-mortem record — everything outside the portal.

**What if AirCover changes its policy?**
The Coverage Reference tab is "as of" stamped and points you to the platform help URL to verify. We update the workbook periodically. You re-stamp the date when you confirm.

**Why isn't this on Etsy?**
Because a stripped Etsy Lite version of a claim packet is worse than no template. The deadline countdown and message scripts are not optional features — they are the product. We sell this on the own-site only.

**How is this different from just using the Airbnb resolution center?**
The resolution center is a submission form. It does not track deadlines across multiple open claims, does not keep evidence in one place, does not produce an annual recovery rate, and does not exist on VRBO or Booking. This is the workspace that wraps all three platforms.

**Does it work for direct-booking damage?**
Yes. Coverage Reference includes a fourth column for direct-booking claims (security deposit hold + your own insurance). Same evidence discipline.

**Is the 14-day window the same on every platform?**
No — it's Airbnb's standard. VRBO and Booking.com have their own windows. The Coverage Reference tab lists each one. The Settings tab lets you override the default per-claim if you've confirmed a different window with the platform.

**Can I keep the photos in the workbook?**
We track whether a photo exists, not the photo itself. Embedding photos bloats the file and makes it slow to open. Keep photos in a per-claim folder; the Evidence checklist references the folder path.

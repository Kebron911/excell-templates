Most launches die in month two because the host bought the cabin and the couches and forgot the runway. The 90-day reserve line on this workbook is the single most expensive thing you'll skip — and the reason this workbook exists.

## FAQ

**Are the price ranges accurate for 2026?**
Yes. Low / mid / high reflect Amazon, Wayfair, and Costco bulk-buy benchmarks as of the version date stamped in the workbook. A "low" mattress is around $250. A "high" mattress is around $1,200. Both are real prices. Re-check before purchasing — prices move.

**Does this work for rental arbitrage?**
Mostly. Skip the Acquisition tab (you're leasing, not buying). Furnish, Launch, and 90-day Reserve apply directly. A dedicated arbitrage SKU (ACQ-003) is on the roadmap with the lease-deposit and landlord-approval framing.

**Why is the 90-day reserve so high?**
Because most new hosts launch in the slow season, hit one bad month, and can't make mortgage. Three months is the conservative floor. The Start tab will flag you if your cash invested approaches your available cash.

**Does this include rehab or construction costs?**
No. Minor cosmetic only. Major rehab needs the Rehab Budget SKU.

**Is the Y1 ROI projection accurate?**
It's a projection. ADR × occupancy × available nights minus operating costs. The workbook gives you the math; you bring honest numbers. The Deal Analyzer (ACQ-001) does the deeper underwriting with a stress test.

**Is this US-only?**
Yes. USD only. Permit, LLC, and insurance assumptions are US-based.

## What's inside (the workbook)

- **Welcome** — cover, host first name, property name, stay dates auto-display
- **Arrival** — door code, parking, where to park the second car, late-arrival instructions
- **WiFi + Tech** — network name, password, TV inputs, streaming logins, smart-lock how-to, thermostat
- **House Rules** — quiet hours, smoking, pets, max guests, parties — section-headed so it reads, not lectures
- **Local Guide** — restaurants, coffee, groceries, urgent care, vet — distance, phone, notes
- **Trash + Recycling** — pickup day, bin location, sorting rules
- **Departure** — checkout time, key return, last-minute tasks (dishwasher? thermostat?)
- **Emergency** — hospital, police non-emergency, 24-hr vet, utility outage numbers — host phone auto-pulled from Welcome tab
- **Host Reference (hidden tab)** — your private notes: vendor contacts, where the spare key actually lives, what NOT to share with guests

## FAQ

**Can I edit it?**
Every cell. No password protection, no protected ranges. Edit, rebrand, re-color — it is your file.

**Does it work in Google Sheets?**
Yes. Open via Google Drive → Open with Google Sheets. Some Excel-specific formatting may shift slightly; the content travels intact.

**How do I make a QR code?**
Export your finished workbook to PDF, host it anywhere (Google Drive shareable link, Dropbox, your own site), generate a QR code pointing at the URL (any free QR generator). Place the QR on a small printed placard at check-in.

**Will it look the same when printed?**
Yes. The print area is set on every guest-facing tab. The Host Reference tab is hidden by default in the PDF export — it stays visible for you in the workbook.

**Is the Host Reference tab really hidden from guests?**
Hidden, but not encrypted. If a technically-curious guest opens the .xlsx, they can unhide it. We strongly recommend you only ever share the PDF export with guests, not the .xlsx itself.

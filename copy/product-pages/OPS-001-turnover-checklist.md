## What's inside (the workbook)

- **Cover** — your property name, your cleaner's name, the season. Edit once.
- **Pre-Arrival Checklist** — printable. Bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living, exterior, supplies, hot tub / pool / fire pit if applicable. ~80 line items.
- **Quick-Score Scorecard** — 10-row tab you fill in after each turnover. Auto-rolls into a per-cleaner average, a 30-day trend, and a "next conversation" flag.
- **Supply Pars** — one row per consumable (TP, paper towels, dish soap, coffee, shampoo). When score < target, the row turns red.
- **Photo-Standard Reference** — a 1-page guide of what "made bed" looks like, what "drain hair removed" looks like, what "coffee station reset" looks like. Print and tape inside the supply closet.
- **Settings** — your cleaner names, properties, supply pars.

## FAQ

**Will my cleaner actually use a paper checklist?**
Most do. Cleaners who already do the job well like the checklist because it stops you re-explaining "the usual" — they can prove they finished. Cleaners who fight the checklist usually have a reason worth knowing about.

**Can I customize it for my property?**
Yes. Every line item is editable. Add a hot tub row. Remove the hardwood-mop row if you have all carpet. Add the specific drawer where the spare batteries live. Re-print.

**Is the Scorecard for me or for the cleaner?**
You. Fill it in after the turn or during your spot-check. Your cleaner does not need to see the score every time — but most appreciate seeing "92% on 30-day average" once a month.

**Does it print clean on letter and A4?**
Yes. Print area set, margins tight, single-page per checklist. International A4 hosts: paper size override is a 2-click change in Excel.

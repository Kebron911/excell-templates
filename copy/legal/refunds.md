## Refund Policy

We sell digital products — Excel templates, PDF guides, and downloadable manuals. By their nature, digital goods are non-returnable once delivered. That said, we want every customer to be happy, so here is how we handle refunds.

### 14-day satisfaction window (own-site purchases)

If you bought from {{ thestrledger.com | strmanuals.com }} and the template, guide, or manual didn't do what we said it would, email **hello@{{ thestrledger.com | strmanuals.com }}** within **14 days of purchase** with:

1. Your order number
2. One sentence describing what didn't match expectations

We'll refund you in full, no questions asked. Refunds are processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.

### What is covered

- The file didn't open or was technically broken
- The product description didn't match what you received
- You bought a duplicate by mistake
- The product simply wasn't useful for your situation

### What is NOT covered

- Requests outside the 14-day window
- Bundle purchases where you've downloaded 3+ files of the bundle (partial-bundle refunds aren't possible because the goods are now permanently in your possession)
- Affiliate or referral-credit-based purchases (refund the credit instead)
- Requests after you've requested a chargeback through your bank (handled via chargeback process, not refund)

### Etsy and Gumroad purchases

Purchases made through our Etsy shop or Gumroad listings are handled by Etsy's and Gumroad's policies, not this one. Each platform has its own refund window and process:

- **Etsy:** Use the Etsy "Help with order" flow. We approve all reasonable refund requests but Etsy's UI does not let us refund outside that flow.
- **Gumroad:** Email hello@thestrledger.com — we'll process via Gumroad admin within 1 business day.

### Subscriptions and recurring products

If we ever introduce subscription products (compliance alerts, AI-tool monthly tiers), the cancellation and refund terms will be specifically called out at checkout for those products. We do not auto-renew without showing you the renewal price + cancel option 7 days before each charge.

### Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank without first contacting us, your account is suspended pending review. We will respond to the chargeback with the order details, and once the bank resolves it (in either direction), you may contact us to restore your account access.

For any digital product where a chargeback is ruled in our favor (very common — the product was delivered, the file is documented), the account remains suspended.

### Honest note

We are a small operation. We do not want to be in the refund-collection business; we'd much rather solve whatever problem the product didn't solve. If a template needs an update or a guide needs a fix, send us the details and we'll fix it for everyone (including you). That is more useful to us than a refund.

### Contact

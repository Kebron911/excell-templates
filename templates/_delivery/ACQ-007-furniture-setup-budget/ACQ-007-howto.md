# How to Use Your Furniture / Setup Budget Calculator

## What this workbook does

Pre-furnishing budget for a new STR. Walks you through every room, lists every essential item with cost benchmarks, tracks actual vs estimated. Each item carries a 7-year MACRS class flag — feeds directly into TAX-013 depreciation tracker. Includes a month-1 operating reserve line so you don't accidentally spend furniture money on cleaning or supplies in your first month.

Most STR launches blow the furniture budget by 50-100% because the buyer underestimates the long tail (toaster, can opener, ice tray, soap dispenser, doormat). This workbook is the comprehensive list that catches the long tail.

## First-time setup (20 min)

1. Open tab **Property Snapshot** — beds, baths, target opening date, total furniture budget.
2. Tab **Room-by-Room Walkthrough** — bedrooms, kitchen, baths, living, exterior, technology, supplies. Pre-loaded with 200+ items per room category.
3. For each item: mark Buy / Already Have / Skip and enter estimated cost. Cost benchmarks shown for each item (low / typical / premium).

## During furnishing

- Use the Master Shopping List tab as your buying queue. Group by retailer (Amazon, IKEA, Target, Wayfair) for efficient runs.
- Update Actual column as receipts come in.
- The Furnishing Density metric shows total $ ÷ square footage. Industry benchmark: $25-35/sqft for 4-star STR.

## At launch + tax time

- The 7-yr MACRS flag column lets you dump qualifying items directly into TAX-013 Depreciation Tracker for the asset register.
- Items eligible for §179 (typically furniture, appliances, decorative fixtures) flow into TAX-009 Section 179 Planner — front-load the deduction in year 1.

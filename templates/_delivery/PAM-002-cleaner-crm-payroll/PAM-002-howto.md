# How to Use Your Cleaner CRM + Payroll

## What this workbook does

For Pro Pam (managing 10+ properties with a cleaning team). Combines: cleaner CRM (contact info, performance, certifications), turnover assignment log, payroll calculator (hourly + per-turnover + bonus), and 1099-NEC year-end summary auto-feeding TAX-003.

Solves: "I have 6 cleaners, 12 properties, 30 turnovers a week, and I'm trying to schedule all of this on a whiteboard."

## First-time setup (45 min — yes, really)

1. Open tab **Cleaner Roster** — every cleaner / cleaning service:
   - Name, business name, contact, EIN/SSN (for 1099-NEC year-end)
   - W-9 on file? Y/N + W-9 received date
   - Hourly rate + per-turnover flat rate (some are one, some are both)
   - Properties certified to clean (some cleaners only do specific properties)
   - Performance score (1-5 from past work)
2. Tab **Property Roster** — list properties with cleaner-preference order (primary, backup, emergency).
3. Tab **Settings** — pay period (weekly / biweekly), payment method, bonus rules.

## Weekly turnover assignment

1. Open the Assignment Calendar. For each turnover:
   - Property + date + check-in time
   - Pick cleaner (from cleaner-preference order). Workbook flags if the cleaner is double-booked.
   - Estimated hours, estimated supplies cost
2. After turnover: update actual hours, actual supplies, performance notes.

## Payroll calculation (every pay period)

The Payroll tab auto-aggregates from the Assignment Log:
- Per cleaner: total turnovers, total hours, hourly pay, per-turnover pay, bonuses
- Total amount due per cleaner
- Print pay stubs OR ACH list ready for QuickBooks / Gusto / direct deposit

## Year-end 1099-NEC

The 1099 Summary tab pulls every cleaner who received ≥$600 in the calendar year. Outputs:
- Cleaner name + EIN/SSN + address
- Total paid in calendar year
- W-9 status (red flag if missing — chase before December 15)

This data feeds directly into TAX-003 1099-NEC Tracker for Form 1099-NEC filing.

## When to scale to a SaaS

If you're managing 25+ properties with 10+ cleaners, the workbook starts straining. Consider Properly, Breezeway, or Turno. For 10-25 properties, this workbook + a calendar app is sufficient and saves $50-150/mo in SaaS fees.

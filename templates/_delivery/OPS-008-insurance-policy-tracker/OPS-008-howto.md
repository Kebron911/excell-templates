# How to Use Your Insurance Policy Tracker

## What this workbook does

Single register of every insurance policy touching your STR portfolio — STR-specific dwelling insurance, umbrella liability, vehicle, business equipment, AirCover backup. Surfaces expiring policies in the next 60 days, premium totals, claim history, and "uninsured exposure" gaps.

## First-time setup (15 min)

1. **Policy Register** — for each policy:
   - Carrier, policy #, type (STR dwelling / umbrella / vehicle / business / cyber / other)
   - Properties covered
   - Coverage limits (dwelling, contents, liability, business interruption)
   - Deductible
   - Annual premium
   - Renewal date
   - Agent contact + emergency claim contact
2. **Coverage Gap Analysis** (the killer feature): the workbook walks through 8 risk categories and flags which you DO and DO NOT have coverage for.

## Every month

1. Open the Calendar. Yellow flags 60 days before any renewal.
2. For yellow-flagged policies: shop carriers (insurance carriers raise rates more aggressively at renewal than during term).
3. Update the Policy Register with new terms.

## The 8 risk categories

Coverage Gap Analysis tab walks through:
1. **Dwelling/structure damage** (storm, fire, theft) — base policy
2. **Personal liability** — base policy
3. **Loss of business income** during repairs — often missing
4. **Short-term rental endorsement** — many homeowners' policies EXCLUDE STR use
5. **Commercial liability** above platform's AirCover coverage
6. **Cyber / data breach** (if you collect guest data on direct-booking site)
7. **Vehicle (if used for business)** — personal auto policies often exclude business use
8. **Workers comp** (if you have employees, even part-time)

## Common gaps the workbook catches

- "STR endorsement not on dwelling policy" — claim denied when guest damages property
- "Umbrella excludes STR rental" — fine print most hosts don't read
- "Vehicle commercial use not covered" — claim denied for accident on supply run
- "Business interruption coverage missing" — roof damage = 6 weeks closed = $48K of lost revenue with no insurance recovery

## At renewal

1. Open Policy Register row for the renewing policy.
2. Click into Carrier-Comparison tab — paste 3 quotes (current carrier + 2 competitors).
3. Compare on premium, deductible, coverage limits, exclusions.
4. Switch carrier OR negotiate with current.

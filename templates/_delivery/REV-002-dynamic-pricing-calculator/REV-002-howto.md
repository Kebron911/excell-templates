# How to Use Your Dynamic Pricing Calculator

## What this workbook does

Computes a suggested nightly rate per night of a 365-day calendar based on: base rate, season multiplier (4 seasons), day-of-week multiplier, and event lift (concerts, holidays, playoffs). Outputs a pricing CSV you can paste into Airbnb / VRBO custom-pricing — or use as a sanity check against a paid tool like PriceLabs / Wheelhouse.

Most STR hosts either flat-rate (leaving 15-30% revenue on the table) or pay $20-50/mo for a pricing tool they don't fully understand. This workbook is the cheaper, transparent alternative for hosts running ≤5 properties.

## First-time setup (15 min)

1. Open tab **Property Inputs** — base nightly rate, target occupancy.
2. Tab **Season Multipliers** — 4 seasons × your local pattern (default: peak +35%, shoulder +0%, off-peak −20%, deep off-peak −35%).
3. Tab **Day-of-Week Multipliers** — Fri/Sat lift (default +20-40%), Sun-Thu base.
4. Tab **Events Calendar** — local concerts, sporting events, festivals, holidays. Add date + lift % for each.

## Reading the outputs

- **365-Day Calendar tab:** every night with suggested rate
- **Pricing CSV Export:** ready to paste into Airbnb's bulk-rate import or use as PriceLabs reference
- **Min-Floor Check:** flags any night where the calculated rate falls below your minimum (rare but happens in deep off-peak)

## Refresh cadence

- **Weekly:** scan the next 30 days for events you missed (concerts pop up late). Update the Events tab.
- **Monthly:** re-export the CSV. Push to Airbnb / VRBO.
- **Seasonally:** review whether your seasonal multipliers still match reality (use FIN-005 YoY Comparison to validate).

## When to upgrade to PriceLabs / Wheelhouse

If you're running 5+ properties OR your market has high day-by-day volatility (NYC, LA, beach towns with weather-sensitive demand), a paid tool earns its $20-50/mo. For most hosts running ≤5 properties in stable markets, this workbook gets ~85% of the value at $0/mo.

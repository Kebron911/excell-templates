# How to Use Your STR License Renewal Calendar

## What this workbook does

A calendar-first view of every STR-related renewal deadline across your portfolio: STR licenses, business licenses, sales/lodging tax permits, fire/safety inspections, and any state lottery or city-specific registrations. Lighter than the full License/Permit Tracker (OPS-003) — this workbook is the at-a-glance view you check on the 1st of every month.

For single-property hosts, this is sufficient. For 3+ properties across multiple jurisdictions, pair with OPS-003 for the deeper register.

## First-time setup (10 min)

1. Open tab **Property Roster** — list every property + jurisdiction.
2. Tab **Renewals** — for each renewal: type (STR license / business license / TOT registration / inspection), expiration date, renewal cost, lead time.
3. Pre-loaded with the 8 most common renewal types per US STR market.

## Every month

1. Open the Calendar tab on the 1st. Scan the next 90 days.
2. Yellow flags = renew this month (60-day window).
3. Red flags = renew this week (30-day window or already overdue).
4. Click into the flagged row, follow the "Renew" link to the issuing authority's portal.
5. After renewal: update expiration date + uploaded confirmation document link.

## Why this exists separately from OPS-003

OPS-003 is the full register — every detail of every permit, audit-ready. This workbook is the at-a-glance dashboard. They pull from the same data structure if you own both. If you only own one, this is the right one for hosts running ≤2 properties; OPS-003 is the right one for portfolios.

## When a permit lapses

The Penalty Reference tab lists what each jurisdiction does to expired permits. Common penalties:
- $250-$2,500 fine per day late
- Listing suspension (Airbnb / Vrbo deactivate when they get the city notice)
- Re-registration as a new applicant (not renewal — sometimes harder)
- Loss of grandfathered status if you were operating under a hardship clause

Renewing on time is cheaper than any of these.

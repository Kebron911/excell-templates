# How to Use Your DSCR Tracker

## What this workbook does

Computes Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR = Net Operating Income ÷ Annual Debt Service) per property and portfolio-wide. Critical for STR operators pursuing DSCR loans — most lenders require ≥1.20 to qualify, and many will rate-shop you down if you're at 1.50+. Surfaces which properties qualify for refinance, which need rate-shopping, which are at-risk if occupancy drops 10%.

## First-time setup (10 min)

1. Open tab **Property Roster** — every property with its NOI and annual debt service. Pull from TAX-002 P&L (NOI) and your loan amortization schedule (debt service).
2. Tab **Settings** — DSCR thresholds: refi-eligible (≥1.25 typical), at-risk (<1.10), warning (1.10–1.20).

## Reading the outputs

- **Per-Property DSCR:** color-coded green / yellow / red based on threshold
- **Portfolio DSCR:** aggregate NOI / aggregate debt service — what your overall lender sees
- **Refinance-Eligible List:** properties at ≥1.25 + at least 24 months seasoned. These are your refi candidates.
- **10% Sensitivity:** re-runs DSCR with revenue × 0.90. Properties that flip to red here are exposed to a market softening.

## Quarterly review

1. Update NOI per property (year-to-date annualized).
2. Open the Refinance-Eligible List. Cross-check against current DSCR rates from your lender list.
3. For any property in the warning band: review occupancy + ADR for compression, talk to lender about portfolio-restructure options.

## Loan-shopping comparison tab

When you do refinance, the Loan-Shopping tab lets you compare 3 lender offers side-by-side: rate, term, points, prepay penalty, total cost over 5 years. Saves $5K–$25K per refi vs. taking the first offer.

# How to Use Your Glamping / Unique-Stay P&L

## What this workbook does

P&L tailored to glamping / unique-stay operators (yurts, tiny homes, A-frames, treehouses, cave stays, domes, vintage trailers). Differs from a standard cabin P&L: heavy seasonality, propane/generator costs, port-a-potty / off-grid utilities, more frequent linen replacement, weather-related cancellations, propane-tank tracking, alternative-revenue lines (firewood sales, on-site experiences).

## First-time setup (20 min)

1. **Property Snapshot** — type (yurt / dome / tiny home / A-frame / etc.), site amenities, capacity, season.
2. **Off-Grid Utility Costs** — propane (per tank, refill cadence), generator fuel, water delivery if any, septic/port-a-potty pump cadence.
3. **Specialized Line Items**:
   - Propane tank refills (winter heat)
   - Generator fuel (off-grid power)
   - Port-a-potty pump-outs
   - Linen replacement (more frequent than standard cabin — outdoor wear)
   - Weather-cancellation refunds
4. **Bonus Revenue**:
   - Firewood sales
   - On-site experiences (hosted dinner, guided hike, sauna access, hot tub upgrade)
   - Pet fees specific to glamping (often higher %)

## Reading the outputs

- **Per-Stay Net Revenue** — properly accounts for all the off-grid variable costs that standard P&Ls miss
- **Seasonality Curve** — most glamping has 60-80% revenue concentration in 4-6 peak months
- **Off-Grid Cost as % of Revenue** — typically 8-15% (vs 3-6% for cabins)
- **Bonus Revenue %** — track whether your firewood / experiences are material or just nice-to-have

## Specialty seasons

Glamping / unique-stay typically has:
- **Peak (3-4 months):** May-Sep typical, weather-dependent
- **Shoulder (2 months):** April + Oct, often heavy weather cancellation
- **Off (4-5 months):** winter weeks at deep discount or closed entirely

The workbook lets you set per-month occupancy + ADR independently rather than the standard P&L's seasonal multiplier.

## At year-end

Print P&L. Drop into TAX-002 single-property template or hand to CPA. The off-grid utility lines map to Schedule E Line 18 (utilities) — same line as cabin utilities, just more itemized.

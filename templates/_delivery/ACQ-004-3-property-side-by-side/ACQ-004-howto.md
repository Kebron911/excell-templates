# How to Use Your 3-Property Side-by-Side Comparison

## What this workbook does

Triples the STR Deal Analyzer (ACQ-001) into three columns side-by-side. Compares three acquisition candidates head-to-head on NOI, cash-on-cash, cap rate, break-even occupancy, and total cash invested. Output: a winner-per-metric highlight strip + a BUY / WALK / NEGOTIATE verdict per deal.

Use this when you have 2-3 properties under serious consideration and you need to pick one before submitting an offer. Running each through ACQ-001 separately works, but it's hard to see the relative pros/cons at a glance.

## First-time setup (15 min)

1. Open tab **Property A / B / C** — fill in the underwriting inputs for each (price, financing, projected revenue, OpEx). Same fields as ACQ-001.
2. The Comparison tab auto-populates with the three deals side-by-side.

## Reading the outputs

- **Winner-per-metric strip:** for each metric (NOI, CoC, cap rate, break-even, IRR), highlights which property wins.
- **Verdict per property:** BUY / WALK / NEGOTIATE based on your thresholds (Settings tab).
- **Sensitivity column:** which property is most resilient to a 10% revenue drop.

## When to walk on all three

If all three deals fail at the BUY threshold, you don't have three deals — you have three bad deals in disguise. Tighten your criteria and walk.

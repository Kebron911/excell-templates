## The one specific pain

"My title and photos are mid. My listing has been sitting at 65% occupancy when comps in my market hit 80%. I don't know if it's title length, photo order, missing amenities, or my response time. I need a checklist that tells me what to fix — not a 'tips and tricks' blog post."

## What this template does

A listing-audit workspace that:
1. Scores the listing across **40 weighted criteria** in 5 categories (Title, Description, Photos, Amenities, Trust Signals)
2. Generates a **prioritized fix list** — each red item paired with a specific actionable fix in the same row
3. Separates **Airbnb ranking factors** (algorithm — response time, instant book, cancellation rate) from **guest-conversion factors** (photos, title, copy) — the two different problems hosts conflate
4. Provides a **public methodology page** ("How we score") citing each criterion's evidence — Airbnb published / host community / data study — with weights and last-verified date
5. Includes a **Top-5 Comp Compare** tab — paste 5 competitor listings, see side-by-side weakness map (Full only)
6. Sets up a **manual rescore reminder** with a date field — built-in re-audit cadence

## The 40 criteria (Full)

**Category A — Title (5 criteria, 15% weight):**
1. Title length 35-50 chars (10 pts)
2. Includes city/neighborhood (10 pts)
3. Includes 1-2 highest-value amenities (hot tub / view / walkable) (10 pts)
4. No ALL CAPS, no emojis, no clickbait (10 pts)
5. Differentiating phrase or experience hook (10 pts)

**Category B — Description (8 criteria, 20% weight):**
6. Opens with a hook line (NOT "Welcome to my home!") (10 pts)
7. ≥150 words, ≤500 words (10 pts)
8. Lists what makes property unique in first 3 sentences (10 pts)
9. Mentions surrounding area + walkability/drive times (10 pts)
10. Has dedicated bedrooms section with sleeping config (10 pts)
11. Has dedicated amenities section (formatted) (10 pts)
12. Includes house rules (positive framing) (10 pts)
13. Closes with hospitality / response promise (10 pts)

**Category C — Photos (12 criteria, 30% weight — the highest-impact category):**
14. ≥25 photos (10 pts)
15. Cover is exterior or hero interior shot (10 pts)
16. Bedroom photo per bedroom (10 pts)
17. Bathroom photo per bathroom (10 pts)
18. Kitchen photo (10 pts)
19. Living/common space photo (10 pts)
20. Outdoor space photo (if applicable) (10 pts)
21. Twilight or sunset photo (10 pts)
22. Professional quality (sharp, well-lit, level horizon) (10 pts)
23. Photos sequenced as guest journey (arrival → living → bedrooms → bath → kitchen → outdoor) (10 pts)
24. Captions on every photo (10 pts)
25. No phone-camera-mirror shots, no clutter visible (10 pts)

**Category D — Amenities (8 criteria, 20% weight):**
26. ≥40 amenities checked (10 pts)
27. Top-tier amenities (hot tub / pool / view / fireplace) listed (10 pts)
28. Fast WiFi listed with speed (10 pts)
29. Workspace / desk listed (10 pts)
30. Self check-in OR clear arrival instructions (10 pts)
31. Pet policy clearly stated (10 pts)
32. Family-friendly amenities (if family-friendly listing) (10 pts)
33. Accessibility features listed (10 pts)

**Category E — Trust signals (7 criteria, 15% weight — Airbnb algorithm factors):**
34. Response time <1hr (10 pts)
35. Response rate ≥95% (10 pts)
36. Instant Book on (10 pts)
37. Superhost status (10 pts)
38. Cancellation policy = Flexible or Moderate (10 pts)
39. ≥20 reviews with ≥4.8 avg (10 pts)
40. Verified ID + government ID requirement set (10 pts)

Total max score: 40 × 10 = 400 points → display as 0-100% scale.

## Outputs

**Audit Score:**
- 5 category subtotals + overall %
- Each criterion row: criterion text | weight | met? (Y/N/Partial) | points | suggested fix (specific)
- Conditional formatting: red <60%, gold 60-79%, gold-soft 80-89%, parchment ≥90%

**Start tab headline:**
- Score: XX / 100 (Georgia 36pt, conditional fill)
- "Top 3 fixes to prioritize" — top 3 lowest-scoring criteria sorted by weight × points-missed
- "Re-audit by: <date>" — input cell + reminder

**Top-5 Comp Compare (Full):**
Paste 5 comp listings (URL or paste data). 6 columns (mine + 5 comps), 40 rows of criteria. Reveals: where am I behind comps?

**Methodology — How We Score (Full):**
40 rows, one per criterion, columns: criterion / weight / why it matters / source (Airbnb published / host community / data study) / last-verified date. Footer: "v1.x updated <date>."

## External data references

- Airbnb's published ranking factors (response time, instant book, cancellation, completeness)
- Host-community consensus from Reddit r/AirBnBHosts, BiggerPockets STR forum, Hospitable blog studies
- AirDNA Marketscore methodology (referenced for amenities weighting)
- "Algorithm changes — methodology updated <date>" stamp

## Business logic

- **Score without a fix list = useless.** Every red criterion has a same-row specific fix (NOT generic "improve photos"; specific "Reorder photos: cover should be exterior, then living, then bedrooms").
- **Two-problem separation.** Algorithm factors (response time, instant book) shown distinct from conversion factors (photos, copy). Hosts conflate these and fix the wrong one.
- **Public methodology** is the moat. Other Etsy SEO audits are black-box. Showing our work is a marketing asset (lead magnet candidate).
- **No score inflation.** Real audits score 60-75% for typical listings. Don't ship a template that gives everyone 92%.
- Capacity: 1 listing per workbook (wizard mode, re-run per property).
- Re-audit reminder is manual (date input, not auto). Re-run after material changes.

## QA sample data

Sample listing: "Smokies Ridge Cabin — Hot Tub, Mountain Views, Pet-Friendly"
- Title: 51 chars (just over 50 — FAIL)
- Description: 220 words ✅
- 19 photos (FAIL — needs ≥25)
- Cover photo: bedroom (FAIL — should be exterior or hero living)
- 38 amenities ✅
- Response time: 4hr (FAIL — should be <1hr)
- Instant Book: OFF (FAIL)
- Superhost: Yes ✅
- Cancellation: Strict (FAIL — Moderate or Flexible better)

Expected score: ~67% (Gold band).
Top 3 fixes: (1) Turn on Instant Book, (2) Add 6+ photos with cover swap, (3) Drop title to ≤50 chars.

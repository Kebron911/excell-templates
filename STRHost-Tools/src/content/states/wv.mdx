## How West Virginia lodging tax works

West Virginia charges a **6% state sales tax** on lodging plus a **local hotel occupancy tax** of up to 6% added by counties and municipalities. Combined effective rates run **6% to 12%**. Top STR markets:

- **Harpers Ferry (Jefferson County)** — 6% state + 6% county hotel = **12% effective**. National Historical Park draws year-round traffic; DC/Baltimore weekend market.
- **Seneca Rocks / Pendleton County** — 6% state + 4% county = **10%**. Climbing, hiking, and fall-foliage cabin market.
- **Snowshoe (Pocahontas County)** — 6% state + 6% county = **12%**. Largest ski resort market in the state; winter and summer dual-season demand.
- **Charleston, Morgantown, Huntington** — 6% state + 6% city = **12%**. WVU football weekends drive Morgantown.
- **New River Gorge area (Fayette County)** — 6% state + 6% county = **12%**. National Park designation (2020) drove a STR boom.

Stays of **30 consecutive nights or longer** are exempt from the 6% state sales tax and from local hotel occupancy tax in most jurisdictions.

## Platform collection

Airbnb collects and remits the **6% state sales tax** AND most **local hotel occupancy taxes** in West Virginia under a marketplace facilitator agreement. **Vrbo does not have a current statewide marketplace agreement** in WV — Vrbo hosts are responsible for the full stack themselves.

## What this means in practice

- **Airbnb hosts** — state and most local tax handled. Verify on your listing's tax disclosure.
- **Vrbo or direct-booking hosts** — register with the WV State Tax Department for a sales tax permit; file monthly or quarterly. Also register with your county for hotel occupancy tax.
- **Snowshoe hosts** — Pocahontas County hotel occupancy tax is one of the higher local rates in the state; verify platform is collecting the full 6%.
- **New River Gorge area** — Fayette County saw a STR-supply spike after the National Park designation in 2020; tax compliance enforcement also tightened.
- **30+ night stays** — exempt from state and local lodging tax in most cases.

## How to use the calculator above

1. Enter your booking subtotal (nightly × nights + cleaning fee, before tax).
2. Enter the local add-on rate. Jefferson, Pocahontas (Snowshoe), and Fayette County are 6%; Pendleton is 4%; Charleston, Morgantown, Huntington are 6%.
3. The result shows the effective rate, total tax, and the guest line item.

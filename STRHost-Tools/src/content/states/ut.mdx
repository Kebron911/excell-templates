## How Utah lodging tax works

Utah layers several charges on short-term rental stays:

1. **4.85% state sales tax** — applies to the rental charge.
2. **0.32% statewide local sales tax** — a uniform statewide add.
3. **Local sales tax** — county/city add 1%–2%.
4. **Transient room tax (TRT)** — counties levy 0.5%–4.25% specifically for STR/hotel stays.
5. **Resort communities tax** — Park City, Snowbird, Brian Head, and others add 1.1% on top.

Effective rates in the top markets:
- **Park City** (Summit County): 4.85% + 0.32% + 1% local + 4.25% TRT + 1.1% resort = **roughly 11.5%**
- **Moab** (Grand County): 4.85% + 0.32% + 1.5% local + 4.25% TRT = **roughly 10.9%**
- **Salt Lake City** (Salt Lake County): 4.85% + 0.32% + 1.75% local + 4.25% TRT = **roughly 11.2%**
- **St. George** (Washington County): 4.85% + 0.32% + 1.5% local + 4% TRT = **roughly 10.7%**
- **Springdale / Zion**: 4.85% + 0.32% + 2% local + 4.25% TRT = **roughly 11.4%**

## Platform collection

Airbnb and Vrbo collect and remit **state sales tax + statewide local + transient room tax + most county/city local taxes** as marketplace facilitators across Utah. Coverage is comprehensive for major STR markets (Summit, Grand, Salt Lake, Washington counties).

The [Utah State Tax Commission](https://tax.utah.gov/) publishes the marketplace-facilitator agreement list. Always verify against the latest.

## What this means in practice

- For **platform bookings** — all major layers handled.
- For **direct bookings** — register with the Utah State Tax Commission, file monthly (or quarterly if low volume), and remit. Use the [TAP portal](https://tap.tax.utah.gov/) for filings.
- **30+ night stays** are exempt from the transient room tax in most jurisdictions; state sales tax treatment varies. Verify locally.
- **Park City is strict.** STR permitting requirements + the resort-communities tax mean you must register both with the city and the state. Park City actively audits STRs.
- **Moab** has STR caps in certain zones. Tax registration does not equal zoning approval.

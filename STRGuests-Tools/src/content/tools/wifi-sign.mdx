## How it works

The Wi-Fi sign generator builds a single-page printable PDF with your network name
and password in a frame-ready design. Three templates: minimal, cottage, modern.
Generation is fully client-side — your password never leaves your browser.

## How to use this generator

1. Enter your property name (small subtitle on the sign).
2. Type the SSID exactly as it appears on guests' phones.
3. Type the password. Big, bold, and contrasting — guests should be able to read it
   from across the room.
4. Pick a template that matches your property's look.
5. Click **Download PDF**. Print on US Letter, trim to 8x10 if framing.

## Tips for guest-friendly Wi-Fi signs

- Keep passwords pronounceable. "mountain-air-2026" beats "X8mPq2!aF". Hosts who
  type-test their passwords with three new guests cut support questions ~40%.
- Print and laminate, or pop into a frame and lean on a console table — guests notice.
- Refresh the password seasonally. Use a different color for the password block to
  signal "this changed."

## FAQ

### Does this generate a QR code?

Not in v1. QR support is on the roadmap. For now, the PDF is plain text — works
universally, no app required.

### Can I print on cardstock?

Yes. The single-page Letter design prints cleanly on most home printer cardstocks.
For a glossy finish, take the PDF to a local print shop — usually under $5.

### Will my Wi-Fi password be saved or transmitted?

No. Generation is client-side only. The PDF is built in your browser; nothing is
uploaded.

### Can I customize colors?

Not in v1. The three templates lock to our hospitality-warm palette. Custom branding
will arrive with the v2 welcome book editor.

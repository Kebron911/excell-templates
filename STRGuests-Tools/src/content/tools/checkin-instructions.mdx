## The clearer the instructions, the smoother the arrival

Most "where do we park?" messages happen between 9pm and midnight, when guests are tired, the host is asleep, and the booking platform's auto-translate is butchering the door code. A printable PDF — sent in the booking confirmation, then opened on the guest's phone at the door — pre-empts most of it.

This generator builds a multi-page check-in PDF in your browser with optional door and parking photos. Nothing uploaded; PDF is yours.

## Sections (each optional)

1. **Cover** — property name + arrival window + host signature.
2. **Getting here** — address + parking notes + optional parking photo.
3. **Getting in** — door code in oversized monospace + notes + optional photo of the lockbox or front door.
4. **Wi-Fi** — credential tile (use the Wi-Fi sign generator for the printable QR version).
5. **First-night basics** — short numbered list: lights, thermostat, trash day, drinking water.
6. **Emergency contacts** — host phone (auto-formatted), nearest hospital, police.

## How to use the printable

- **Send the PDF in the pre-arrival message.** Most platforms attach PDFs in the booking confirmation thread.
- **One photo is worth ten lines.** A 30-second iPhone shot of the lockbox or the parking lot saves you an arrival-night message exchange.
- **Keep door codes on cover OR section 3, not both.** Codes get screenshotted, screenshots get shared. Section 3 is best.
- **Re-generate per booking if codes rotate.** If you rotate door codes per stay, generate a per-booking PDF and attach it to the confirmation.

## Frequently asked

**What image formats are supported?**
PNG and JPG. Files are read in-browser and embedded directly into the PDF — nothing is uploaded.

**Is there a max image size?**
Practically a few MB. Big images make big PDFs. iPhone Live Photos / HEIC are not supported — convert to JPG first.

**Does this also handle the wifi QR?**
The Wi-Fi page here has SSID + password tiles, but no QR. For a scannable Wi-Fi sign, use the [Wi-Fi Sign Generator](/wifi-sign).

**What about a digital signature line?**
Not yet. The Welcome Book builder has a host-signature line on the cover; this generator focuses on the operational arrival flow.

## A welcome book turns a stay into a host

The welcome book is the highest-leverage piece of guest collateral most hosts skip. It pre-empts 80% of the messages that show up at 11pm on arrival night ("what's the wifi?", "is there a coffee maker?", "where do we park?"), and it doubles as the property's brand voice — the first thing guests touch when they walk in.

This builder generates a multi-page PDF in your browser. No upload, no signup, nothing tracked. The PDF is yours.

## How it works

The book is built in five sections. Each is optional — toggle on what fits the property:

1. **Cover** — title page with property name, tagline, and optional host signature.
2. **Wi-Fi & access** — network credentials in a high-contrast tile, plus door/garage codes.
3. **Neighborhood favorites** — your local picks, one line each: coffee shop, hike, neighborhood gem.
4. **House tips** — the small instructions that prevent damage and confusion: hot tub flush, trash day, thermostat range.
5. **Emergency contacts** — host phone, nearest hospital, police.

Each enabled section becomes its own page. The cover always renders.

## How to use the printable

- **Print one for inside the property.** Bind it, hole-punch it, or laminate it. Bonus points: a clean clipboard on the kitchen counter.
- **Send a digital copy with the booking confirmation.** Most guests skim it before arrival, then re-read on-site.
- **Re-generate when anything changes.** Wi-Fi password rotation, new garage code, neighborhood spot opens or closes — re-run the builder, replace the printed copy.
- **Strip the brand line if you want a fully unbranded version.** Open the PDF in Preview/Acrobat and edit the footer.

## Frequently asked

**How many pages does the book get?**
Anywhere from 1 (cover only) to 6 (cover + all five sections). Most hosts ship 4–5 page books.

**Can I add photos?**
Not in this version — text-only. The Check-in Instructions generator (#4 in the cluster) supports image upload for door photos and parking maps.

**Does the PDF work on guest phones?**
Yes — it's a standard PDF, opens fine in iOS/Android viewers and most messaging apps.

**Why is the cover so plain?**
Restraint. Welcome-book covers that try too hard date quickly; restrained covers age well. If you want a custom cover, drop your own JPG into the PDF in Preview/Acrobat.

## Why house rules belong in writing, not just on the listing

A guest who breaks a rule "they didn't see" is a guest you can't easily charge for damage. Airbnb, VRBO, and Booking dispute resolution all favor hosts who can show that house rules were posted, signed, and visible inside the property. A printable, framed copy on the fridge — or tucked into the welcome book — turns a vague expectation into a documented one.

This generator builds a single-page (or multi-page) PDF in your browser. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is sent anywhere. The PDF is yours.

## How it works

1. **Pick presets that fit the property.** Twenty-two common rules covering smoking, parties, pets, quiet hours, occupancy, pool/hot tub, and checkout.
2. **Add your specifics.** Custom rules go in a free-form box, one per line. Anything property-specific — gate latches, dish handling, neighbors — lives here.
3. **Name the property.** Shows in the header so guests know which property the rules belong to (matters when you run more than one).
4. **Optional host signature.** Sign the bottom — guests are more likely to honor rules that feel personal.
5. **Download.** PDF generates on your device, branded with the strguests.tools footer (you can remove the brand line in the welcome-book builder if you want a fully unbranded version).

## How to use the printable

- **Print one for inside the property.** Frame it or magnet it to the fridge. Visibility is half the enforcement.
- **Send a digital copy with the booking confirmation.** Most platforms let you attach a PDF in the pre-arrival message. Guests won't read it carefully — but they'll have it.
- **Update annually.** Quiet hours, occupancy rules, and pet policies drift. Re-generate every January.
- **Don't legalize the language.** "Please don't smoke" reads better than "smoking is strictly prohibited under penalty of forfeiture." Most hosts find friendlier wording leads to fewer disputes, not more.

## Frequently asked

**Is this legally binding?**
A house-rules PDF isn't a substitute for a rental agreement. Platforms like Airbnb embed their own contractual terms when a guest books — your house rules are property-specific guidance that those platforms surface during the resolution process. For long-stay rentals, talk to a local attorney about a separate rental agreement.

**Can I print this on my own letterhead?**
Yes. The download is a standard PDF — open it in Preview, Acrobat, or Word and add a logo before printing. A future version of this tool will let you upload a logo directly.

**Does it work on mobile?**
The form works on phones, but the preview pane is easier to review on desktop. The PDF generates fine either way.

**Is the PDF accessible?**
The PDF includes title and author metadata. Body text uses the embedded Helvetica font, which screen readers handle. We're working on full PDF/UA tagging for future versions.

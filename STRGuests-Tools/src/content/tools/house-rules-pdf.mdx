## How it works

This generator builds a single-page printable PDF of your house rules with a branded
header, numbered checkbox list, and an optional signature block. Everything happens in
your browser — your rules never leave your device.

## How to use this generator

1. Enter your property name and address. These appear under the title.
2. Set check-in and check-out times — these print as a contrast band at the top.
3. Paste your rules, one per line. Edit freely; the preview updates as you type.
4. Optionally add your name, contact phone, and a signature line for guests to sign.
5. Click **Download PDF** when the preview looks right.

## What good house rules look like

Concise, kind, and specific. Avoid blanket prohibitions ("no noise"); name the time
window ("quiet hours 10pm–8am"). Lead with safety items, then property protection,
then preferences. Five to ten rules is usually enough — guests skim, so density costs
compliance.

## FAQ

### Are these rules legally binding?

A signed house rules sheet is evidence of agreement, but enforceability depends on
your jurisdiction's short-term rental regulations and your booking platform's terms.
This is not legal advice — consult a local attorney for binding agreements.

### Does this work for VRBO and Booking.com too?

Yes. The PDF is platform-agnostic. Print it for in-property display, or attach to
booking confirmations.

### Can I add my logo?

Not in v1 — the header uses our default brand. Logo upload is on the roadmap for the
welcome book builder, where it has more visual impact.

### Does the PDF download capture my email?

No. The download is unconditional. We surface an optional newsletter signup
afterward, but the PDF is yours either way.

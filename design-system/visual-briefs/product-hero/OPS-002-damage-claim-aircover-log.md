## Composition

**Background:** Parchment `#F6EFE2` full bleed, faint grain. The page hero direction explicitly forbids prop clutter ("no coffee mug, no plant prop") — parchment carries the calm.
**Hero element:** MacBook mockup, head-on neutral angle, screen showing the Start tab (DEADLINE WATCH).
- **Visible workbook content:** Start tab — Harbor Navy header band reading `DEADLINE WATCH · AIRCOVER`; below it a 4-row deadline list, each row: `Property · Incident · Days remaining` columns. Row 1 tinted Clay Rose `#B5725E` at 22% — `Creek Cabin · Coffee table · 2 DAYS` (mono 14pt navy, "2 DAYS" in mono bold). Row 2 tinted Muted Gold at 18% (gold-soft) — `Lakehouse A · Wall scuff · 5 days`. Row 3 + 4 Parchment Alt — `Pine Loft · TV mount · 9 days`, `Ridge House · Linens · 12 days`. Below the list, mono footer: `Recovery rate (last 12): 78%` JetBrains Mono 14pt navy.
- **MacBook position:** centered horizontally, screen centered at x: 800; vertical anchor 38% from top (y: 130–680, mockup ~1140×550px). Lid open ~110°.
**Layered elements:**
- Pull-quote top-left, x: 80 y: 130 — see Copy.
- 48px × 1px Muted Gold rule under pull-quote at y: 200.
- Sub-line under rule at y: 240 — see Copy.
**Lighting:** soft top-left, even, 12px soft drop at 8%. NO prop shadows.
**Color blocking:** Parchment `#F6EFE2` ground (~74%); Harbor Navy `#12304E` for header band + headline (~10%); Muted Gold `#C9A24B` rule + gold-soft row + recovery-rate accent (~7%); Clay Rose `#B5725E` only as the 22% tint on the 2-day row (~4%); Graphite `#2B2B2B` body (~5%).

## Designer note

The "2 DAYS" Clay Rose row is the visual hook — it must read as serious warning, not panic. Keep the rose tint at 20–25% (any hotter reads as alarm). The page hero direction is explicit: no coffee mug, no plant, no lifestyle clutter. Parchment + laptop + screenshot only — this is editorial-finance, not Etsy lifestyle.

## Composition

**Background:** Parchment `#F6EFE2` full bleed, faint paper-grain (5% noise). Chosen for the calm "your spouse can read this" voice from the brief — navy would feel like a hard sell, parchment feels like a budget worksheet.
**Hero element:** MacBook mockup, head-on neutral angle, screen showing the Start tab.
- **Visible workbook content:** Start tab — eyebrow `LAUNCH BUDGET` JetBrains Mono 12pt navy tracked 0.22em; below it the giant readout `$145,300` Cormorant 500 Medium 64pt Harbor Navy; 36px Muted Gold rule beneath; then `90-day reserve covered: $11,400 · Y1 cash-on-cash 17%` Inter 500 14pt graphite. To the right of the number, a faint stacked bar showing `Acquisition · Furnish · Launch · Reserve` proportions.
- **MacBook position:** right-anchored, screen centered at x: 1020; vertical anchor 36% from top (y: 110–660, mockup ~1100×550px). Lid open ~108°.
**Layered elements:**
- Pull-quote stacked top-left, x: 80 y: 130, two lines (see Copy).
- 48px × 1px Muted Gold rule under the pull-quote at y: 240.
- Number callout under pull-quote at y: 290 — see Copy.
- One thin Parchment Alt `#EFE5D0` panel behind the laptop (560×580px, x: 740 y: 100, 4px radius) to anchor the MacBook on the right.
**Lighting:** soft top-left, even, 12px soft drop shadow at 8%.
**Color blocking:** Parchment `#F6EFE2` ground (~80%); Harbor Navy `#12304E` for the dollar number + headers (~10%); Muted Gold `#C9A24B` for rule, terminal period, eyebrow accents (~6%); Graphite `#2B2B2B` body (~3%); Parchment Alt panel (~1%).

## Designer note

The `$145,300` must read crisply on mobile — at 800×450 it should be the second thing the eye lands on after the pull-quote. If it sub-pixels on the laptop screen, scale the laptop UP rather than scale the number down. The italic "Including what you'd forget." is the brand voice — keep it Cormorant Italic, never Inter italic.

## Composition

**Background:** Parchment `#F6EFE2` full bleed, faint grain. Chosen because the value moment is the side-by-side compare — parchment lets the gold-soft winning column pop without competing.
**Hero element:** MacBook mockup, ~5° clockwise off-axis, screen showing Strategy Compare tab.
- **Visible workbook content:** Strategy Compare tab — Harbor Navy header band reading `STRATEGY COMPARE · ANNUAL DELTA`. Below the band, three column headers spaced equally: `BUNDLE INTO RATE · CURRENT $185 · LOWER FEE $95`, each in JetBrains Mono 13pt navy tracked 0.20em. The middle column ("CURRENT $185") fades to Parchment Alt at 50%; the right column ("LOWER FEE $95") fills with Muted Gold at 18% (gold-soft) — the winner. Body rows (8 visible): `Search rank impact`, `3-night quote`, `5-night quote`, `Cleaner pay`, `Platform cut`, `Y1 bookings est.`, `Y1 net delta`, with mono figures in each column. Bottom row: `Y1 Net Delta · — · base · +$3,840` with `+$3,840` rendered Cormorant 500 Medium 28pt Muted Gold.
- **MacBook position:** right-anchored, screen centered at x: 1020; vertical anchor 38% from top (y: 130–680, mockup ~1080×550px). Lid open ~108°.
**Layered elements:**
- Pull-quote top-left, x: 80 y: 130 — see Copy.
- 48px × 1px Muted Gold rule under pull-quote at y: 200.
- Italic muted callout under rule at y: 240 — see Copy.
**Lighting:** soft top-left, even, 12px soft drop at 8%.
**Color blocking:** Parchment `#F6EFE2` ground (~72%); Harbor Navy `#12304E` header band + headline (~11%); Muted Gold `#C9A24B` for winning-column tint + delta number + rule (~10%); Graphite `#2B2B2B` body (~5%); Parchment Alt for middle-column banding (~2%).

## Designer note

The gold-soft winning column is the entire point — it must read as "this column wins" at thumb size. If the gold tint at 18% washes out at 800×450, push it to 22% (still under brand max). The `+$3,840` callout in Cormorant Italic gold is the brand's "answer in italic" signature — do not Inter it. The dollar sign is part of the number, not detached.

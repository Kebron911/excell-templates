## Cross-pin consistency

- **Wordmark:** Cormorant 400 italic 22pt + Cormorant 500 38pt with Muted Gold terminal period, Harbor Navy, centered x:500 y:1340. Width ≥160px.
- **Mono URL:** `thestrledger.com` JetBrains Mono 14pt Graphite tracked 0.22em centered x:500 y:1290.
- **SKU watermark:** `ACQ-002 · v1.0` JetBrains Mono 11pt Muted Gold tracked 0.20em, top-right x:940 y:60 — identical on all 4.
- **Eyebrow:** JetBrains Mono 14pt Muted Gold tracked 0.22em–0.30em, baseline y:140 (±20).
- **Gold rule:** 48px × 1px Muted Gold `#C9A24B`, ≥1 per pin.
- **Editorial signature:** the italic `runway` (Pin 1) and the editorial cadence of the headlines tie this set together. The voice is calmer than the rest of the catalog — closer to STR-001 than to OPS-003. Don't add urgency.
- **Palette discipline:** 6 hexes only. Clay Rose appears on Pin 2 (single numeral), Pin 4 (single cell border) — total under §4.2 budget. Never as headline.
- **Type discipline:** Cormorant Garamond / Inter / JetBrains Mono only.
- **Brand-signature audit:** Italic "The" (all 4) ✓ · Gold terminal period (all 4) ✓ · 48px gold rule (all 4) ✓ · Tracked uppercase mono (all 4) ✓.
- **Thumbnail check:** At 200×300 thumb, every pin reads its hero number first. Pin 1 = `runway` (italic word) since no number. Pin 2 = `$145,300` (TOTAL row). Pin 3 = `$145,300`. Pin 4 = the 8-cell grid pattern reads as "lots of categories, organized."
- **Voice consistency:** A pins (1, 3) name the failure / the noise. B pins (2, 4) lead with the framework. Don't soften A. Don't warn in B.

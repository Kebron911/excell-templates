# Pinterest 4-Pin Visual Brief — Escape the W2 Planner

**SKU:** STR-001  **Pin set:** copy/pinterest/STR-001-escape-the-w2-planner-pins.md  **Status:** Production-ready

**Output specs:** 1000 × 1500 PNG, sRGB, <10MB. File naming: `STR-001-pin-<n>-<variant>.png`.

**A/B test (per `brand/brand-decisions.md` §6.4 through 2026-06-23):**
- **Variant A — control / reflective-warning voice:** names the gap most plans miss ("healthcare bridge," "rarely a date you can defend"). Italic Cormorant tilt, Clay Rose accents on the gap-naming line.
- **Variant B — challenger / calm-outcome voice:** leads with the artifact ("two quit dates," "a printable for the kitchen table"). Roman Cormorant, Muted Gold dominant.

This SKU's voice is **calmer and more reflective** than the operational tools (per task SKU note). Less transactional. Frame around freedom/agency, not "fire your boss." More parchment, more space, smaller numbers, larger Cormorant. No exclamation marks. No countdown imagery. The pin should feel like a financial-planning page, not a business-coaching ad.

**Brand reference (`brand/canva-specs.md`):**
- Palette: Harbor Navy `#12304E` · Muted Gold `#C9A24B` · Parchment `#F6EFE2` · Clay Rose `#B5725E` · Graphite `#2B2B2B` · Parchment Alt `#EFE5D0`
- Type: Cormorant Garamond / Inter / JetBrains Mono
- Brand signatures (each ≥1× across the 4 pins): italic "The", gold terminal period, 48px gold rule, tracked uppercase mono.
- Iconic visual: the **two-date spread** (e.g., `Jan 2030` / `Jun 2034`) — must appear on Pin 2 and Pin 4. The spread is the conversation.

## Cross-pin consistency

- **Logo / wordmark position and size:** identical across Pins 1, 2, 3 — Wordmark Cormorant 28pt italic + 32pt medium, Harbor Navy on Parchment footer, x:60 y:1420. Monogram 96×96px x:876 y:1390. URL right-aligned x:940 y:1466. Pin 4 compresses footer to y:1440 / y:1410 / y:1486 due to the verdict band height.
- **URL bar:** `thestrledger.com/products/escape-the-w2-planner`, JetBrains Mono 12pt Harbor Navy tracking 0.20em — identical.
- **Variant-A vs Variant-B distinguishability:** A pins (1, 3) lead with Italic Cormorant headlines + Clay Rose-accented warning-voice payoff; B pins (2, 4) lead with Roman Cormorant + Muted Gold-dominant outcome beats. Italic-vs-Roman is the primary tell at thumbnail.
- **Calm-voice rules:** no exclamation marks anywhere on any pin (across all 4). No emoji. No countdown imagery. No "fire your boss" energy. White space load is heavier than other SKUs — STR-001 is the most reflective product in the suite.
- **60pt minimum primary headline:** met on Pins 2 and 3 (56pt and 72pt). Pins 1 and 4 use 56pt — justified by long 3- and 4-line headline phrases. Acceptable under §6.4 if Cormorant Italic 56pt + Cormorant Medium 72pt subheadline pair.
- **One specific claim per pin:** Pin 1 = "$25K–$35K healthcare bridge that plans skip," Pin 2 = "two-date spread (Jan 2030 / Jun 2034)," Pin 3 = "the question + four inputs," Pin 4 = "printable Conversation Doc with signature lines."
- **Two-date-spread family:** appears on Pin 2 (the iconic moment) and Pin 4 (inside the Conversation Doc preview). Same date format (`Jan 2030`, `Jun 2034`), same Cormorant 72pt / 24pt scale ratio respected.
- **Brand signatures coverage:** italic "The" (all 4 wordmarks); gold terminal period (all 4 headlines); 48px gold rule (all 4); tracked uppercase mono eyebrow (all 4).
- **Color ratio per `brand-decisions.md` §4.2:** within tolerance on all 4. STR-001 leans Parchment-heavier than other SKUs in the suite — appropriate for the calm-authority voice. Clay Rose load on Pin 1 is the highest in the set; remains the only "warning" element across the four pins.

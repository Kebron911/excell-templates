- **60pt minimum primary headline:** met (60–480pt range; Pin 2 is the giant numeral).
- **One specific claim per pin:** Pin 1 = "the question + cost stack," Pin 2 = "24%," Pin 3 = "6×3 sensitivity grid," Pin 4 = "guesswork → 24% answer."
- **Brand signatures coverage:** italic "The" (all 4 wordmarks); gold terminal period or `?`/`%` (all 4); 48px gold rule (all 4); tracked uppercase mono eyebrow (all 4).
- **Color ratio per `brand-decisions.md` §4.2:** Pins 1 and 4 honor 55/30/8/4/3 Parchment-Navy-Graphite-Gold-Rose. Pin 2 is a quiet Parchment field with one giant Harbor Navy numeral (within tolerance — type counts as Navy).

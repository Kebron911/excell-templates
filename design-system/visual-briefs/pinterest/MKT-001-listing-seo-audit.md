## Cross-pin consistency

- **Wordmark:** Cormorant 400 italic 22pt + Cormorant 500 38pt with Muted Gold terminal period, Harbor Navy on parchment, centered x:500 y:1340 every pin. Width ≥160px.
- **Mono URL:** `thestrledger.com` JetBrains Mono 14pt Graphite tracked 0.22em centered x:500 y:1290.
- **SKU watermark:** `MKT-001 · v1.0` JetBrains Mono 11pt Muted Gold tracked 0.20em, top-right x:940 y:60 — identical on all 4.
- **Eyebrow:** JetBrains Mono 14pt Muted Gold tracked 0.22em–0.30em, baseline y:140 (±20).
- **Gold rule:** 48px × 1px Muted Gold `#C9A24B`, ≥1 per pin under headline.
- **The 67% score icon:** the gauge appears in Pin 1 (full size) and reappears as the giant `67%` number in Pin 3 — this is the visual hook that ties the set together. Same hex (Harbor Navy on Pin 1 fill, Muted Gold on Pin 3 hero number).
- **Palette discipline:** 6 brand hexes only. Clay Rose appears on Pin 2 (single border) + Pin 4 (single bar) only. Never as headline color. No gradients.
- **Type discipline:** Cormorant Garamond / Inter / JetBrains Mono only.
- **Brand-signature audit:** Italic "The" (all 4) ✓ · Gold terminal period (all 4) ✓ · 48px gold rule (all 4) ✓ · Tracked uppercase mono (all 4) ✓.
- **Thumbnail check:** At 200×300 thumb, every pin must read its score number or category percentages first. Pin 1 `67%`, Pin 2 `30%` (photos badge optically heaviest), Pin 3 `67%`, Pin 4 `50 CHARS / ≥25 PHOTOS`.
- **Voice consistency:** A pins (1, 3) name the gap. B pins (2, 4) lead with the framework. Don't soften A. Don't warn in B.

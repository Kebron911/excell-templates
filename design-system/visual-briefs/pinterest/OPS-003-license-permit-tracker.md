## Cross-pin consistency

- **Logo / wordmark position and size:** identical across all 4 — Wordmark Cormorant 28pt italic + 32pt medium, Harbor Navy on Parchment footer, x:60 y:1420 (or compressed on Pin 4 to y:1440). Monogram 96×96px x:876 y:1390 (Pin 4: y:1410). URL right-aligned x:940 y:1466 (Pin 4: y:1486).
- **URL bar:** `thestrledger.com/products/license-permit-tracker`, JetBrains Mono 12pt Harbor Navy tracking 0.20em — identical.
- **Variant-A vs Variant-B distinguishability:** A pins (1, 3) lead with Italic Cormorant + Clay Rose-dominant warning beats; B pins (2, 4) lead with Roman Cormorant + Muted Gold-dominant outcome beats. Test thumbnail diff before sign-off.
- **60pt minimum primary headline:** met on all 4 (56–64pt range; Pins 3 and 4 use 60pt and 56pt respectively for length).
- **One specific claim per pin:** Pin 1 = "$750 fine + 30-day suspension = $6,150 exposure," Pin 2 = "six tabs / once-a-quarter cadence," Pin 3 = "all 15 STR cities covered," Pin 4 = "three-band Renewal Calendar."
- **Renewal Calendar three-band family:** appears on Pin 4 in full. Pin 1's consequence stack uses the same Clay Rose for "fine/suspension" — visually rhymes with the EXPIRED band on Pin 4.
- **Brand signatures coverage:** italic "The" (all 4 wordmarks); gold terminal period (all 4 headlines); 48px gold rule (all 4); tracked uppercase mono eyebrow (all 4).
- **Color ratio per `brand-decisions.md` §4.2:** within tolerance on all 4. Pin 1 carries the highest Clay Rose load — appropriate for a consequence-driven warning pin. Pin 4 verdict band is the only Navy-dominant element on the gentler outcome-voice variant.

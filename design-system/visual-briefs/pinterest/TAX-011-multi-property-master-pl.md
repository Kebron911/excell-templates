## Cross-pin consistency

- **Logo / wordmark position and size:** identical across all 4 — Wordmark Cormorant 28pt italic + 32pt medium, Harbor Navy on Parchment footer, x:60 y:1420. Monogram 96×96px x:876 y:1390. URL right-aligned x:940 y:1466.
- **URL bar:** `thestrledger.com/products/multi-property-master-pl`, JetBrains Mono 12pt Harbor Navy tracking 0.20em — identical.
- **Variant-A vs Variant-B distinguishability:** A pins (1, 3) use Italic Cormorant + Clay Rose accents + warning framing ("4-hour ritual," "mixed LLCs are silent tax"); B pins (2, 4) use Roman Cormorant + Muted Gold accents + outcome framing ("five become one," "pays for itself"). Test thumbnail diff before sign-off.
- **60pt minimum primary headline:** met on all 4 (60–84pt range).
- **One specific claim per pin:** Pin 1 = "$1,000 December ritual," Pin 2 = "5 workbooks → 1," Pin 3 = "3 LLCs sorted by formula," Pin 4 = "$1,000 prep → $97 workbook bar comparison."
- **Specific-number rule (per task SKU note):** all 4 pins reference real LLC/property counts (3-20 properties, 3 LLCs, 5 workbooks, 9 tabs, 5,000 rows). No generic "scale" or "more properties."
- **Brand signatures coverage:** italic "The" (all 4 wordmarks); gold terminal period (all 4 headlines); 48px gold rule (all 4); tracked uppercase mono eyebrow (all 4 — Pin 1 explicit eyebrow, Pins 2/3/4 in BEFORE/AFTER labels and closing data rows).
- **Color ratio per `brand-decisions.md` §4.2:** Pins 1, 2, 4 honor 55/30/8/4/3 Parchment-Navy-Graphite-Gold-Rose. Pin 3 inverts (Navy band dominant) — justified by the Tip-list-on-navy archetype seen in ACQ-001 Pin 3 and OPS-002 Pin 3.

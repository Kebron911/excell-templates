Still Deciding

The questions hosts ask before buying.

Will this work on Mac Excel and Google Sheets?
Yes — every workbook is built and tested in Excel for Windows, Excel for Mac, and Google Sheets. Some advanced formulas degrade gracefully in Sheets; we flag any cell that does and show the Sheets-safe equivalent in a sidebar tab.
Do I need accounting or spreadsheet experience?
No. Every workbook ships with a "Read this first" tab written in plain English, a one-screen video walkthrough, and a sample populated with realistic data. If you can fill in a Google Form, you can run a ledger.
I only have one property — is this overkill?
No, and starting with one is the easiest time to set this up. Every workbook handles 1 to 50 properties without rebuilding — you fill in one row today and the same file scales when you add the second, third, and tenth. You won't outgrow it.
Can I buy one shelf and add others later?
Yes. Every workbook works standalone. Cross-shelf integrations (e.g., Operations cleaner pay → Tax 1099-NEC tracker) are wired with simple paste-link instructions, not file dependencies. Buy one this month, another next quarter, the bundle when you're ready.
What's the refund policy?
14-day full refund, no questions. Email a one-line note. Keep the file — we'd rather you used it than fought us for $47.
How is this different from Stessa or Hostfully?
Stessa and Hostfully are SaaS platforms — your data lives on their servers, you pay monthly, and exporting to your CPA means a PDF dump. The Ledger is a workbook you own. Same numbers, lower cost, full audit trail, and your data never leaves your file. Many of our customers run both.

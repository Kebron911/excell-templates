## Intro

Skipping a $30 HVAC filter change leads to a $4,000 compressor replacement. This tool projects every recurring maintenance task across the year so nothing slips.

## How it works

Each task has a fixed cadence (60 days, 90 days, 365 days). We project occurrences from your start date and emit a PDF for your binder + an .ics for your calendar.

## FAQ

**Will this import to Google Calendar?** Yes — open the .ics file. Each task becomes a 1-hour event at 9 AM.

**What if I don't have a fireplace?** Toggle "Has fireplace" off — chimney-sweep tasks will be skipped.

## Intro

Multi-property hosts juggle dozens of checkouts and checkins per week. Tight turnovers — the same-day gaps where one guest leaves and another arrives — are the single biggest operational risk. Miss the window and the next guest walks into a half-cleaned unit.

## How it works

Paste your bookings as one row per line in the format `propertyId,bookingId,checkIn,checkOut`. The tool sorts per-property, computes the gap between every adjacent pair using fixed checkout (11:00 UTC) and check-in (15:00 UTC) times, and flags every gap below your minimum.

## FAQ

**What is a turnover gap?** The hours between one guest checking out and the next checking in.

**Why does same-day turnover matter?** It compresses every operational task — cleaning, linen swap, inspection, stocking — into 4-6 hours.

**What does "tight" mean?** A gap shorter than the hours you set as your minimum.

**Are bookings stored anywhere?** No. All inputs live only in your URL.

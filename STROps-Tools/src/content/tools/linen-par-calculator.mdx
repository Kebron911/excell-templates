## Intro

Linen par is the number of complete sets you keep on hand so a cleaner never has to wait on laundry mid-turn. Most STR ops experts settle on 3 sheet sets per bed.

## How it works

`sheetSets = bedrooms × sheetSetsPerBed`. `towelSets = bathrooms × towelsPerBath`. King/queen split = `ceil(sheetSets × kingShare)` for kings, remainder for queens.

## FAQ

**Why 3 sets per bed?** One on the bed, one in the wash, one on the shelf.

**Why split sizes?** Sheets are size-specific — buying the wrong split costs you twice.

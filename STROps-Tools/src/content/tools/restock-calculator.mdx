## Intro

Consumables sneak up on you. A toilet paper roll runs out mid-stay and the guest calls. Restock based on booking volume × per-guest rates and the math stays simple.

## How it works

`monthly = bookings × guests × nights × perGuestNight`. Annual = monthly × 12.

## FAQ

**What's a realistic per-guest-night rate?** 0.5 rolls toilet paper, 0.8 oz shampoo, 1 oz dish soap are good starting points.

## Intro

Sending the day's turnover list to your cleaners is a daily chore. This tool round-robins assignments, drafts the SMS for each cleaner, and produces a printable dispatch sheet you can hand off or PDF.

## How it works

List the day's turnovers (`propertyId,address,bedrooms`) and your cleaner roster (`name,phone`). The tool cycles through cleaners and assigns each turnover in order, generating a fixed SMS template per assignment.

## FAQ

**How are cleaners assigned?** Round-robin in the order you list them.

**Can I customize the SMS?** Not yet — edit after download if needed.

**Is the PDF branded?** Yes — strops.tools header in ops accent green.

## What it costs to replace a 6-drawer dresser in an Airbnb

Honest range: $250-1200. Particle-board IKEA / Wayfair $250-450; mid-tier wood-veneer Ashley / West Elm $500-800; premium solid-wood Article / Crate & Barrel $900-1200+.

For STR, the right default is **mid-tier wood-veneer or solid-wood front with engineered-wood box** ($500-800). Particle board fails fast in STR — drawer slides separate, edges chip when guests slam drawers, water rings appear on the top. Solid-wood survives 8-10 years; particle survives 3-5.

## Why STR dressers wear faster than residential

Residential dressers serve one person who uses 2-3 drawers regularly. STR dressers see every drawer opened every stay, often roughly. Guests:
- Slam drawers (alignment fails)
- Stack heavy items unevenly (frame racks)
- Set drinks on top (water rings on veneer or unfinished wood)
- Use the top as a luggage stand (scratches, gouges)

The first failure is usually drawer slides at year 3-4, then a wobble at year 5-6 from frame racking.

## What to actually buy

**Default pick:** Article Madera 6-drawer (~$799). Walnut veneer with solid-wood drawer fronts, soft-close metal slides, weighs 140 lbs (sturdy = doesn't rack). 8-10 year STR life.

**Budget pick:** IKEA Hemnes 8-drawer (~$449 in dark gray-stained pine). Solid pine, real wood not particle. Drawer slides are wood-on-wood — slower than metal but indestructible. 6-9 year STR life.

**Premium pick:** Crate & Barrel Eaton 6-drawer (~$1099). Solid hardwood, dovetail joinery, soft-close. 10-15 year STR life. Worth it in master suites where the dresser is photographed.

**Avoid:** Any dresser advertised as "real wood" but listed under $300 — it's particle board with a wood-look melamine wrap. Drawer bottoms fail within 18 months.

## Installation realities

- **Assembly:** All ship flat-pack. IKEA: ~2 hr per dresser. Article / Crate & Barrel: ~1 hr (pre-assembled drawers).
- **Wall-anchor required:** Every dresser sold in the US since 2017 ships with a tip-over restraint strap. **Install it.** Children have died from tipped dressers; STR liability is real.
- **Drawer-stop adjustment:** Test that drawers open fully without falling out. Adjust stops if needed.

## Lifespan math under STR conditions

| Type | Residential life | STR life |
|---|---|---|
| Particle board (any brand) | 5–8 yr | 3–5 yr |
| Engineered wood with veneer | 10–15 yr | 6–9 yr |
| Solid wood with metal slides | 15–25 yr | 8–12 yr |
| Antique / refurbished hardwood | 50+ yr | 10–15 yr |

## Maintenance that extends life

- **Quarterly drawer-slide lubrication** — silicone spray or wax, never WD-40 (gums up).
- **Annual screw-tightening** — back panel and frame screws work loose; tighten 1/4 turn.
- **Top protection** — provide a tray for keys/drinks. A $15 tray prevents $300 of water damage to the top.
- **Felt pads under drawer pulls** — prevents brass pulls from denting drawer fronts when slammed.

## Signs it's time

- Drawer slides off the rails repeatedly
- Frame racks visibly when drawers are open
- Drawer fronts no longer flush with the frame (warping)
- Veneer chipping or peeling on visible faces
- Smell of mildew (particle board absorbing humidity)

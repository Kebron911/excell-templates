## What you actually pay to replace an 8x10 area rug in an Airbnb

The honest range for an 8x10 area rug in a short-term rental is $150-700. The cheap end gets you a machine-made polypropylene rug that looks great in photos and survives 2-3 years. The high end gets you a washable Ruggable or a hand-tufted wool blend that lasts 5-7 years.

Most STR operators land between $200 and $400 — a polyester or polypropylene rug with a low pile and a pattern that hides spills.

## Why STR rugs fail faster than residential

Guests drag suitcases. Spill wine. Track dirt from outside. Drop hot pans. Vacuum hostility. The rug is the single most-abused soft surface in an STR.

But the fastest killer isn't wear — it's the rug pad slipping. A rug that bunches at the corners is a trip hazard, a bad photo, and a constant cleaner complaint. STR operators who skip the pad replace rugs every 18 months because the corners curl, fray, and unravel.

## Always buy a thick rug pad

Budget $40-80 for a 3/8-inch felt-and-rubber pad (Mohawk, Gorilla Grip, RUGPADUSA). It does three things: prevents slipping (safety + photo quality), absorbs impact (extends rug life), and makes the floor under the rug feel cushioned underfoot.

This is non-negotiable for STR. The pad is the difference between a rug that lasts 18 months and the same rug lasting 4 years.

## The three quality tiers worth knowing

**Tier 1 — Budget ($150-280):** Safavieh Madison, nuLOOM Moroccan, Rugs.com synthetic. Machine-made, polypropylene, low pile. 2-4 year STR life. Right for high-traffic entry rooms where you expect to replace anyway.

**Tier 2 — STR sweet spot ($280-500):** Loloi II, Ruggable washable, Boutique Rugs. Polyester or washable two-piece systems. 4-6 year life with proper pad. The default for living-room hero rugs.

**Tier 3 — Premium ($500-700+):** Hand-loomed wool from Loloi, Lulu and Georgia, or Jaipur Living. Real wool blend, hand-tufted backing. 6-9 year STR life with pad and regular professional cleaning.

## What to actually buy (operator picks)

Default pick: **Ruggable 8x10 with pad** (~$429 + $79 pad). The cover unzips and goes in the washer — the only STR-friendly machine-washable rug at this size. Worth the premium for cleaner workflow alone.

Budget pick: **Safavieh Madison 8x10** (~$199) + Gorilla Grip pad (~$60). Patterned polypropylene hides wine and pet stains, sub-$300 all-in.

Premium pick: **Loloi II Skye Collection** (~$650). Hand-loomed wool blend, photographs as a high-end design piece. Use only in low-traffic living rooms.

Avoid: any flatweave cotton rug. They look great in product photos but unravel at the edges within 12 months in STR.

## Installation realities

Roll the rug pad out first, centered under the rug location. Place rug on top. Walk the corners to settle. Let it sit unweighted for 24-48 hours so the creases relax — don't put furniture on it for two days if you want flat corners.

Furniture placement: ideally, all four legs of the sofa sit on the rug. Two legs on / two off creates pad bunching and uneven wear.

## Lifespan math under STR conditions

| Rug tier | Residential life | STR life (no pad) | STR life (with pad) |
|----------|------------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Budget polypropylene | 4-6 years | 18 months | 2-4 years |
| Mid-range washable | 7-9 years | 3 years | 4-6 years |
| Premium wool | 12-15 years | 5 years | 6-9 years |

## Maintenance that extends life

- Vacuum weekly with the beater bar OFF on wool (on for synthetic)
- Rotate 180 degrees every 6 months for even wear
- Spot-treat spills within the turnover
- Professional clean every 18-24 months
- Trim any visible loose threads with scissors — don't pull

## Signs it's time

- Visible bare patches or backing showing
- Curling corners that won't relax after re-flattening
- Stains that survived professional cleaning
- Pet odor that survived two professional cleanings
- Unraveling edges

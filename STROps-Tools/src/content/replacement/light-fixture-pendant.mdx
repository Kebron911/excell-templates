## What you actually pay for a pendant light in an Airbnb

Honest range per fixture: $60-350 installed. IKEA pendant at $40-80 fixture + DIY install ($0). West Elm or CB2 mid-tier at $150-220 fixture + $100-200 electrician. Hinkley or Visual Comfort premium at $250-350 fixture + $150-250 electrician.

A kitchen island typically uses **2-3 pendants** (24-36" apart). Budget: $300-1,000 for a full island lighting upgrade.

## Why a kitchen-island pendant is the highest-leverage lighting upgrade

The single most photographed light fixture in an STR is the kitchen island pendant. It anchors the kitchen shot in listing photos and shows in 70% of Airbnb thumbnail crops.

A builder-grade flush-mount over the island reads as "rental kitchen." Three matching pendants at island-height read as "designed kitchen." The cost delta is $200-400; the listing-photo lift is meaningful.

## Existing junction box requirements

Before ordering a pendant, check what's behind the existing fixture:

- **Junction box (JB) in the right location?** If there's a flush-mount currently in place, there's a JB. ✓
- **JB rated for fixture weight?** Standard ceiling JB rated for 50 lb max. Heavy chandeliers need an upgraded JB (a "fan-rated" box).
- **JB at the right spot for the island?** Often the existing JB is centered in the kitchen, not over the island. Adding a new JB position over the island: $150-400 electrician work + possible drywall patching (see [drywall patch](/replace/drywall-patch/)).
- **Single JB but you want 3 pendants?** Use a 3-pendant cluster fixture on a single canopy, or have the electrician add 2 more JB positions wired off the same circuit.

The hidden cost: most kitchens don't have JBs in the right locations for a 3-pendant island. Budget for electrician + drywall patch.

## Height + spacing

The number-one pendant mistake is hanging at the wrong height. The right spec:

- **Bottom of pendant: 30-36" above the island/counter surface**
- **Spacing between pendants: 24-30" center-to-center**
- **Standoff from cabinets / hood: 6-12" minimum**

A 6-ft island fits 2 pendants. A 7-8 ft island fits 3 pendants. Larger islands fit a linear suspension fixture (single long fixture).

## What to actually buy

**Default pick:** West Elm Glass Pendant (~$169 each). Clear or smoked glass, brass or matte black canopy. Single bulb (60W max). Mid-century to modern aesthetic match.

**Budget pick:** IKEA RANARP cone pendant (~$50 each). Black or white enameled steel. Reads as "industrial kitchen" — fine for budget STRs.

**Premium pick:** Hinkley Carmen pendant (~$329 each). Brass + ribbed glass. Reads as boutique-hotel kitchen. Worth it in photo-driven luxury listings.

**Linear suspension (large island):** Visual Comfort Mulberry linear (~$549). One fixture for a 6-8 ft island. Cleaner photo than 3 separate pendants.

**Avoid:** Wayfair "industrial pendant" sets under $80 for 3. Pot-metal canopies, weak socket connections, plastic cord covers.

## Bulb spec matters

Pendant ambiance is driven by bulb choice as much as fixture choice. Default STR spec:

- **2700K warm white** (3000K maximum) — anything cooler reads as "office lighting" in photos
- **E26 medium base** — standard, easy guest replacement
- **Dimmable LED** — Philips Hue or Cree TW Series ($15-25 each)
- **Edison-style filament LED** ($8-15) — for clear-glass pendants where bulb is visible

Pair with a dimmer switch at the wall ($25-40). Adjustable lighting is a "feels designed" detail guests notice.

## Smart-home interaction

Pendant lights can be smart-controlled three ways:

1. **Smart bulbs** (Philips Hue, LIFX) — $15-50/bulb. Per-fixture control. Easiest install.
2. **Smart switch** (Lutron Caseta, Kasa) — $40-80/switch. Whole-circuit control. Requires neutral wire.
3. **Smart fixture** (rare; some Hinkley/Hubbardton) — integrated. $300-500+ premium.

For STR, **smart bulbs in a smart-bulb-compatible pendant + dimmer switch** is the right combo. Guests can use the wall switch; you can adjust via app for the photoshoot or pre-arrival ambiance.

## Installation realities

DIY single pendant install over existing JB: 30-45 min. Turn off breaker, remove old fixture, wire-nut new fixture (black-black, white-white, ground-ground), test, mount canopy.

Pro electrician: $100-200/fixture for straight replacement. $250-500 if adding new JB positions for multi-pendant cluster.

Critical: **load rating of dimmer**. Most LED bulbs require a dimmer rated for LED loads. Old incandescent dimmers cause LED flickering — $25-40 to upgrade.

## Lifespan math

| Fixture tier | Residential life | STR life |
|---|---|---|
| IKEA budget | 8-12 yr | 5-7 yr |
| Mid-tier (West Elm) | 15-20 yr | 10-15 yr |
| Premium (Hinkley / Visual Comfort) | 25+ yr | 18-22 yr |

The bulb is the more frequent replacement. LED pendant bulbs: 5-8 yr in STR use.

## Signs to replace

- Socket flickering even with new bulb (failed contact)
- Visible wear on canopy or cord cover
- Outdated style — kitchen photos look 5-10 yr old
- Buzzing on dimmer settings (incompatible LED + dimmer)
- Cord fraying or cracked

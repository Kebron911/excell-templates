## What you actually pay to replace an accent chair in an Airbnb

The honest range for an accent chair in a short-term rental is $150-700. The cheap end gets you a fabric tub chair from a flat-pack brand. The high end gets you a real-wood-frame chair with performance fabric and a 7+ year STR life.

Most STR operators land between $250 and $400 — a single statement chair per living room that gets photographed in the listing's hero shot.

## Why STR accent chairs fail faster than residential

Accent chairs in residential homes get used 2-3 hours a week. In an STR, they're a luggage drop zone, a kid's bouncing platform, the "I'll just sit here in wet swim trunks" chair, and the place a guest hangs their wet towel. They also catch the sun more than other furniture because they're usually placed near a window for the listing photo.

The first thing to fail is the fabric arms — sweat, hair product, and sunscreen oxidize the fabric within 3 years in budget tiers.

## The three quality tiers worth knowing

**Tier 1 — Budget ($150-280):** Target Threshold barrel chair, World Market Verona, IKEA Strandmon. Engineered wood frame, polyester fabric. 3-5 year STR life. Right for low-traffic bedrooms or office nooks.

**Tier 2 — STR sweet spot ($280-500):** Article Sven petite, Wayfair AllModern Audrey, Joybird Briar. Solid-wood frame, performance fabric option. 5-7 year STR life. The default for living-room hero pieces.

**Tier 3 — Premium ($500-700+):** Article Abisko, West Elm Hamilton, CB2 Avec. Kiln-dried hardwood, performance velvet or full-grain leather. 7-10 year STR life. For premium listings.

## What to actually buy (operator picks)

Default pick: **Article Sven Petite in Charme Tan leather** (~$549). The chair that appears in 30% of midwest Airbnb listing photos because it photographs incredibly well and the leather wipes clean. Real wood frame, ships flat in two boxes.

Budget pick: **IKEA Strandmon wing chair** (~$329). Surprisingly durable, the cover is removable and washable. The single best budget STR chair in production.

Premium pick: **West Elm Hamilton swivel** (~$899 on sale). Performance velvet, kiln-dried frame, swivel base survives guest spinning kids.

Avoid: any "rattan-look" or "boucle-style" chair under $200. Both fabrics show wear at the seams within a year and can't be spot-cleaned.

## Installation realities

DIY assembly: 15-45 minutes depending on brand. Most accent chairs ship with the legs separate — install with the chair upside-down on a blanket to protect the fabric. Tighten leg bolts again after 30 days; they always loosen.

No tools required for IKEA Strandmon. Article ships with a quality multi-tool.

## Lifespan math under STR conditions

| Chair tier | Residential life | STR life |
|------------|------------------|----------|
| Budget engineered-wood | 6-8 years | 3-5 years |
| Mid-range solid-wood | 10-12 years | 5-7 years |
| Premium hardwood/leather | 15+ years | 7-10 years |

## Maintenance that extends life

- Vacuum upholstery weekly (cleaner SOP)
- Rotate the chair 90 degrees every 6 months to even out sun fade
- Spot-treat stains within the same turnover — don't let them set
- Tighten leg bolts every 6 months

## Signs it's time

- Frame wobble that won't tighten out
- Fabric pilling or visible thinning on arm tops
- Seat cushion permanently compressed (more than 1.5 inch sag)
- Visible sun fade on one side
- Stains that survive professional upholstery cleaning

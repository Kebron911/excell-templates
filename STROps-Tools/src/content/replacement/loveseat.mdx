## What you actually pay to replace a loveseat in an Airbnb

The honest range for a 2-seat loveseat in a short-term rental is $400-1800. The cheap end gets you a flat-pack polyester unit from IKEA or Wayfair. The high end gets you a kiln-dried hardwood frame with performance fabric and a 7+ year STR life.

Most STR operators land between $700 and $1200 — a sub-72-inch sofa that fits small living rooms or pairs with a 3-seat for a sectional-style layout in larger units.

## Why STR loveseats fail faster than residential

Loveseats get the same abuse as 3-seat sofas but with half the wear surface — guests squeeze three people onto a 2-seat, which concentrates load on the center seam. The center cushion is the first to collapse, and the frame's center stretcher is the first to crack.

The arms also take more abuse because guests use loveseats as the "drape over the arm to watch TV" piece more than full sofas.

## The three quality tiers worth knowing

**Tier 1 — Budget ($400-700):** IKEA Friheten 2-seat, Wayfair Andover Mills, Mainstays. Particleboard frame, polyester fabric. 3-5 year STR life. Right for studios or bedroom sitting areas.

**Tier 2 — STR sweet spot ($700-1200):** Article Sven loveseat, Castlery Mia, Burrow Nomad 2-seat. Solid-wood frame, performance fabric. 6-8 year STR life. The default for living-room duty.

**Tier 3 — Premium ($1200-1800+):** Joybird Bryant, West Elm Harmony, Pottery Barn Comfort. Kiln-dried hardwood, eight-way hand-tied springs, performance velvet or full-grain leather. 8-10 year life.

## What to actually buy (operator picks)

Default pick: **Article Sven loveseat in performance fabric** (~$1099). The most-photographed STR sofa in production for a reason — solid frame, real fabric, ships in two manageable boxes through a doorway.

Budget pick: **IKEA Friheten 2-seat with storage** (~$649). Convertible to a guest bed, removable washable cover. The right choice for "this room needs a sofa AND a backup sleeper."

Premium pick: **Burrow Nomad Loveseat in olefin** (~$1495). Modular — you can add a third seat later. Stain-resistant olefin survives wine and coffee.

Avoid: any leather-look polyurethane loveseat under $800. The PU coating cracks at the seat front within 18 months and peels in sheets by year 3.

## Installation realities

Most premium loveseats ship modular (Burrow, Article) and assemble in 15-30 minutes — no tools or one provided wrench. IKEA Friheten is a 90-minute assembly with two people.

Doorway check: measure your tightest doorway before ordering. Most loveseats are 60-72 inches wide; if the box doesn't break down further, you may need to remove a door.

## Lifespan math under STR conditions

| Loveseat tier | Residential life | STR life |
|---------------|------------------|----------|
| Budget particleboard | 5-7 years | 3-5 years |
| Mid-range solid-wood | 10-12 years | 6-8 years |
| Premium hardwood | 15+ years | 8-10 years |

## Maintenance that extends life

- Vacuum weekly, including between cushions (cleaner SOP)
- Rotate and flip cushions every turnover if reversible
- Spot-treat within the turnover, not after
- Tighten frame bolts every 6 months

## Signs it's time

- Center cushion 1.5+ inch sag
- Frame creak when sat on
- Visible fabric wear at seat front or arm tops
- Springs felt through the cushion
- Stains that survive professional cleaning

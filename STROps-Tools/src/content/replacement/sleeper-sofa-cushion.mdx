## What you actually pay to replace a sleeper sofa cushion

Honest range for a sleeper-sofa cushion replacement is $120-400 — and that's just the cushion(s), not the sofa itself. Most STR operators land at $180-250 for a foam-and-fiber replacement on a queen pull-out, or $250-350 for a high-density hybrid foam upgrade. A full sleeper sofa replacement runs $1500-3500, so cushion-only is the right move when the frame is still solid.

The sleeper mattress pad that comes with most pull-outs is 4-5 inches thick and made of poly foam that compresses to a pancake in 18 months of STR use. Replacement options range from cheap 4" foam ($80-150) to gel-memory hybrids that actually sleep like a real bed ($300-500).

## Why STR pull-outs wear faster than residential

Residential sleeper-sofa pads last 7-10 years because they're used a few weekends a year. STR pull-outs get unfolded every booking, slept on for 2-3 nights, and folded wet (sweat, humidity) before guest checkout. That cycle accelerates foam breakdown 4x.

The failure pattern is predictable: visible body impression at month 6, springs poking through the cover by month 12, complete loss of support by month 18. Guests review it as "the pull-out was unusable" — and that's a Superhost-killer review.

## Quality tiers worth knowing

**Budget ($120-180):** 4" high-density poly foam from FoamOnline or Foam Factory. 1.8-lb density. Lasts 2-3 years STR. Adequate for a property where the pull-out is genuinely the 4th sleeping option.

**Mid-tier ($180-280):** 5" gel-infused memory + poly hybrid. 2.5-lb density. Lasts 4-5 years STR. The sweet spot for properties where the pull-out is regularly slept on.

**Premium ($280-400):** 6" multi-layer with latex top and high-density base. Custom-cut to the sleeper frame dimensions. Lasts 5-7 years STR. Worth it on properties where adult guests routinely use the pull-out.

## What to actually buy

**Default pick:** Foam Factory 5" gel-memory hybrid sleeper replacement (~$220 queen). Custom-cut, ships flat, available in queen / full / twin pull-out dimensions.

**Hot-market upgrade:** Lucid 6" gel-memory foam pull-out replacement (~$330). Bagged-spring + memory top combo. Closer to a real mattress feel.

**Avoid:** generic Amazon "sleeper sofa mattresses" under $100. They're 1.2-lb density poly that flattens in 4 months.

## Installation realities

15-minute swap. Pull the old pad, vacuum the sleeper frame mesh, set the new pad. Don't fold the new pad immediately — let it expand 24-48 hours flat first, otherwise the memory foam takes a permanent crease at the fold line.

Replace the protective mattress encasement at the same time ($25-40). It's the single biggest extender of pad life — keeps sweat, body oils, and accidents out of the foam.

## Lifespan math

| Pad tier | Residential life | STR life |
|---|---|---|
| Budget poly | 5-8 yr | 2-3 yr |
| Mid gel hybrid | 7-10 yr | 4-5 yr |
| Premium multi-layer | 10-12 yr | 5-7 yr |

## Signs it's time

- Visible body impression that doesn't recover
- Springs from the frame poking through
- Sour smell that washing the cover won't fix
- Guest reviews specifically calling out the pull-out

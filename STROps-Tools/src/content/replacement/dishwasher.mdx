## What it costs to replace a dishwasher in an Airbnb

Honest installed range: $400-1400. Builder-grade Frigidaire / Whirlpool / GE land around $500; mid-tier Bosch 100 or 300 series at $750-1000; top-tier Miele or Bosch 800 at $1100-1400.

For STR, the sweet spot is **Bosch 300 series ($700-900)**: quiet enough that guests can run it without hearing it across the property, durable enough to clear 6-8 years of guest punishment, and the warranty processes claims that some budget brands ignore.

## Why STR dishwashers die early

Residential dishwashers average 10 years. STR dishwashers average 5-7. Guests:
- Run partial loads
- Don't pre-rinse (food cakes the spray arm)
- Add 3x normal detergent ("more is better")
- Run the dishwasher while leaving for the day (vent dripping into cabinets)
- Mix non-dishwasher items (silicone, wooden cutting boards, nonstick pans)

The combined result: spray-arm clogs at year 3, pump-bearing seals at year 4, control-board failures at year 5-6.

## What to actually buy

**Default pick:** Bosch SHEM63W55N (300 series, ~$849). 44 dBA (whisper-quiet), stainless tub, 3rd rack, plastic bottom rack tines that don't rust. Reliable installer base — most cities have a Bosch-trained tech within 25 miles.

**Budget pick:** Whirlpool WDF520PADM (~$549). 55 dBA (audible across an open kitchen but not painful), plastic tub. Lasts 4-6 years in STR. Use when outfitting multiple properties or stocking a vacation cabin.

**Premium pick:** Bosch SHE89PW75N (800 series, ~$1349). 40 dBA, 3rd rack with adjustable tines, water-softener built in (matters in hard-water markets like Phoenix, Vegas, Tucson). Worth it on high-ADR properties.

**Avoid:** Samsung dishwashers in any tier — Samsung dishwashers have consistently high failure rates in independent reliability surveys.

## Installation realities

- **Delivery + haul-away + installation:** $150-300 typical. Some big-box stores include haul-away with old appliance pickup.
- **Hose + power-cord kit:** $30-60. Many dishwashers don't include the supply hose; the installer will add it on day-of.
- **GFCI outlet:** Most newer installs require a GFCI outlet under the sink. Add $100-200 if your panel doesn't have one.

## Lifespan math under STR conditions

| Tier | Residential life | STR life |
|---|---|---|
| Builder-grade (Frigidaire / GE budget) | 8–10 yr | 4–6 yr |
| Mid-tier (Bosch 100 / 300, KitchenAid mid) | 12–14 yr | 6–8 yr |
| Premium (Bosch 800, Miele, KitchenAid top) | 14–16 yr | 8–10 yr |

The single biggest extender is **monthly self-clean cycles** with a dishwasher cleaner tablet (Affresh or Bosch brand). Bake into the maintenance schedule.

## Signs it's time

- Dishes consistently coming out cloudy or with residue
- Inside-the-door rust or visible bubbling
- Cycle taking 2x longer than spec
- Pump making grinding noises
- Control board not retaining selections, error codes recurring

Repair-vs-replace breakpoint: if the repair quote exceeds 50% of replacement AND the unit is 5+ years old, replace.

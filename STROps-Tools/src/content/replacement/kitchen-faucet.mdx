## What it costs to replace a kitchen faucet in an Airbnb

Honest installed range: $120-400 per faucet. Builder-grade pull-down $80-150; mid-tier Moen / Delta / Kohler $180-280; premium Pfister / Brizo / Hansgrohe $300-400+. Plus install: $80-180 if you hire a plumber.

For STR, the right default is a **mid-tier single-handle pull-down with magnetic dock** ($200-280). Pull-down vs pull-out matters less than magnetic dock matters: the magnetic dock prevents the head from drooping over time, which is the #1 visible failure on guest-rated kitchens.

## Why STR faucets fail in 5-7 years

Residential kitchen faucets last 12-15 years. STR faucets average 5-8. Guests:
- Yank pull-down hoses to maximum length and let them snap back (breaks the weight)
- Hand-tighten cleaning sprayers (cracks the swivel ball)
- Leave handle in mid-position (drips, calcium-builds on the seat)
- Use the wrong side (hot when they meant cold) and crank the handle harder

The Moen/Delta cartridge inside survives — but the visible parts (handle finish, sprayer wand, hose retraction) wear visibly.

## What to actually buy

**Default pick:** Moen Arbor (Model 7594ESRS, ~$229). Single-handle pull-down with reflex magnetic dock, MotionSense optional, brushed-nickel or matte-black finish. Moen LifeShine finish doesn't tarnish.

**Budget pick:** Kraus KPF-2620 (~$179). Pull-down with magnetic dock, single-handle, commercial-style spring spout. Photographs well, lasts 4-6 years STR.

**Premium pick:** Brizo Solna or Hansgrohe Talis (~$329-449). Touchless or push-button activation, premium finish, 7-9 year STR life. Worth it on high-ADR kitchens where the faucet is a marketing photo.

**Avoid:** $50-80 Home Depot specials. They look fine but the cartridge fails at 18-24 months in STR use; replacing them is a 2-hour job each time.

## Installation realities

- **Existing supply lines:** Replace with stainless braided at install ($15-25 pair). Rubber lines fail at 8-10 years.
- **Sink hole count:** 1-hole vs 3-hole faucet — confirm before ordering. If switching from 3-hole to 1-hole, you need a deck plate ($15-30).
- **Total DIY install:** ~1.5 hours. Easy job if shutoff valves under the sink work.
- **Plumber install:** $80-180.

## Lifespan math under STR conditions

| Tier | Residential life | STR life |
|---|---|---|
| Budget pull-down (no-name) | 8–10 yr | 4–6 yr |
| Mid-tier (Moen, Delta, Kohler) | 12–15 yr | 7–9 yr |
| Premium (Brizo, Hansgrohe, Kallista) | 15–20 yr | 9–12 yr |

## Maintenance that extends life

- **Quarterly aerator clean** — unscrew, soak in vinegar 1 hr, rebuild. Restores flow that calcium has reduced.
- **Cartridge replacement** ($25) when drip starts — replaceable on Moen/Delta/Kohler with the brand cartridge.
- **Hose-weight check** annually — the metal weight on the pull-down hose works loose; reattaches in 2 minutes.
- **Spray-head soak** every 6 months — calcium clogs the spray nozzles; vinegar soak restores.

## Signs it's time

- Spray head won't retract or droops
- Handle has play / wiggle independent of operation
- Visible corrosion on the finish
- Drip you've replaced the cartridge twice on
- Crack at the base where it meets the sink
